### P-01020 - Crafting a Thematic Poem with Emotional Depth

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative_writing` `emotion` `thematic`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a short poem that explores a specific theme and evokes a clear emotional response.

**Context:**  
You are a beginner poet aiming to express an emotion through a focused theme. Your goal is to craft a poem that connects with readers by using vivid imagery and simple, concise language.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Emotion:** {theme}, {emotion} (the central idea and feeling to convey)  
- **Tone:** {tone} (e.g., hopeful, melancholic, joyful)  
- **Poetic Form:** {form} (e.g., free verse, haiku, quatrain)  
- **Imagery Keywords:** {keywords} (a list of 3-5 concrete words or images related to the theme)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin by reflecting on the {theme} and the associated {emotion} you want to express.  
2. Choose a {tone} that complements the emotion and theme.  
3. Select the {form} as the structure guideline for your poem.  
4. Incorporate the {keywords} into your poem to create vivid imagery.  
5. Write a poem between 150 and 300 words that balances theme, emotion, and imagery clearly and simply.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Poem written in stanzas according to the selected {form}  
- Style: Clear, accessible language suitable for beginner poets  
- Structure: Must adhere to the specified poetic form and include the imagery keywords naturally

**Output Format:**  
A poem of 150-300 words, structured according to the {form}, that expresses the given {emotion} through the {theme} using the provided {keywords}.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for beginner poets seeking to practice thematic and emotional expression through poetry. It helps develop skills in imagery use and form adherence while fostering creativity in writing emotionally resonant verse.
