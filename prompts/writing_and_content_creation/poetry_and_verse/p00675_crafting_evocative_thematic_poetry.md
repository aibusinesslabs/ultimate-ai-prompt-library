**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative-writing` `theme` `verse`

#### Prompt:

**Output Format:**  
A fully composed poem meeting the provided thematic, tonal, and formal criteria, presented as plain text poem formatted for readability.
