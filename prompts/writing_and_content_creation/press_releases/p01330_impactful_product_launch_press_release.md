### P-01330 - Impactful Product Launch Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional and engaging press release announcing the launch of a new product designed to capture media and customer attention.

**Context:**  
You are a PR specialist tasked with creating an official press release for a company launching a new product. The goal is to clearly communicate the product’s key features, benefits, and launch details while appealing to both media outlets and potential customers.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_type}, {key_features} (name, category/type, and standout features of the product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (name and brief background of the company)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {availability} (date of launch and where/how the product will be available)  
- **Target Audience & Benefits:** {target_audience}, {customer_benefits} (intended users and primary advantages for them)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a compelling headline featuring the product name and an attention-grabbing hook.  
2. Write a concise introductory paragraph summarizing the launch news and its significance.  
3. Detail the product’s key features and explain how these benefit the target audience.  
4. Include relevant company background to establish credibility and context.  
5. Provide clear launch details including date, availability, and any calls to action.  
6. End with a boilerplate paragraph about the company and contact information for media inquiries.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and boilerplate  
- Style: Formal, clear, and persuasive tone suitable for media distribution  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to introduction, body, and closing statement

**Output Format:**  
A complete press release text ready for distribution, formatted with a headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and a boilerplate section.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing press releases for new product launches to ensure clear, professional communication that attracts media attention and informs customers. Ideal for PR professionals, marketing teams, and content creators aiming to maximize launch impact.
