### P-00209 - Crafting a Compelling Short Film Script Outline

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `screenwriting` `shortfilm` `scriptoutline` `storystructure`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a detailed outline for a short film script, emphasizing clear narrative structure, character motivation, and key plot points.

**Context:**  
You are an experienced screenwriter tasked with developing a concise yet compelling short film script outline. Your goal is to help define the story arc, character goals, and pivotal scenes to guide the scriptwriting process.

**Input:**  
- **Story & Theme**: {{story_concept}}, {{central_theme}} (brief summary and underlying theme of the film)  
- **Characters**: {{main_character}}, {{antagonist_or_conflict}} (primary protagonist and main source of conflict)  
- **Setting & Tone**: {{setting}}, {{tone}} (location/time period and emotional atmosphere)  
- **Duration & Genre**: {{estimated_duration}}, {{genre}} (target runtime and film genre)

**Instructions:**  
1. Summarize the story concept and define the central theme clearly.  
2. Outline the protagonistâ€™s main goal and the antagonist or key conflict opposing them.  
3. Break down the plot into three acts: setup, confrontation, and resolution, identifying major scenes within each.  
4. Describe the setting and establish the tone, ensuring they support the story and genre.  
5. Include any genre-specific elements that should be highlighted and ensure the outline fits the estimated duration.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Clear, organized outline with headings for each act  
- Style: Professional and concise, suitable for screenwriters and producers  
- Structure: Logical flow following three-act structure, with focus on narrative clarity and character motivation

**Output Format:**  
A structured short film script outline divided into:  
- Story & Theme Summary  
- Character Descriptions and Motivations  
- Act 1: Setup (key scenes)  
- Act 2: Confrontation (key scenes)  
- Act 3: Resolution (key scenes)  
- Setting and Tone Overview  
- Genre and Duration Considerations

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for screenwriters, filmmakers, and content creators who need to organize their short film ideas into a coherent outline before writing the full script. It helps structure the narrative efficiently, ensuring the story is engaging and professionally presented for pitching or production planning.
