### P-01469 - Crafting a Compelling Short Film Script

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `scriptwriting` `shortfilm` `screenplay` `creativewriting`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a concise, engaging short film script based on the given theme and character details.

**Context:**  
You are a novice screenwriter tasked with creating a short film script that captures the audience’s attention through strong dialogue and clear scene descriptions. The script should be suitable for a 5-minute film.

**Input:**  
- **Film Details:** {film_title}, {genre}, {theme} (title, type, and central idea of the short film)  
- **Characters:** {main_character_name}, {main_character_trait}, {secondary_character_name}, {secondary_character_trait} (names and defining traits of the two main characters)  
- **Setting:** {location}, {time_period} (where and when the story takes place)  
- **Plot Points:** {conflict}, {resolution} (primary conflict and how it is resolved)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a clear scene heading establishing the setting and time.  
2. Introduce the main characters with brief but vivid descriptions aligned with their traits.  
3. Develop the plot around the given conflict, showing character interactions and progression.  
4. Incorporate natural dialogue that reflects each character’s personality.  
5. Conclude with a resolution that ties back to the theme and provides closure.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Standard screenplay format with scene headings, action lines, and dialogue  
- Style: Simple, clear, and engaging language appropriate for beginner screenwriters  
- Structure: Beginning, middle, and end with a logical flow  

**Output Format:**  
A short film script text formatted with scene headings, character names centered above dialogue, and concise action descriptions.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for beginner writers looking to practice screenwriting by creating a complete short film script around a specific theme and character setup. It supports learning the basics of formatting and storytelling in screenplay form while focusing on effective dialogue and scene composition.
