### P-00614 - Crafting a Compelling Short Film Script

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `scriptwriting` `shortfilm` `storystructure` `dialogue`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a compelling short film script that effectively communicates a clear story arc within a limited timeframe.

**Context:**  
You are an aspiring screenwriter tasked with creating a short film script that captures audience interest using well-structured scenes, concise dialogue, and engaging characters.

**Input:**  
- **Story Details:** {genre}, {main_theme} (the genre and primary theme of the short film)  
- **Characters:** {protagonist_name}, {antagonist_name} (names and brief roles of primary characters)  
- **Setting:** {location}, {time_period} (where and when the story takes place)  
- **Plot Points:** {beginning_summary}, {conflict_summary}, {resolution_summary} (key plot outline points)

**Instructions:**  
1. Develop a three-act structure: setup, confrontation, and resolution.  
2. Create vivid, concise scene descriptions that reflect the setting and mood.  
3. Write natural and purposeful dialogue that reveals character and advances the plot.  
4. Ensure the story has a clear conflict and resolution consistent with the input plot points.  
5. Keep the script engaging yet concise, suitable for a 5-10 minute short film.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Standard screenplay format with scene headers, action lines, and dialogue  
- Style: Clear, dramatic, and accessible for beginner screenwriters  
- Structure: Three acts with at least three scenes total

**Output Format:**  
A short film script in screenplay format including scene headings, action descriptions, and dialogue aligned with the inputs and instructions.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt to guide beginner writers aiming to create focused, professional short film scripts for festivals, portfolios, or practice. Ideal for those learning script structure and concise storytelling.
