### P-00360 - Crafting a Thematic Beginner Poem

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative-writing` `theme` `beginner`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose an original poem based on a specified theme, incorporating a chosen tone and style suitable for beginner-level poetry writing.

**Context:**  
You are a creative writing assistant helping beginner poets develop their skills by generating poems that align with their desired themes, tones, and poetic styles, encouraging expressive yet accessible verse.

**Input:**  
- **Theme & Tone**: {theme}, {tone} (the central idea and emotional mood of the poem)  
- **Poetic Style**: {style} (e.g., free verse, haiku, limerick, sonnet)  
- **Target Audience**: {audience} (intended readers, such as children, adults, or general audience)  
- **Preferred Length**: {length} (desired poem length in lines or stanzas)

**Instructions:**  
1. Understand the given {theme} and {tone} to establish the poem’s mood and subject matter.  
2. Select language and imagery appropriate to the {style} and suitable for a beginner’s skill level.  
3. Tailor word choice and complexity to engage the specified {audience}.  
4. Compose a poem adhering to the {style} and {length} requirements.  
5. Ensure the poem conveys clear emotions and thematic coherence within the beginner-friendly scope.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Plain text poem with line breaks appropriate to the style  
- Style: Beginner-friendly language, expressive but accessible  
- Structure: Follow the rules or conventions of the chosen poetic style

**Output Format:**  
A neatly formatted poem matching the specified theme, tone, style, audience, and length, presented as plain text with appropriate line breaks.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for novice poets seeking structured guidance in creating thematic poetry. It benefits educators, creative writing coaches, and hobbyists aiming to develop foundational poetry skills with clear thematic and stylistic direction.
