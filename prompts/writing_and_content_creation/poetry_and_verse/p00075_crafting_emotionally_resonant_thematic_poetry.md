### P-00075 - Crafting Emotionally Resonant Thematic Poetry

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative_writing` `emotional_expression` `thematic_verse`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a thematically coherent poem that evokes a specific emotional response by integrating vivid imagery and poetic devices.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled poet tasked with creating a compelling original poem that explores a central theme with emotional depth. The poem should resonate with readers by using metaphor, simile, rhythm, and other poetic elements to enhance the thematic impact.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Emotion:** {theme}, {emotional_tone} (the central topic and the emotion to be conveyed)  
- **Poetic Style and Form:** {style}, {form} (e.g., free verse, sonnet, haiku, narrative)  
- **Imagery and Symbolism:** {imagery_elements} (key images or symbols to include)  
- **Audience and Purpose:** {target_audience}, {purpose} (intended readers and the poem’s goal)

**Instructions:**  
1. Analyze the provided theme and emotional tone to establish the poem’s core message.  
2. Select appropriate poetic devices and structural elements aligned with the specified style and form.  
3. Incorporate the given imagery and symbolism to enrich sensory experience and thematic depth.  
4. Tailor language and tone to suit the target audience and fulfill the poem’s purpose effectively.  
5. Ensure the poem maintains coherence, emotional resonance, and artistic integrity throughout.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Written as a continuous poem (no prose), using line breaks and stanzas as appropriate  
- Style: Use a consistent poetic style and tone that reflects the chosen emotional atmosphere  
- Structure: Follow the specified poetic form strictly, maintaining meter and rhyme if required

**Output Format:**  
An original poem composed according to the inputs, formatted with appropriate line breaks and stanzas, ready for presentation or publication.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when you need to generate emotionally impactful poetry tailored to a specific theme and audience—ideal for poets, content creators, educators, or marketing professionals seeking meaningful verse that connects deeply with readers. It supports creative expression while adhering to formal poetic conventions.
