### P-00655 - Crafting Impactful Product Launch Press Releases

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional press release announcing a new product launch that effectively communicates key features, benefits, and company vision to media outlets and potential customers.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled public relations specialist tasked with crafting a compelling press release for a company launching a new product. Your goal is to generate interest, provide clear information, and align the messaging with the company’s brand voice.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_features}, {unique_selling_points} (core attributes and differentiators)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background}, {brand_tone} (company overview and communication style)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {availability}, {pricing} (key logistics about release and purchasing)  
- **Target Audience & Media:** {target_audience}, {preferred_media_outlets} (intended readers and channels for distribution)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong, attention-grabbing headline including the product name and launch announcement.  
2. Write an engaging opening paragraph summarizing the product launch and its significance.  
3. Detail the product’s main features and unique selling points, explaining benefits to the target audience.  
4. Incorporate relevant company background information and brand tone to enhance credibility.  
5. Include launch timing, availability, pricing, and call-to-action encouraging further engagement or inquiries.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and boilerplate statement  
- Style: Professional, clear, and persuasive with a formal tone consistent with brand voice  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to company information, followed by product details and closing remarks

**Output Format:**  
A fully formatted press release text ready for distribution, including headline, dateline, body, and company boilerplate.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt to create polished press releases for new product launches that need to attract media attention and inform customers clearly. Ideal for PR professionals, marketing teams, and content creators working in product communications.
