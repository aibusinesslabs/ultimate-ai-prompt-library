### P-00717 - Engaging Landing Page Copy for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Landing Pages  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `landing page` `copywriting` `product launch` `marketing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write compelling and clear landing page copy that highlights the benefits and key features of a new product to encourage visitor engagement and conversions.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled copywriter tasked with creating persuasive landing page content for a new product launch. The goal is to connect with potential customers by clearly communicating value propositions in a concise and engaging manner.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {{product_name}}, {{product_description}} (name and brief description)  
- **Target Audience:** {{audience_description}} (demographics and pain points)  
- **Key Features & Benefits:** {{features_list}}, {{benefits_list}} (main characteristics and advantages)  
- **Call to Action:** {{cta_text}} (desired user action and phrasing)

**Instructions:**  
1. Introduce the product with a captivating headline using {{product_name}}.  
2. Summarize the product using {{product_description}} tailored to {{audience_description}}.  
3. Clearly present {{features_list}} linked to {{benefits_list}} emphasizing how the product solves customer pain points.  
4. Use persuasive and accessible language suitable for beginners.  
5. End with a strong, clear {{cta_text}} encouraging immediate action.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Paragraphs separated by line breaks, no bullet points  
- Style: Professional, friendly, and persuasive  
- Structure: Headline, product summary, features & benefits, call to action

**Output Format:**  
A ready-to-publish landing page copy text block meeting the above requirements.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when launching a new product requiring engaging landing page content that converts visitors by clearly communicating value to a targeted audience. Ideal for marketers, copywriters, and small business owners building product pages with limited copywriting experience.
