### P-00374 - Crafting a Compelling Feature Film Script Outline

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `screenwriting` `feature_film` `story_structure` `character_development`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Develop a detailed and coherent outline for a feature-length film script that includes key plot points, character arcs, and scene progression, ensuring strong narrative flow and emotional engagement.

**Context:**  
You are an experienced screenwriter tasked with creating a comprehensive outline for a feature film script. The outline should serve as a blueprint for writing the full screenplay, balancing structure, character development, and pacing to captivate audiences.

**Input:**  
- **Story Information:** {genre}, {premise} (the film’s genre and a brief one-sentence premise)  
- **Characters:** {main_character_description}, {antagonist_description} (brief descriptions including motivations and conflicts)  
- **Setting:** {time_period}, {location} (the temporal and geographical setting of the story)  
- **Tone & Style:** {tone}, {style} (desired emotional tone and writing style, e.g., dark and suspenseful, light-hearted and witty)

**Instructions:**  
1. Outline the three-act structure highlighting the Setup, Confrontation, and Resolution phases.  
2. Identify and describe at least five key plot points, including inciting incident, midpoint twist, climax, and resolution.  
3. Define the main character’s arc with clear development milestones and emotional transformations across the story.  
4. Integrate the antagonist’s goals and obstacles to create meaningful conflict that drives the narrative forward.  
5. Include brief notes on important scenes or sequences that establish tone, setting, and style, ensuring consistency with the input parameters.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Structured outline with clear headings and bullet points for readability  
- Style: Professional, concise, and engaging; suitable for presentation to producers or collaborators  
- Structure: Must follow a logical flow corresponding to classic screenplay storytelling conventions

**Output Format:**  
A well-organized outline document containing:  
- Title/Header section with genre and premise  
- Three-act breakdown with labeled plot points  
- Character arc summaries for protagonist and antagonist  
- Scene notes aligned with tone and style guidelines

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing a professional feature film script outline for pitching, planning, or collaboration with producers and directors. It benefits screenwriters seeking to clarify story structure and character dynamics before writing the full screenplay. Ideal for advanced users who require a comprehensive but concise blueprint to guide their creative process.
