### P-00089 - Crafting a Compelling Feature Film Script Outline

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `screenwriting` `film` `story-structure` `character-development`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a detailed and coherent feature film script outline that effectively integrates plot progression, character arcs, and thematic elements.

**Context:**  
You are an experienced screenwriter tasked with developing a professional outline for a feature-length film. Your goal is to structure the story for maximum dramatic impact while ensuring clarity of character motivations and thematic resonance.

**Input:**  
- **Story Elements**: {{genre}}, {{logline}} (the film’s genre and one-sentence story summary)  
- **Characters**: {{protagonist}}, {{antagonist}}, {{supporting_characters}} (main and key secondary character descriptions including motivations and conflicts)  
- **Setting & Tone**: {{time_period}}, {{location}}, {{tone}} (when and where the story takes place, and the overall narrative mood)  
- **Key Plot Points**: {{inciting_incident}}, {{midpoint_twist}}, {{climax}} (major turning points driving the narrative)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a concise introduction summarizing the film’s premise based on the genre and logline.  
2. Develop a three-act structure outline, clearly delineating Setup, Confrontation, and Resolution phases.  
3. Within each act, specify the key scenes or sequences, highlighting their purpose in advancing the plot and character development.  
4. Integrate character arcs throughout the outline, showing how protagonists and antagonists evolve in response to conflicts and events.  
5. Emphasize how the setting and tone reinforce the story’s themes and emotional impact, ensuring consistency and immersion.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Structured outline with clearly labeled acts and scenes  
- Style: Professional, clear, and engaging narrative voice suitable for industry script development  
- Structure: Logical progression with emphasis on plot clarity, character dynamics, and thematic coherence

**Output Format:**  
A formatted, act-by-act screenplay outline including:  
- Act titles and brief summaries  
- Scene-by-scene bullet points or short paragraphs describing key events  
- Character arc notes integrated within plot points  
- Thematic and tonal commentary where relevant

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for screenwriters, script consultants, and film students who need to create or refine a feature film outline. It helps in early-stage script development, pitching to producers, or preparing for full scriptwriting by ensuring a structured, compelling narrative foundation.
