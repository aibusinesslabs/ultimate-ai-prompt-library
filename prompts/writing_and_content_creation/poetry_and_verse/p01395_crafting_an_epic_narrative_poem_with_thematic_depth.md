### P-01395 - Crafting an Epic Narrative Poem with Thematic Depth

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `narrative` `poetry` `thematic` `creative-writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a compelling epic narrative poem that weaves a complex theme throughout a vivid storyline, demonstrating mastery of poetic devices and advanced verse structure.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled poet and storyteller renowned for creating immersive, multi-layered poetic narratives. Your goal is to craft an epic poem that not only tells a captivating story but also explores profound themes through symbolic imagery and varied poetic techniques.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Setting**: {primary_theme}, {historical_or_fantastical_setting} (core theme and narrative environment)  
- **Main Characters and Conflict**: {protagonist_name_and_traits}, {antagonist_name_and_traits}, {central_conflict_description} (key figures and their driving opposition)  
- **Tone and Mood**: {desired_tone}, {emotional_mood} (atmosphere and emotional undercurrent)  
- **Poetic Form and Style Preferences**: {poetic_forms}, {preferred_rhyme_scheme}, {meter_requirements} (structural and stylistic guidelines)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin by outlining the narrative arc based on the input theme, characters, and conflict to establish a clear epic progression.  
2. Develop vivid imagery and symbolism aligned with the primary theme, enhancing the depth and resonance of the poem.  
3. Employ advanced poetic devices such as metaphor, alliteration, enjambment, and varied meter to enrich the texture and flow.  
4. Maintain the specified tone and mood consistently throughout, ensuring emotional engagement and atmosphere.  
5. Follow the given poetic form, rhyme scheme, and meter precisely, crafting stanzas that adhere to advanced verse structure standards.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Written as a continuous poem divided into stanzas; include a title and brief thematic epigraph (1-2 sentences)  
- Style: Elevated, sophisticated language with nuanced vocabulary; balance narrative clarity with poetic artistry  
- Structure: Clear narrative progression with introduction, rising action, climax, and resolution embedded in poetic form; consistent rhyme and meter as per input

**Output Format:**  
A titled epic narrative poem with a thematic epigraph, structured in stanzas, adhering strictly to the input poetic form, rhyme scheme, and meter, spanning 500-750 words.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for poets, writers, and educators seeking to create or teach advanced narrative poetry that combines storytelling with thematic exploration. It suits scenarios where deep creative expression and technical mastery of verse are required, such as literary workshops, portfolio development, or advanced creative writing courses.
