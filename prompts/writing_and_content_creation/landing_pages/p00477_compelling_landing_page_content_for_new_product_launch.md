### P-00477 - Compelling Landing Page Content for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Landing Pages  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `landing page` `copywriting` `product launch` `conversion`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a clear, persuasive landing page copy designed to promote a new product and encourage visitors to take action.

**Context:**  
You are a professional copywriter specializing in crafting landing pages that convert. Your goal is to highlight the product’s key features and benefits while addressing the target audience’s needs to drive engagement and conversions.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description} (name and brief summary of the product)  
- **Target Audience:** {audience_description} (description of ideal customers)  
- **Key Benefits:** {benefit_1}, {benefit_2}, {benefit_3} (three main advantages of the product)  
- **Call to Action:** {cta_text} (desired user action, e.g., “Buy Now” or “Sign Up”)

**Instructions:**  
1. Write a captivating headline featuring {product_name} that grabs attention.  
2. Craft a concise introduction summarizing {product_description} and its relevance to {audience_description}.  
3. Highlight the three {benefit_1}, {benefit_2}, and {benefit_3} clearly and persuasively.  
4. Incorporate a strong, motivating call to action using {cta_text}.  
5. Ensure the tone is friendly, professional, and easy to understand for beginners.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Paragraph style with short, clear sentences  
- Style: Professional, engaging, and accessible  
- Structure: Headline, introduction, benefits section, and call to action in sequence

**Output Format:**  
A complete landing page copy text, ready to be used or adapted for a product promotion page.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for marketers and small business owners launching a new product who need quick, effective landing page content without extensive copywriting experience. It helps create focused messaging that resonates with the target audience and boosts conversions.
