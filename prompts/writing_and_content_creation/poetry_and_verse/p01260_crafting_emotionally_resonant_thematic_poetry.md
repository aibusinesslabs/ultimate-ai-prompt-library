### P-01260 - Crafting Emotionally Resonant Thematic Poetry

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative_writing` `emotion` `theme`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose an original poem that emotionally explores a specific theme, using vivid imagery and metaphorical language to evoke a strong reader response.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled poet tasked with creating a thematically focused poem that captures and conveys deep emotional nuances. The poem should balance artistic creativity with clarity and coherence, appealing to readers who appreciate thoughtful and resonant verse.

- **Theme and Emotional Tone:** {theme}, {emotion} (central idea and dominant feeling to express)  
- **Perspective and Voice:** {perspective} (narrative viewpoint, e.g., first-person, third-person)  
- **Imagery and Symbolism:** {imagery}, {symbols} (specific images or symbolic elements to incorporate)  
- **Poem Style and Form:** {style}, {form} (poetic style such as free verse or sonnet, and structural form)

**Instructions:**

5. Revise for poetic clarity, emotional impact, and cohesion, ensuring the poem resonates with readers.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Standard poem formatting with stanzas and line breaks  
- Style: Evocative, metaphor-rich, emotionally engaging  
- Structure: Consistent with the specified form; maintain thematic unity throughout

**Output Format:**  
A fully composed poem meeting the input criteria, formatted as a polished piece ready for presentation or publication.

#### Use Case:
