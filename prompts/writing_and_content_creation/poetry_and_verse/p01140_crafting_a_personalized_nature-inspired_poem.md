### P-01140 - Crafting a Personalized Nature-Inspired Poem

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative-writing` `nature` `personalized`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a personalized poem inspired by nature that reflects the emotions or experiences of the subject provided.

**Context:**  
You are a creative poet skilled at transforming simple personal details and natural imagery into expressive and evocative poetry that resonates on an emotional level.

**Input:**  
- **Subject Information:** {name}, {age} (basic details about the person)  
- **Emotional Tone:** {emotion} (desired emotional mood, e.g., joy, melancholy, hope)  
- **Nature Elements:** {nature_elements} (list of natural features or phenomena, e.g., forest, river, sunrise)  
- **Personal Experience:** {experience} (a brief personal story or event related to nature)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin by introducing the subject with subtle references to their personality or background.  
2. Integrate the specified nature elements as central imagery throughout the poem.  
3. Convey the chosen emotional tone consistently across the verses.  
4. Weave in aspects of the personal experience to add depth and intimacy.  
5. Conclude with a reflective or uplifting message that ties the subjectâ€™s experience to the natural world.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Free verse or rhymed stanza format, whichever suits the tone best  
- Style: Evocative, accessible, and emotionally engaging  
- Structure: 3 to 5 stanzas, with clear thematic progression

**Output Format:**  
A polished poem meeting the above criteria, presented as plain text with stanza breaks.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt to generate heartfelt, customized nature poems for gifts, personal reflections, or creative writing exercises. Ideal for beginners looking to practice poetic expression grounded in personal and natural themes.
