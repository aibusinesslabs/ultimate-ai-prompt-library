### P-00265 - Impactful Product Launch Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `branding`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a compelling and professional press release announcing a new product launch to generate media interest and customer excitement.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled PR specialist tasked with crafting a press release for a company introducing a new product. The goal is to clearly communicate the productâ€™s unique features, benefits, and availability while maintaining a formal and engaging tone.

**Input:**  
- **Company & Product Details:** {company_name}, {product_name}, {product_category} (basic information about the company and the product being launched)  
- **Product Features & Benefits:** {feature_list}, {benefit_summary} (key features and the primary advantages for customers)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {availability}, {pricing} (when and where the product will be available, including price points)  
- **Contact & Quote:** {spokesperson_name}, {spokesperson_title}, {contact_info} (company representative details and a relevant quote for credibility)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that captures attention and summarizes the announcement.  
2. Write a concise introductory paragraph stating the product launch and its significance.  
3. Describe the product features and benefits clearly, highlighting what sets it apart.  
4. Include launch details such as date, availability, and pricing information.  
5. Add a quote from the spokesperson to provide authority and a personal touch.  
6. End with a brief company background and contact information for media inquiries.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline, dateline, body, and boilerplate  
- Style: Formal, informative, and engaging  
- Structure: Logical flow with clear separation between sections (headline, introduction, features, launch info, quote, boilerplate)

**Output Format:**  
A fully formatted press release text ready for distribution, containing all specified sections and adhering to the word count and style requirements.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when a company needs to announce a new product launch professionally to media outlets and potential customers. It benefits PR professionals, marketers, and communications teams aiming to create effective press materials that boost brand visibility and product interest.
