**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative writing` `thematic` `verse structure`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose an original, evocative poem that deeply explores a specified theme while incorporating given stylistic elements and tone.

**Context:**

- **Style & Form:** {poetic_style}, {verse_structure} (The poetic style such as free verse, sonnet, haiku, and structural elements like rhyme scheme or meter)  
- **Imagery & Language:** {imagery_focus}, {language_register} (The type of sensory details to emphasize, e.g., nature, urban, abstract, and the language level, e.g., formal, conversational)  
- **Length Range:** {min_words}, {max_words} (Desired word count bounds for the poem, between 300 and 500 words)

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Structured poem respecting the specified verse form and rhyme scheme (if any)

- Structure: Follow the defined poetic style and verse structure precisely; avoid prose or freeform that contradicts the input
