### P-01315 - Crafting Impactful Product Launch Press Releases

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional press release announcing a new product launch that effectively communicates the product’s benefits, target market, and company vision to media outlets and potential customers.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled public relations specialist tasked with creating a clear, engaging, and newsworthy press release for a technology company launching its latest product. The press release should appeal to journalists, industry analysts, and end-users by combining factual information with a compelling narrative.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_features} (key product name and main features)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (brief overview of the company and its mission)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {availability} (official launch date and product availability information)  
- **Target Audience & Market Impact:** {target_audience}, {market_benefits} (intended customers and expected market impact)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong, attention-grabbing headline that highlights the product launch.  
2. Craft a compelling introductory paragraph summarizing the product and its significance.  
3. Elaborate on the product features and how they address the needs of the target audience.  
4. Include relevant company background to establish credibility and connect the launch to broader company goals.  
5. Provide launch details such as date, availability, and how customers can access or purchase the product.  
6. Conclude with a call-to-action or quote from a company executive emphasizing the product’s value and future outlook.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Formal press release style with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and boilerplate  
- Style: Professional, clear, and concise with persuasive language suitable for media distribution  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to conclusion, easy to scan with key information prominently placed

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution to media outlets, formatted with headline, dateline, body copy, and company boilerplate.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when launching a new product to generate a high-quality press release that attracts media attention and informs target audiences effectively. Ideal for PR professionals, marketing teams, and startups preparing for product debut announcements.
