### P-01120 - Crafting Impactful Corporate Press Releases

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `corporate communication` `professional writing` `media relations`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional and engaging press release that effectively announces a corporate event or milestone, capturing the attention of media outlets and stakeholders.

**Context:**  
You are a corporate communications specialist tasked with drafting a press release for a company to publicize an important announcement. The press release should reflect the organization’s brand voice and provide clear, concise information tailored to journalists and the public.

**Input:**  
- **Event Details**: {event_name}, {event_date}, {event_location} (name, date, and venue of the event or milestone)  
- **Company Profile**: {company_name}, {industry}, {company_background} (brief overview of the company and its market position)  
- **Key Message**: {announcement_summary} (main news or achievement to be highlighted)  
- **Quotes & Contacts**: {executive_quote}, {media_contact_info} (a quote from a company leader and contact details for press inquiries)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a compelling headline that summarizes the announcement clearly and attractively.  
2. Write a strong opening paragraph that covers the who, what, when, where, and why of the news.  
3. Elaborate on the significance of the event or milestone, linking it to the company’s mission and industry impact.  
4. Incorporate the executive quote naturally to add credibility and a personal touch.  
5. Conclude with a call to action or additional information, followed by the media contact details for follow-up.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and contact information  
- Style: Formal, clear, and engaging, suitable for professional media distribution  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to closing, avoiding jargon and maintaining reader interest

**Output Format:**  
A professionally formatted press release document ready for distribution, including headline, dateline, body text, executive quote, and media contact section.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when crafting official announcements for new product launches, corporate milestones, partnerships, or events. PR professionals and communications teams benefit from this prompt to produce consistent, media-friendly press releases that enhance brand visibility and stakeholder engagement.
