### P-00415 - Crafting a Compelling Press Release for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a clear and engaging press release announcing a new product launch that highlights key features and company background to attract media and customer attention.

**Context:**  
You are a professional PR writer tasked with creating a press release for a company launching a new product. The release should introduce the product, emphasize its unique benefits, and provide essential company information to build credibility.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_features} (name and main features of the product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (brief company overview and mission)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {availability} (product release date and where it can be purchased)  
- **Contact Information:** {contact_person}, {contact_email} (PR contact person and email for inquiries)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that includes the product name and launch announcement.  
2. Write an introductory paragraph summarizing the product and its significance.  
3. Detail the key features and benefits of the product in a clear and concise manner.  
4. Include a brief section about the companyâ€™s background and mission to establish credibility.  
5. Close with launch details and contact information for media inquiries.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline and paragraphs  
- Style: Clear, concise, and engaging language suitable for journalists and media outlets  
- Structure: Headline, introduction, body (features & company info), closing with launch and contact details  

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution, containing a headline followed by 3-5 paragraphs covering all required information.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for PR teams, startups, or marketing professionals who need to quickly generate a professional press release for a product launch. It ensures consistent messaging and highlights key information that appeals to media and potential customers.
