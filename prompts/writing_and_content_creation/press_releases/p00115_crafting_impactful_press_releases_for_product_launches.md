### P-00115 - Crafting Impactful Press Releases for Product Launches

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a professional, engaging press release announcing a new product launch that effectively communicates key information and generates media interest.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled communications specialist tasked with drafting a press release that highlights the unique features and benefits of a new product. The goal is to inform journalists, potential customers, and stakeholders while maintaining a tone that balances professionalism and excitement.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_type}, {key_features} (core information about the product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {availability} (when and where the product will be accessible)  
- **Company Background:** {company_name}, {company_mission} (brief company overview and mission statement)  
- **Call to Action:** {contact_info}, {website_url} (how readers can find more information or make inquiries)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a compelling headline that captures the essence of the product launch.  
2. Write a clear and concise introductory paragraph stating the launch, product name, and significance.  
3. Elaborate on the product's key features and benefits, emphasizing what sets it apart from competitors.  
4. Include relevant background information about the company to build credibility and context.  
5. End with a strong call to action, providing contact details and where readers can learn more.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and boilerplate  
- Style: Professional, clear, and engaging with a persuasive tone  
- Structure: Logical flow from announcement to details, ending with contact information and company boilerplate

**Output Format:**  
A well-structured press release text ready for distribution, including headline, dateline, body, and boilerplate sections.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing to announce a new product to the media and public. It benefits PR professionals, marketers, and business owners who need a polished press release to maximize exposure and generate interest ahead of a product launch. Ideal for startups and established companies alike.
