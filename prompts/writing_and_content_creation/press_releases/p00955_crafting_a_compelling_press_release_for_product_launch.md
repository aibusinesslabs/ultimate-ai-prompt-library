### P-00955 - Crafting a Compelling Press Release for Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a clear and engaging press release announcing a new product launch.

**Context:**  
You are a professional communications specialist tasked with creating a concise press release that highlights the key features and benefits of a newly launched product to attract media attention and potential customers.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_type} (name and category of the product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_location} (date and place of product launch)  
- **Key Features:** {feature_list} (a bullet or comma-separated list of main product features)  
- **Company Background:** {company_name}, {company_overview} (name and brief description of the company)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that includes the product name and its significance.  
2. Introduce the product and its launch details in the opening paragraph.  
3. Elaborate on key features and benefits in the following section.  
4. Include a brief background about the company to establish credibility.  
5. End with a call to action or contact information for media inquiries.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Use professional press release structure with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and closing  
- Style: Clear, concise, formal, and engaging tone suitable for media distribution  
- Structure: Logical flow with headline, introduction, body, and conclusion  

**Output Format:**  
A fully formatted press release text ready for distribution, including headline and dateline.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt to quickly generate professional press releases for new product launches by startups, small businesses, or marketing teams looking to announce their offerings in a clear and media-friendly format. Ideal for PR professionals and content creators aiming to boost product visibility.
