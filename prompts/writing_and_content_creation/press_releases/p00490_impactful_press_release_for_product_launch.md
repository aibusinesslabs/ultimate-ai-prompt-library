### P-00490 - Impactful Press Release for Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `media communication`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a compelling and professional press release announcing the launch of a new product, designed to attract media attention, inform potential customers, and establish the brand’s authority in the market.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned PR specialist crafting a press release on behalf of a company unveiling its latest product. Your objective is to communicate key product details clearly while generating excitement and credibility among journalists, stakeholders, and consumers.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_type}, {key_features} (name, category, and main attributes of the product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (brand name and brief company overview)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {availability} (official release date and distribution channels)  
- **Quotes and Contact:** {executive_quote}, {media_contact} (statement from a company executive and press contact information)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong, attention-grabbing headline that clearly states the product launch.  
2. Write an engaging lead paragraph summarizing the product and its significance.  
3. Elaborate on the product’s unique features and benefits, emphasizing innovation and market relevance.  
4. Incorporate the executive quote to add authenticity and executive perspective.  
5. Provide clear launch details including date and where the product will be available.  
6. Conclude with a brief company background and include media contact information for follow-up.  
7. Ensure the tone is professional, concise, and persuasive, suitable for distribution to media outlets.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, dateline, body, and boilerplate  
- Style: Formal yet engaging, objective but promotional  
- Structure: Logical flow starting from headline to body, ending with company info and contact details

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for publication, formatted with a headline, dateline, body paragraphs, executive quote, company boilerplate, and media contact section.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when needing to draft a high-quality press release for a product launch that will be distributed to media outlets, industry influencers, and potential customers. PR professionals, marketers, and startup founders benefit from this prompt to ensure clarity, professionalism, and strategic messaging in their announcements.
