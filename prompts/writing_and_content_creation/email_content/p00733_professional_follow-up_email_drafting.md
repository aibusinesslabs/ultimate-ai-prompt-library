### P-00733 - Professional Follow-Up Email Drafting

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Email Content  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `email` `follow-up` `professional` `communication`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a clear and professional follow-up email to a business contact to maintain engagement and encourage a response.

**Context:**  
You are a professional communicator tasked with drafting a polite and concise follow-up email after an initial meeting or outreach, aiming to reinforce the connection and prompt further action.

**Input:**  
- **Recipient Details**: {recipient_name}, {recipient_position} (Name and job title of the email recipient)  
- **Previous Interaction**: {interaction_date}, {interaction_topic} (Date and subject of the last contact or meeting)  
- **Purpose of Follow-Up**: {follow_up_reason} (Reason for following up, e.g., feedback request, next steps)  
- **Sender Details**: {sender_name}, {sender_position}, {sender_company} (Name, job title, and company of the email sender)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a polite greeting addressing {recipient_name} appropriately.  
2. Reference the previous interaction on {interaction_date} related to {interaction_topic}.  
3. Clearly state the purpose of the follow-up using {follow_up_reason}.  
4. Encourage a response or propose next steps in a courteous manner.  
5. Close professionally with the senderâ€™s details: {sender_name}, {sender_position}, and {sender_company}.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Standard professional email format (greeting, body, closing)  
- Style: Polite, concise, and professional tone  
- Structure: Logical flow with clear paragraphs separating introduction, purpose, and closing

**Output Format:**  
A complete follow-up email draft formatted as a professional business email.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for professionals who need to maintain communication with clients, partners, or colleagues after initial contact. It helps users craft polite and effective follow-up emails that foster engagement and encourage timely responses in business settings.
