### P-01110 - Evoking Emotion Through Thematic Epic Poetry

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `poetry` `epic` `creative-writing` `thematic`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a thematically rich epic poem that explores a profound human experience, evoking deep emotional resonance and vivid imagery throughout the narrative.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned poet tasked with creating an advanced-level epic poem that blends classical poetic techniques with contemporary thematic relevance. The poem should demonstrate mastery of language, metaphor, and structure to engage and move a sophisticated audience.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Emotional Tone:** {theme}, {emotional_tone} (core subject and prevailing mood of the poem)  
- **Narrative Elements:** {main_character}, {setting}, {conflict} (key protagonist, location, and central struggle)  
- **Stylistic Preferences:** {poetic_meter}, {rhyme_scheme} (preferred meter and rhyme pattern, or specify free verse)  
- **Additional Imagery and Symbols:** {symbols}, {imagery_style} (specific motifs and descriptive style to incorporate)

**Instructions:**  
1. Establish the setting and introduce the main character within the first stanza, using vivid imagery consistent with the specified style.  
2. Develop the central conflict by weaving in emotional depth and symbolism aligned with the theme and tone.  
3. Employ the chosen poetic meter and rhyme scheme rigorously, or create a compelling free verse structure if specified.  
4. Use extended metaphors and evocative language to enhance the thematic resonance and emotional impact.  
5. Conclude with a reflective or transformative resolution that encapsulates the poemâ€™s message and leaves a lasting impression.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Narrative epic poem with stanzas clearly delineated  
- Style: Elevated, lyrical, and immersive, appropriate for advanced literary audiences  
- Structure: Cohesive narrative arc with a clear beginning, development, climax, and resolution

**Output Format:**  
A single, polished epic poem formatted into stanzas, respecting the input parameters and stylistic instructions, ready for presentation or publication.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when you want to create a sophisticated epic poem that probes complex human themes with artistic depth and formal poetic craftsmanship. Ideal for poets, educators, and creative writers aiming to produce memorable and emotionally powerful verse for literary journals, anthologies, or performances.
