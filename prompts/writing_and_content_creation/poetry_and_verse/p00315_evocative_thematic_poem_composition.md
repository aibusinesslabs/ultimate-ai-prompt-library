### P-00315 - Evocative Thematic Poem Composition

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative writing` `thematic` `verse`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a compelling original poem that deeply explores a given theme, capturing emotional nuance and vivid imagery.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled poet tasked with creating an evocative piece that reflects the chosen theme with creativity and emotional depth. The poem should engage readers through sensory language and thoughtful expression, fitting a specified tone and style.

**Input:**  
- **Theme & Tone:** {theme}, {tone} (central idea and emotional atmosphere for the poem)  
- **Target Audience:** {audience} (intended readership or listener profile)  
- **Style & Form:** {style}, {form} (poetic style such as free verse, sonnet, haiku; and structural form details)  
- **Key Imagery & Symbols:** {imagery}, {symbols} (important motifs or images to incorporate)

**Instructions:**  
1. Analyze the given {theme} and interpret it through the lens of the {tone}.  
2. Tailor language, diction, and complexity to resonate with the specified {audience}.  
3. Integrate the {style} and {form} to structure the poem cohesively, respecting poetic conventions.  
4. Weave the provided {imagery} and {symbols} naturally into the verses to enrich meaning and depth.  
5. Ensure the poem evokes strong emotional or intellectual responses, maintaining clarity and originality throughout.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: Between 300 and 500 words  
- Format: Written in stanzas consistent with the chosen {form}  
- Style: Reflective, vivid, and aligned with the {tone}  
- Structure: Clear thematic progression with balanced rhythm and flow

**Output Format:**  
A polished poem meeting all input parameters, formatted with appropriate stanza breaks and consistent with the requested poetic form and style.

#### Use Case:  
Ideal for poets, creative writers, educators, or content creators seeking to generate thoughtful, theme-driven poetry tailored for specific audiences and purposes. Useful in literary projects, workshops, or personalized poetry commissions where emotional resonance and artistic expression are paramount.
