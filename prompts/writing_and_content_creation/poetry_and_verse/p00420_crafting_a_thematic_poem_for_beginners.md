### P-00420 - Crafting a Thematic Poem for Beginners

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative writing` `theme` `beginner`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**

**Context:**  
You are a novice poet seeking to create an engaging poem that captures the essence of a chosen theme. The poem should be clear, expressive, and demonstrate basic poetic techniques such as metaphor, simile, and rhythmic flow.

- **Theme and Tone:** {theme}, {tone} (the central subject and mood of the poem)

- **Imagery Elements:** {imagery1}, {imagery2} (two specific images or sensory details to include)  
- **Target Audience:** {audience} (intended readers, e.g., children, adults, general audience)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Select the theme and tone to guide the mood and subject matter of the poem.  
2. Choose the poem type to determine structure and style.  
3. Incorporate the two imagery elements to enrich sensory experience.  
4. Write the poem ensuring the language is accessible for beginners while evoking emotion.  
5. Review and refine the poem to maintain clarity, rhythm, and adherence to the chosen theme and tone.

- Length: 150-300 words
