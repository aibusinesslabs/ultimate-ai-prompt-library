### P-01094 - Crafting a Compelling Short Film Script

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `scriptwriting` `shortfilm` `screenplay` `creativewriting`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a concise and engaging short film script based on the given theme, characters, and setting.

**Context:**  
You are a screenwriter tasked with developing a beginner-friendly short film script. The script should effectively introduce characters, establish a setting, and convey a clear conflict and resolution within a brief format.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Genre:** {theme}, {genre} (central idea and tone of the short film)  
- **Characters:** {main_character}, {supporting_character} (names and brief traits of up to two characters)  
- **Setting:** {location}, {time_period} (where and when the story takes place)  
- **Conflict:** {central_conflict} (main problem or challenge driving the story)

**Instructions:**  
1. Write a short film script between 150 and 300 words.  
2. Introduce the main and supporting characters naturally within the opening scenes.  
3. Establish the setting clearly through description or dialogue.  
4. Present the central conflict early and develop it through character interactions.  
5. Conclude with a resolution or an impactful ending that aligns with the theme.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Script format with character names and dialogue clearly indicated  
- Style: Clear, engaging, and beginner-friendly language  
- Structure: Include scene headings, dialogue, and brief action descriptions  

**Output Format:**  
A formatted short film script containing scene headings, character names, dialogue, and minimal stage directions, suitable for beginner screenwriters.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for aspiring screenwriters or creative writing students looking to practice concise storytelling in script format. It helps develop skills in character creation, conflict development, and script structuring within a manageable word count.
