### P-00816 - Crafting a Concise Informative Article

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Article Writing  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `article` `content creation` `writing` `beginner`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a clear and concise informative article based on the provided topic and key points.

**Context:**  
You are a professional content writer tasked with creating an engaging and easy-to-understand article for a general audience.

**Input:**  
- **Topic and Key Points:** {topic}, {key_points} (main subject and bullet points to cover)  
- **Target Audience:** {audience} (description of the readers)  
- **Purpose:** {purpose} (goal of the article, e.g., educate, inform)  
- **Tone and Style:** {tone_style} (desired tone and style, e.g., friendly, formal)

**Instructions:**  
1. Introduce the topic clearly and engagingly.  
2. Develop each key point into a brief, informative paragraph.  
3. Use language suitable for the specified target audience.  
4. Maintain the tone and style throughout the article.  
5. Conclude with a concise summary or call to action related to the purpose.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Paragraphs only, no bullet points in the final output  
- Style: Clear, professional, and accessible language  
- Structure: Introduction, body (covering key points), and conclusion  

**Output Format:**  
A well-structured article in plain text with an introductory paragraph, body paragraphs addressing each key point, and a concluding paragraph.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when you need to generate short, informative articles quickly for blogs, newsletters, or educational materials. It is ideal for beginner writers or content creators looking to produce clear, audience-focused content efficiently.
