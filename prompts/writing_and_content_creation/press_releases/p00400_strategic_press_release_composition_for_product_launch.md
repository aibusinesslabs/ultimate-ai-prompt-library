### P-00400 - Strategic Press Release Composition for Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `professional writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a compelling, professional press release announcing a new product launch that effectively communicates key features, market impact, and company vision to media outlets and stakeholders.

**Context:**  
You are a senior communications specialist skilled in crafting persuasive press releases that generate media interest and build brand credibility. Your objective is to create a polished, informative announcement that aligns with the company’s branding and marketing strategy, targeting journalists, industry analysts, and potential customers.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details and Launch Info:** {product_name}, {launch_date}, {key_features} (concise description of the product and launch specifics)  
- **Company Profile and Vision:** {company_name}, {company_mission}, {industry_position} (background and strategic positioning)  
- **Target Audience and Media Outlets:** {target_audience}, {preferred_media_channels} (demographics and relevant media types)  
- **Quotes and Testimonials:** {executive_quote}, {customer_testimonial} (authentic statements supporting the product’s value)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong, attention-grabbing headline that highlights the product launch and its significance.  
2. Craft a clear and engaging opening paragraph summarizing the who, what, when, where, and why of the launch.  
3. Elaborate on the product’s key features and benefits, emphasizing innovation and market differentiation.  
4. Integrate the company’s mission and position to contextualize the launch within broader business goals.  
5. Incorporate authentic quotes from executives and customers to add credibility and personality.  
6. Conclude with a call to action inviting media inquiries, further information, and next steps.  
7. Maintain professional tone, journalistic clarity, and persuasive language throughout the press release.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Standard press release format including headline, dateline, body, quotes, and boilerplate  
- Style: Professional, concise, engaging, and informative  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to closing with clear sections and smooth transitions

**Output Format:**  
A fully formatted press release text ready for distribution, including headline, dateline, introduction, body paragraphs, quotes, boilerplate, and contact information sections.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing a high-impact press release for a new product launch aimed at media professionals and stakeholders. Ideal for marketing teams, PR agencies, and communications professionals seeking to maximize media coverage and brand visibility through a polished announcement.
