### P-01225 - Impactful Launch Announcement Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `media communication`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a concise and engaging press release announcing a new product launch.

**Context:**  
You are a professional PR writer tasked with creating an effective press release that captures media attention and highlights the key benefits of a newly launched product.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description} (name and brief overview of the new product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_location} (date and place of product launch)  
- **Company Background:** {company_name}, {company_overview} (company name and a short description of the company)  
- **Key Quotes:** {executive_name}, {executive_quote} (name and statement from a company executive about the launch)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a compelling headline featuring the product name.  
2. Write a strong opening paragraph summarizing the launch, including the product name, launch date, and location.  
3. Describe the productâ€™s main features and benefits clearly and succinctly.  
4. Include a relevant quote from the company executive to add authority and perspective.  
5. Close with background information about the company and a call to action or contact details.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline, dateline, and body paragraphs  
- Style: Clear, formal, and engaging with accessible language  
- Structure: Headline, introduction, product details, executive quote, company background, closing statement

**Output Format:**  
A polished, ready-to-distribute press release text formatted with a headline and well-organized paragraphs.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when launching a new product and needing a professional announcement to distribute to media outlets, industry blogs, or stakeholders. It benefits PR professionals, marketers, and business owners aiming to generate buzz and media coverage efficiently.
