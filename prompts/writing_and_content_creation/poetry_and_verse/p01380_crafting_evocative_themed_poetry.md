### P-01380 - Crafting Evocative Themed Poetry

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative_writing` `themes` `emotions`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a short poem that vividly explores a specific theme and evokes strong emotions in the reader.

**Context:**  
You are a creative poet tasked with writing original verse that captures the essence of a given theme and conveys emotional depth in an accessible, engaging style suitable for beginner poets.

**Input:**  
- **Theme & Emotion**: {theme}, {emotion} (central idea and feeling to inspire the poem)  
- **Tone & Style**: {tone}, {style} (desired mood and poetic style, e.g., contemplative, joyful, haiku, free verse)  
- **Length**: {min_words}, {max_words} (minimum and maximum word count for the poem)  
- **Imagery Preferences**: {imagery} (optional types of imagery or symbols to include, e.g., nature, seasons, colors)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin by reflecting on the {theme} and how it naturally connects to the {emotion}.  
2. Choose language and imagery that enhance the {tone} and fit the {style} specified.  
3. Compose an original poem within the {min_words} to {max_words} word range.  
4. Incorporate the suggested {imagery} if provided to enrich sensory detail.  
5. Review and refine the poem to ensure clarity, emotional impact, and thematic coherence.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: Between {min_words} and {max_words} words (150-300 words for beginner level)  
- Format: Plain text poem without titles or stanza labels  
- Style: Clear, evocative, and beginner-friendly poetic language  
- Structure: Flexible (free verse or simple rhyme), focusing on emotional resonance

**Output Format:**  
A single, coherent poem of 150-300 words capturing the specified theme and emotion in the requested tone and style.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for beginner poets seeking guidance on crafting meaningful poems tailored to specific themes and emotions. It is useful in educational settings, creative writing workshops, or personal practice to develop poetic skills and emotional expression.
