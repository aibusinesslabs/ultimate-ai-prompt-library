### P-01015 - Crafting Impactful Product Launch Press Releases

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a compelling and professional press release announcing the launch of a new product that effectively communicates key features, benefits, and company vision to the target audience.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled communications specialist working for a company preparing to announce its latest product. Your goal is to create an engaging press release that captures media interest, informs stakeholders, and generates positive buzz around the launch.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_type}, {key_features} (name, category, and main attributes of the product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (organization name and brief background)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {availability_info} (official release date and where/how customers can purchase)  
- **Target Audience & Benefits:** {target_audience}, {product_benefits} (intended users and advantages the product offers)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline featuring the product name and launch announcement.  
2. Write an engaging opening paragraph that summarizes the announcement and key highlights.  
3. Elaborate on the productâ€™s features and how they address the needs of the target audience.  
4. Include relevant company information to establish credibility and context.  
5. Provide specific launch details such as date and availability, concluding with a call to action or contact information.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Formal press release format with a headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and boilerplate  
- Style: Professional, clear, and persuasive with a journalistic tone  
- Structure: Logical flow from announcement to details, ending with company boilerplate and media contact info

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution, formatted with headline, dateline, body, and boilerplate sections.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for marketing teams, PR professionals, and content creators who need to draft effective press releases for new product launches. It helps ensure messaging is clear, engaging, and aligned with brand positioning to maximize media coverage and customer interest.
