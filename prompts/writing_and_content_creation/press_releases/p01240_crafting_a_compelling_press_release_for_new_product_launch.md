### P-01240 - Crafting a Compelling Press Release for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `content writing` `marketing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional press release announcing a new product launch that effectively highlights key features and benefits to attract media attention and customer interest.

**Context:**  
You are a communications specialist tasked with creating a clear, concise, and engaging press release for a company launching a new product. The release should inform journalists and potential customers about the product, its unique selling points, and the launch details.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_type}, {key_features} (name, category, and top features of the product)  
- **Company Info:** {company_name}, {company_background} (company name and brief background)  
- **Launch Info:** {launch_date}, {availability} (official launch date and where the product will be available)  
- **Contact Info:** {media_contact_name}, {media_contact_email} (contact person and email for press inquiries)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that includes the product name and launch announcement.  
2. Write an introductory paragraph summarizing the product and launch purpose.  
3. Detail the productâ€™s key features and benefits in a clear, engaging manner.  
4. Include relevant company background to build credibility.  
5. Provide launch date, availability information, and media contact details at the end.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline, body paragraphs, and contact section  
- Style: Clear, concise, and engaging, suitable for journalists and general audience  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to body with a strong closing including contact info

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution, including headline, body content, and contact information section.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing a press release for a new product launch to ensure all essential information is communicated professionally. Ideal for marketing teams, PR professionals, and small business owners seeking to generate media coverage and customer interest efficiently.
