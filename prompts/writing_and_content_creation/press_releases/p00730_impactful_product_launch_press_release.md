### P-00730 - Impactful Product Launch Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a compelling press release announcing the launch of a new product, designed to generate media interest and engage potential customers.

**Context:**  
You are a professional PR writer tasked with crafting an engaging and informative press release for a company’s latest product launch. Your goal is to clearly communicate the product’s unique features, benefits, and availability while maintaining a professional tone suitable for media distribution.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_category}, {key_features} (name, type, and main features of the product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (company name and brief background)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {availability} (official launch date and where/how the product will be available)  
- **Quotes & Contact:** {spokesperson_name}, {spokesperson_title}, {contact_email} (quote attribution and media contact details)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that highlights the product launch and its significance.  
2. Write an engaging opening paragraph summarizing the product and its impact.  
3. Detail the product’s key features and benefits in clear, concise language.  
4. Include relevant information about the company and its expertise to build credibility.  
5. Add a quote from the designated spokesperson to personalize and endorse the launch.  
6. Specify the launch date and product availability details.  
7. Conclude with a call-to-action and provide media contact information for follow-up.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and contact info  
- Style: Clear, concise, engaging, and formal tone appropriate for journalists and industry professionals  
- Structure: Logical flow with distinct headline, introduction, body, quote, and closing sections  

**Output Format:**  
A well-structured press release text ready for distribution, including headline, dateline, body paragraphs, quoted statement, and media contact information.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing press releases for new product launches to ensure clear communication and strong media appeal. Ideal for marketing teams, PR agencies, and business owners looking to maximize the visibility of their latest offerings.
