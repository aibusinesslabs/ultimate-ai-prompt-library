### P-00150 - Crafting a Thematic Poem with Emotional Depth

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative_writing` `thematic` `beginner`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose an original poem that conveys a specific theme and evokes the intended emotion through vivid imagery and expressive language.

**Context:**  
You are a poet tasked with creating a meaningful and engaging poem that resonates with readers by exploring a particular theme and emotional tone. The poem should be accessible to a general audience while showcasing creativity and clarity.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Emotion:** {theme}, {emotion} (the central idea and feeling to be expressed)  
- **Poem Type:** {poem_type} (e.g., free verse, sonnet, haiku, limerick)  
- **Imagery Elements:** {imagery_list} (list of 3-5 sensory details or metaphors to include)  
- **Tone and Style:** {tone_style} (desired tone such as hopeful, melancholic, inspirational; and style preferences)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin by reflecting on the given theme and emotion to capture the core message.  
2. Choose the poem type specified and structure your poem accordingly.  
3. Incorporate the provided imagery elements to create vivid and sensory-rich lines.  
4. Use the indicated tone and style to shape the mood and voice of the poem.  
5. Ensure the poem flows naturally and maintains coherence throughout.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Poetic form matching the selected poem type  
- Style: Clear, expressive, and emotionally resonant language appropriate for beginners  
- Structure: Follow the structural rules of the chosen poem type (if applicable)

**Output Format:**  
Provide the completed poem text only, formatted according to the specified poem type, with a title reflecting the theme.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for beginner writers seeking to practice thematic poetry with emotional expression. It benefits educators, students, and creative writers looking to develop foundational skills in poetry composition within a guided framework.
