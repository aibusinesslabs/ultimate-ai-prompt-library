### P-01482 - Engaging Landing Page Copy for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Landing Pages  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `landing page` `copywriting` `product launch` `marketing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a compelling and concise landing page copy that highlights the key benefits and features of a new product, encouraging visitors to take action.

**Context:**  
You are a professional copywriter tasked with drafting landing page content for a company launching a new product. The goal is to engage potential customers, clearly communicate the product’s value, and prompt conversions.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details**: {product_name}, {product_category}, {primary_feature} (basic information and main selling point)  
- **Target Audience**: {audience_description} (demographics and pain points)  
- **Unique Selling Proposition**: {usp} (what makes the product stand out)  
- **Call to Action**: {cta_text} (desired user action such as “Buy Now” or “Sign Up”)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a captivating headline using the product name and category.  
2. Introduce the product with a brief overview emphasizing the primary feature.  
3. Address the target audience’s needs and how the product resolves their pain points.  
4. Highlight the unique selling proposition clearly and persuasively.  
5. End with a strong, clear call to action encouraging immediate user engagement.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Plain text, suitable for web landing page  
- Style: Professional, engaging, and easy to understand  
- Structure: Headline, introduction, benefits, USP, call to action (in that order)

**Output Format:**  
A fully written landing page copy text block adhering to the structure and style requirements.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for marketers, copywriters, and small business owners launching new products who need quick, effective landing page content to boost conversions and engage their target market. It simplifies the creation process by focusing on clarity and persuasion for beginner-level users.
