### P-01154 - Crafting a Compelling Short Film Script

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `screenwriting` `short film` `storytelling` `script format`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a concise and engaging short film script that effectively conveys a clear story arc within a limited timeframe.

**Context:**  
You are a professional screenwriter tasked with creating a short film script that captures viewersâ€™ attention quickly and delivers a meaningful narrative suitable for a debut project or film festival submission.

**Input:**  
- **Story Details:** {main_character}, {setting}, {central_conflict} (key elements of the story)  
- **Genre and Tone:** {genre}, {tone} (e.g., drama, comedy, suspense; lighthearted, serious)  
- **Duration:** {duration_minutes} (intended length of the short film)  
- **Key Dialogue or Scenes:** {highlighted_moments} (optional important lines or scenes to include)

**Instructions:**  
1. Develop a clear beginning that introduces the {main_character} and {setting}.  
2. Establish the {central_conflict} early to engage the audience.  
3. Use the {genre} and {tone} to shape the dialogue and pacing.  
4. Structure the script to fit within the {duration_minutes} time frame, keeping it concise and focused.  
5. Incorporate any {highlighted_moments} naturally into the script to enhance emotional impact or plot development.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Standard screenplay format with scene headings, character names, and dialogue  
- Style: Clear, accessible language suitable for beginner screenwriters  
- Structure: Three-act structure (setup, confrontation, resolution) within a short format  

**Output Format:**  
A formatted short film script that includes scene headings, character names centered above dialogue lines, and clear stage directions where necessary.

#### Use Case:  
Ideal for aspiring screenwriters looking to practice or create a polished short film script quickly. This prompt helps beginners focus on core storytelling elements and script formatting to develop their skills for film festivals, portfolio building, or class assignments.
