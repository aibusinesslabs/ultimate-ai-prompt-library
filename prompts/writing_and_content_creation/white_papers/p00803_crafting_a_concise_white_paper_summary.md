### P-00803 - Crafting a Concise White Paper Summary

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** White Papers  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `white paper` `summary` `professional writing` `business communication`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a concise and professional summary for a white paper that highlights its key points and value proposition.

**Context:**  
You are an experienced business writer tasked with summarizing detailed white papers for busy executives who need quick insights without reading the full document.

**Input:**  
- **Document Details:** {white_paper_title}, {author_name} (title and author of the white paper)  
- **Core Content:** {main_findings}, {key_recommendations} (summary of main findings and recommendations)  
- **Target Audience:** {industry}, {audience_type} (industry focus and primary audience)  
- **Purpose:** {objective} (primary goal or objective of the white paper)

**Instructions:**  
1. Introduce the white paper by stating its title, author, and purpose.  
2. Summarize the main findings in clear, accessible language.  
3. Highlight key recommendations or solutions provided.  
4. Tailor the tone and focus to the specified industry and audience type.  
5. Close with a brief statement on the white paperâ€™s overall value or impact.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Paragraph form, no bullet points  
- Style: Professional, clear, and engaging  
- Structure: Introduction, body summary, conclusion

**Output Format:**  
A polished white paper summary suitable for use in executive briefs, marketing materials, or internal communications.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt to efficiently generate executive summaries for white papers that help decision-makers grasp essential information quickly. Ideal for marketing teams, business consultants, and content creators producing digestible content from technical documents.
