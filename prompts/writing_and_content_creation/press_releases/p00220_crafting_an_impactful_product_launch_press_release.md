### P-00220 - Crafting an Impactful Product Launch Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional and engaging press release announcing a new product launch that highlights its key features, benefits, and market impact.

**Context:**  
You are a communications specialist tasked with creating a compelling press release to generate media interest and inform the target audience about an upcoming product launch by a technology company.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description} (name and overview of the new product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_location} (date and place of the official launch event)  
- **Key Features & Benefits:** {features_list}, {benefits_summary} (detailed features and primary advantages of the product)  
- **Company Background:** {company_name}, {company_overview} (brief company introduction and brand positioning)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong, attention-grabbing headline that includes the product name and launch announcement.  
2. Craft an introductory paragraph summarizing the launch event and product’s significance.  
3. Elaborate on the product’s key features and benefits with clear, persuasive language.  
4. Include relevant company background to build credibility and context.  
5. End with a call-to-action or contact information for media inquiries.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Formal press release style with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and boilerplate  
- Style: Professional, clear, and concise with engaging yet factual tone  
- Structure: Logical flow from introduction to detailed information, concluding with contact details  

**Output Format:**  
A complete press release document formatted with a headline, dateline, body content segmented into paragraphs, and a company boilerplate at the end.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing a press release to announce a new product launch to journalists, industry influencers, and potential customers. Ideal for PR professionals and content creators needing a structured and effective communication tool to maximize media coverage and audience engagement.
