### P-01210 - Impactful Product Launch Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional and engaging press release announcing a new product launch that effectively communicates the product’s features, benefits, and market impact to media and potential customers.

**Context:**  
You are a PR specialist tasked with crafting a clear, compelling press release for a technology company introducing their latest product. Your goal is to generate interest, build brand credibility, and encourage media coverage.

**Input:**  
- **Company & Product Details:** {company_name}, {product_name}, {product_category} (basic company name, product name, and category)  
- **Product Features & Benefits:** {key_features}, {unique_selling_points} (highlight the main product functionalities and what sets it apart)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {availability} (date of launch and where/how the product will be accessible)  
- **Quotes & Contact:** {executive_quote}, {media_contact} (a quote from a company executive and media contact details for follow-up)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that captures attention and clearly states the product launch.  
2. Write a compelling opening paragraph summarizing the product’s significance and launch details.  
3. Detail the key features and unique selling points, emphasizing customer benefits and market differentiation.  
4. Include the quote from the company executive to add a personal, authoritative voice.  
5. Conclude with information on product availability and contact details for media inquiries.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Formal press release layout with headline, dateline, body, quote, and boilerplate sections  
- Style: Professional, concise, and persuasive language suitable for media professionals  
- Structure: Logical flow from introduction to details, quote, and closing information

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution, formatted with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, executive quote, and media contact information.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for PR professionals or content creators preparing press releases for new product launches. It helps ensure the announcement is clear, engaging, and media-friendly, increasing the chances of positive coverage and customer interest.
