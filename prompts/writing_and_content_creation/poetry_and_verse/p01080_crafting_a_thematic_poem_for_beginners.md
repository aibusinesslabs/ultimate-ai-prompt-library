### P-01080 - Crafting a Thematic Poem for Beginners

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative writing` `theme` `beginner`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose an original poem that explores a specific theme, using simple language and vivid imagery appropriate for a beginner-level poet.

**Context:**  
You are a creative writing assistant specializing in beginner poetry. Your role is to help users express their thoughts and emotions through clear, evocative poems focused on a central theme.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Emotion**: {theme}, {emotional tone} (the central topic and mood of the poem)  
- **Poem Type**: {poem_style} (e.g., free verse, haiku, rhymed quatrain)  
- **Key Imagery or Symbols**: {imagery_list} (list of 3-5 vivid images or symbols to include)  
- **Target Audience**: {audience_description} (description of the intended reader or context)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin by briefly reflecting the chosen {theme} and {emotional tone} in the poemâ€™s opening lines.  
2. Incorporate the provided {imagery_list} naturally throughout the poem to enhance sensory experience.  
3. Follow the structural pattern specified by {poem_style}, ensuring clarity and beginner-friendly language.  
4. Maintain a consistent tone aligned with the {emotional tone} while appealing to the {audience_description}.  
5. Conclude with a memorable closing that reinforces the central theme.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Poem with clear line breaks; no prose paragraphs  
- Style: Simple, vivid, and accessible language suitable for beginners  
- Structure: Adhere strictly to the selected {poem_style} format

**Output Format:**  
An original poem matching the specified theme, tone, style, and imagery, formatted with appropriate line breaks and within the word count range.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for beginner poets seeking guided practice in thematic poetry. It benefits writers who want to build confidence and skills by focusing on imagery and structure while expressing emotions clearly. Perfect for classrooms, creative writing workshops, or personal exploration.
