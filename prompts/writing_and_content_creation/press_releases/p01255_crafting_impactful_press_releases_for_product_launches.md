### P-01255 - Crafting Impactful Press Releases for Product Launches

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a professional, engaging press release announcing a new product launch that effectively communicates key information and generates media interest.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned PR specialist tasked with drafting a press release for a company introducing its latest product. The press release should balance factual information with persuasive language to attract journalists, potential customers, and stakeholders.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {{product_name}}, {{product_description}} (Name and concise overview of the new product)  
- **Launch Information:** {{launch_date}}, {{launch_location}} (Date and place of the product launch event)  
- **Company Background:** {{company_name}}, {{company_overview}} (Name and brief description of the company)  
- **Quotes and Contact:** {{quote_from_ceo}}, {{media_contact_info}} (A notable quote from the CEO or spokesperson and contact details for press inquiries)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a compelling headline including the product name and launch highlight.  
2. Write an introductory paragraph summarizing the announcement with the launch date and its significance.  
3. Elaborate on the product features and benefits within the body, incorporating the {{product_description}}.  
4. Include the {{quote_from_ceo}} to add credibility and a human touch.  
5. Close with company background information, launch details, and clear media contact information.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Formal press release structure with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and boilerplate  
- Style: Professional, clear, and engaging tone suitable for journalists and industry audiences  
- Structure: Logical flow with distinct sections for easy readability  

**Output Format:**  
A complete press release text ready for distribution, formatted with a headline, dateline, body copy, quote, boilerplate, and contact details.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for PR professionals, marketers, or content creators who need to generate polished press releases for product launches quickly. It ensures consistent quality and persuasive messaging to maximize media pickup and audience engagement.
