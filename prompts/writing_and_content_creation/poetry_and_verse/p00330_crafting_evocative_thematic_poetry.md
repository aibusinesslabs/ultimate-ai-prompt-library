### P-00330 - Crafting Evocative Thematic Poetry

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative writing` `thematic` `verse structure`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose an original poem that explores a specific theme with vivid imagery and emotional depth, employing a defined poetic form.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled poet tasked with creating a compelling piece that not only captures the essence of the given theme but also adheres to a chosen poetic structure to enhance its impact and readability.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Tone:** {theme}, {tone} (the central subject and overall mood of the poem)  
- **Poetic Form and Structure:** {form}, {lines_per_stanza} (e.g., sonnet, haiku, free verse; stanza length)  
- **Imagery and Devices:** {imagery_style}, {literary_devices} (types of imagery and literary techniques to incorporate)  
- **Target Audience and Purpose:** {audience}, {purpose} (intended readers and goal of the poem, e.g., inspire, reflect, mourn)

**Instructions:**  
1. Analyze the provided theme and tone to understand the emotional core to convey.  
2. Select and apply the specified poetic form and stanza structure consistently throughout the poem.  
3. Integrate the requested imagery style and literary devices (such as metaphor, alliteration, symbolism) to enrich the poemâ€™s texture.  
4. Tailor language and content to resonate with the target audience and fulfill the stated purpose.  
5. Review and refine the poem to ensure clarity, emotional resonance, and adherence to the form and word count.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Poem with stanzas matching the specified structure  
- Style: Evocative, emotionally engaging, and thematically coherent  
- Structure: Consistent poetic form and stanza length as indicated in inputs

**Output Format:**  
Return the completed poem in plain text, preserving stanza breaks and line formatting.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for poets, creative writers, and educators seeking to produce themed poetry with structural discipline. It benefits users who want to blend creativity with formality to craft meaningful verse suited for publication, performance, or study.
