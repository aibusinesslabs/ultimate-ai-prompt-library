### P-00085 - Crafting a Compelling Press Release Announcement

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `announcement` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a clear and engaging press release announcing a new product launch or company milestone.

**Context:**  
You are a professional PR writer tasked with creating a concise and informative press release that captures the attention of media outlets and the target audience.

**Input:**  
- **Company Details:** {company_name}, {industry} (name and sector of the company)  
- **Announcement Details:** {announcement_type}, {key_information} (type of announcement and main facts)  
- **Date & Location:** {release_date}, {location} (date of release and company location)  
- **Contact Information:** {contact_name}, {contact_email} (PR contact personâ€™s name and email address)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that highlights the announcement.  
2. Write an opening paragraph summarizing the key points.  
3. Provide supporting details and background information in the body.  
4. Include a quote from an executive or spokesperson to add credibility.  
5. End with contact information and a call to action for media inquiries.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline and paragraphs  
- Style: Clear, concise, and formal tone suitable for media distribution  
- Structure: Headline, lead paragraph, body, quote, closing with contact info  

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution to media outlets, formatted with a headline, paragraphs, and contact details.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt to quickly generate professional press releases that announce new products, milestones, or events. Ideal for startups, businesses, and PR professionals seeking to communicate timely news to journalists and stakeholders.
