### P-00355 - Crafting a Concise Company Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `company news` `public relations` `content writing`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a clear and professional press release announcing a new company development, designed to inform media outlets and the public.

**Context:**  
You are a communications specialist tasked with drafting a concise press release for a company. The release should effectively highlight key information and maintain a formal yet engaging tone suitable for distribution to journalists and stakeholders.

**Input:**  
- **Company Details:** {company_name}, {industry}, {headquarters_location} (basic company info)  
- **Announcement Details:** {announcement_type}, {announcement_date}, {key_message} (main news and purpose)  
- **Supporting Information:** {quotes}, {background_context} (optional statements and relevant context)  
- **Contact Information:** {contact_name}, {contact_email}, {contact_phone} (media contact details)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline summarizing the announcement.  
2. Write a concise introductory paragraph covering the who, what, when, where, and why.  
3. Include relevant background information and a meaningful quote from a company representative.  
4. Provide additional context that supports the announcementâ€™s significance.  
5. End with clear contact details for media inquiries and a brief company boilerplate.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and contact info  
- Style: Professional, formal, clear, and engaging  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to conclusion, including a quote and contact section  

**Output Format:**  
A complete, well-structured press release text ready for distribution, formatted with a headline, dateline, body, quote, boilerplate, and media contact section.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when needing to quickly generate effective press releases for company announcements like product launches, partnerships, or milestones. Ideal for PR teams, marketing professionals, and small business owners seeking polished communication materials without extensive writing resources.
