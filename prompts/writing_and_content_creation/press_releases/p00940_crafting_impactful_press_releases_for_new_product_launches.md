### P-00940 - Crafting Impactful Press Releases for New Product Launches

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `media relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional press release announcing a new product launch that effectively communicates the product’s key features, benefits, and availability to media outlets and potential customers.

**Context:**  
You are a communications specialist tasked with creating a polished, engaging press release for a company unveiling its latest product. The goal is to generate media interest, inform the public, and position the product competitively in the market.

**Input:**  
- **Company and Product Details:** {company_name}, {product_name}, {product_type} (Basic identifying information and product category)  
- **Key Features and Benefits:** {features_list}, {benefits_summary} (Core functionalities and advantages of the product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {availability_regions} (When and where the product will be available)  
- **Additional Quotes or Statements:** {executive_quote} (Optional quote from a company executive or product manager)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a compelling headline that highlights the product launch.  
2. Write a strong introductory paragraph summarizing the announcement.  
3. Detail the product’s key features and benefits clearly and persuasively.  
4. Include launch date and availability information to inform timing and access.  
5. Incorporate the provided executive quote to add credibility and a personal touch.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, subheadline (optional), dateline, body, and boilerplate  
- Style: Professional, clear, and engaging tone suitable for media and public audiences  
- Structure: Logical flow from announcement to details, concluding with contact information or company background  

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution, adhering to typical industry formatting and style conventions.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing official announcements for new product launches to ensure clear communication and media readiness. Ideal for PR professionals, marketing teams, and content creators aiming to maximize launch impact and media coverage.
