### P-00190 - Crafting Impactful Press Releases for Maximum Media Engagement

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `press release` `media relations` `public relations` `content marketing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional, compelling press release that effectively announces a company’s major announcement, designed to engage media outlets and target audiences while adhering to journalistic standards.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned public relations specialist tasked with creating a polished press release for a company’s significant news event. The goal is to capture attention, convey key information clearly, and motivate media coverage and public interest.

**Input:**  
- **Company Details:** {company_name}, {industry}, {location} (basic info about the company issuing the press release)  
- **Announcement Details:** {announcement_type}, {key_features}, {launch_date} (nature and specifics of the announcement)  
- **Quotes:** {executive_name}, {executive_position}, {quote_content} (a relevant, impactful quote from a company leader)  
- **Contact Information:** {contact_name}, {contact_email}, {contact_phone} (media contact details for follow-up)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong, engaging headline that summarizes the announcement clearly and concisely.  
2. Write a captivating subheadline that supports the headline and adds context.  
3. Craft a lead paragraph that answers the who, what, when, where, why, and how of the announcement.  
4. Develop subsequent paragraphs to elaborate on the announcement’s benefits, implications, and any unique selling points using clear and persuasive language.  
5. Integrate the provided executive quote naturally to add credibility and a human perspective.  
6. Include relevant background information about the company to establish authority and context.  
7. Close with a call to action and provide the media contact information clearly for follow-up inquiries.  
8. Ensure the tone is professional, factual, and aligned with journalistic standards while maintaining brand voice.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, subheadline, dateline, body paragraphs, and contact info  
- Style: Formal, concise, persuasive, and free of jargon or promotional exaggeration  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to closing statement with clear separation of sections and proper use of paragraphs

**Output Format:**  
A complete press release text formatted with:  
- Headline (bold, centered)  
- Subheadline (italicized, centered)  
- Dateline (city, state, date)  
- Body paragraphs  
- Executive quote paragraph (with attribution)  
- Boilerplate company description paragraph  
- Media contact information at the end  

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt whenever you need to produce a high-quality press release that demands attention from journalists and stakeholders. Ideal for PR professionals, content creators, and marketing teams announcing product launches, corporate milestones, partnerships, or other newsworthy events.
