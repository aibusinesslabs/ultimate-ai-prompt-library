### P-00160 - Crafting Impactful Product Launch Press Releases

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional press release announcing a new product launch that effectively highlights key features, company vision, and market impact to engage media and target audiences.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled PR specialist tasked with creating a compelling product launch press release for a company entering a competitive market. The press release must communicate the product’s unique value and align with the company’s brand messaging.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_features} (name and key attributes of the product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (company name and brief background)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {availability} (official launch date and where/how the product will be available)  
- **Market Impact:** {target_audience}, {competitive_advantage} (intended audience and unique selling points against competitors)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that captures the essence of the product launch.  
2. Write an engaging opening paragraph summarizing the product and its significance.  
3. Elaborate on the product features and benefits tailored to the target audience.  
4. Incorporate relevant company background that supports the product’s credibility and vision.  
5. Conclude with launch details, availability information, and a call to action for media or customers.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Standard press release structure with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and contact info placeholder  
- Style: Professional, clear, and persuasive tone suitable for media distribution  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to conclusion, with concise paragraphs and no technical jargon overload

**Output Format:**  
A neatly formatted press release text ready for distribution, including:  
- Headline  
- Dateline (City, Date)  
- Body (3-4 paragraphs)  
- Contact information placeholder at the end

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing to announce a new product launch to journalists, stakeholders, or customers, ensuring a polished and persuasive message. Ideal for PR professionals and marketers aiming to generate media coverage and build brand awareness during product introductions.
