### P-00070 - Crafting Impactful Press Releases for Product Launches

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a compelling, professional press release announcing a new product launch that effectively communicates key product features and company values while engaging media and potential customers.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled PR professional tasked with drafting a press release for a company unveiling its latest product. The goal is to generate interest, provide clear and concise information, and encourage media coverage and consumer engagement.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_features} (name and key features of the product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (company name and brief background)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {availability} (official launch date and product availability information)  
- **Contact Information:** {contact_person}, {contact_email} (media contact person and email address for follow-up)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that highlights the product launch.  
2. Write an engaging opening paragraph summarizing the product and its significance.  
3. Detail key product features and benefits, linking them to customer needs or market trends.  
4. Include a brief paragraph about the companyâ€™s background and its mission or values relevant to the product.  
5. Provide launch date, availability, and clear contact information for media inquiries.  
6. Maintain a professional tone with clear, concise language suitable for journalists and industry audiences.  
7. Ensure the press release follows an inverted pyramid style, prioritizing the most important information at the top.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, dateline, body, and boilerplate sections  
- Style: Formal, informative, engaging, and objective  
- Structure: Use short paragraphs, include quotes if appropriate, and close with company boilerplate and contact info

**Output Format:**  
A complete, formatted press release text ready for distribution, including headline, dateline, body paragraphs, boilerplate, and media contact details.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing professional press releases for new product launches to maximize media pickup and customer interest. PR managers, marketing professionals, and content creators will benefit from producing clear, engaging announcements tailored for industry and consumer audiences.
