### P-00600 - Crafting a Thematic Epic Poem with Emotional Depth

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `poetry` `epic` `creative_writing` `emotional_depth`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a richly detailed, thematic epic poem that explores a profound emotional journey, blending vivid imagery with sophisticated literary devices to evoke deep reader engagement.

**Context:**  
You are an accomplished poet and literary artist commissioned to create an original epic poem. Your goal is to capture a complex emotional arc—such as loss, transformation, or redemption—while employing formal poetic techniques that reflect classical epic traditions infused with modern sensibilities.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Emotional Focus:** {primary_theme}, {emotional_journey} (central theme and emotional trajectory to guide the poem)  
- **Setting and Time Period:** {setting_description} (the backdrop and era in which the poem unfolds)  
- **Key Characters and Symbols:** {main_characters}, {symbolic_elements} (principal figures and recurring motifs or symbols)  
- **Tone and Style Preferences:** {desired_tone}, {poetic_style} (overall mood and specific poetic form or style requested)

**Instructions:**  
1. Develop a narrative arc that traces the emotional journey defined in {emotional_journey}, ensuring thematic coherence with {primary_theme}.  
2. Employ vivid, sensory-rich imagery aligned with {setting_description} to create an immersive atmosphere.  
3. Integrate {main_characters} and weave {symbolic_elements} throughout the poem to enrich layers of meaning.  
4. Use advanced poetic techniques consistent with {poetic_style}—such as meter, rhyme scheme, alliteration, or enjambment—tailored to {desired_tone}.  
5. Conclude with a resonant resolution that encapsulates the emotional transformation and thematic essence.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Written as a continuous poem with stanzas (minimum 4 stanzas, max 8)  
- Style: Elevated, formal diction with sophisticated literary devices  
- Structure: Narrative progression with clear beginning, development, climax, and resolution reflecting an epic scope

**Output Format:**  
A single, polished epic poem matching the thematic and stylistic inputs, presented as stanzas with line breaks and consistent formatting suitable for publication or performance.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when seeking to generate an original, high-quality epic poem that balances classical poetic craft with deep emotional storytelling. Ideal for poets, writers, and educators aiming to create or analyze thematic verse with literary complexity and artistic impact.
