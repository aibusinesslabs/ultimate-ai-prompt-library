### P-00044 - Crafting a Compelling Feature Film Script Outline

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `screenwriting` `film` `story-structure` `character-development`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Develop a comprehensive and engaging feature film script outline that balances strong narrative arcs, well-defined characters, and thematic depth suitable for a 90-120 minute screenplay.

**Context:**  
You are an experienced screenwriter and story consultant tasked with creating a professional-grade script outline for a feature film. Your expertise ensures the story is well-structured, emotionally resonant, and market-ready, addressing key elements such as plot development, character motivations, and thematic coherence.

**Input:**  
- **Story Elements:** {{genre}}, {{logline}}, {{central theme}} (genre of the film, one-sentence story summary, and the underlying message or moral)  
- **Characters:** {{protagonist_name}}, {{protagonist_goal}}, {{antagonist_name}}, {{antagonist_goal}} (main characters and their primary objectives)  
- **Setting and Tone:** {{time_period}}, {{location}}, {{tone}} (when and where the story takes place, and the overall mood or style)  
- **Key Plot Points:** {{inciting_incident}}, {{midpoint_turn}}, {{climax}}, {{resolution}} (major story beats that define the narrative progression)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a succinct logline that encapsulates the story’s core conflict and stakes.  
2. Outline the three-act structure, detailing the setup, confrontation, and resolution phases clearly.  
3. Develop character arcs for the protagonist and antagonist that align with their goals and the story’s theme.  
4. Integrate the provided key plot points into the narrative flow, ensuring logical progression and escalating tension.  
5. Highlight the setting and tone throughout the outline to reinforce the story’s atmosphere and emotional impact.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Professional script outline with headings for Acts, Characters, and Key Plot Points  
- Style: Clear, concise, and engaging narrative language suitable for industry professionals  
- Structure: Follow a three-act format with distinct sections for character development and thematic exploration

**Output Format:**  
A formatted feature film script outline divided into:  
- Logline  
- Act I: Setup  
- Act II: Confrontation  
- Act III: Resolution  
- Character Arcs  
- Thematic Summary

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for screenwriters, filmmakers, and creative producers aiming to develop a polished script outline for presentation, pitching, or early-stage screenplay development. It supports generating a structured, professional blueprint that ensures narrative clarity and emotional engagement.
