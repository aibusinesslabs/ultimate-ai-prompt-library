### P-01272 - Compelling Landing Page Copy for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Landing Pages  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `landing page` `copywriting` `product launch` `marketing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a persuasive and engaging landing page copy that highlights the key benefits and features of a new product to drive conversions.

**Context:**  
You are a professional copywriter tasked with writing landing page content for a new product launch. The goal is to attract potential customers, clearly communicate the product’s value, and encourage them to take action.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {{product_name}}, {{product_features}} (name and key features of the product)  
- **Target Audience:** {{audience_description}} (description of the ideal customer or market segment)  
- **Primary Benefit:** {{main_benefit}} (most important advantage or solution the product offers)  
- **Call to Action:** {{cta_text}} (desired user action, e.g., “Buy Now,” “Sign Up”)

**Instructions:**  
1. Write a captivating headline featuring {{product_name}} that grabs attention.  
2. Craft a subheadline that clearly states {{main_benefit}} for {{audience_description}}.  
3. Summarize {{product_features}} in concise, benefit-focused bullet points.  
4. Include a persuasive paragraph reinforcing why the product solves a key problem for {{audience_description}}.  
5. End with a strong, clear {{cta_text}} encouraging immediate action.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Clear paragraphs with bullet points for features  
- Style: Professional, approachable, and persuasive  
- Structure: Headline, subheadline, feature bullets, persuasive paragraph, call to action  

**Output Format:**  
A fully formatted landing page copy text block ready for web publishing.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt to quickly generate effective landing page content for new product launches. Ideal for marketers, small business owners, and copywriters seeking beginner-friendly, conversion-focused copy that resonates with their target audience.
