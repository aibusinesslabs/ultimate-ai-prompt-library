### P-01360 - Crafting a Compelling Press Release for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `media communication`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a clear and engaging press release announcing the launch of a new product, highlighting its key features, benefits, and availability.

**Context:**  
You are a communications specialist tasked with drafting a professional press release that captures media attention and informs potential customers about a new product launch by a company.

**Input:**  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {industry} (name and sector of the company)  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_features} (name and main features of the product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {availability} (date of release and where it will be available)  
- **Contact Details:** {contact_name}, {contact_email} (media contact person and email address)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that includes the product name and launch announcement.  
2. Write an engaging opening paragraph summarizing the key news.  
3. Detail the productâ€™s main features and explain the benefits to the target audience.  
4. Include the launch date and availability information clearly.  
5. Close with contact information for media inquiries and a brief company background.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline, body, and contact section  
- Style: Clear, concise, and formal yet engaging  
- Structure: Logical flow from announcement to details, ending with contact info  

**Output Format:**  
A complete press release text ready for distribution, formatted with a headline, introductory paragraph, detailed body, and closing contact information.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when you need to generate a polished and effective press release for a new product launch. It benefits PR professionals, marketers, and business owners looking to communicate product news clearly and professionally to media outlets and customers.
