### P-00460 - Crafting an Engaging Press Release for Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `content writing` `PR`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a clear, concise, and engaging press release announcing the launch of a new product.

**Context:**  
You are a professional PR writer tasked with drafting a press release that grabs media attention and effectively communicates the key details of a product launch to a broad audience.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_features} (name and main features of the product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (company name and brief background)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {availability} (official launch date and how/where the product will be available)  
- **Contact Information:** {contact_person}, {contact_email} (media contact details for further inquiries)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a compelling headline that highlights the product launch.  
2. Write a strong opening paragraph summarizing the product and its significance.  
3. Elaborate on key product features and benefits in the body section.  
4. Include a brief introduction to the company and its mission or reputation.  
5. Close with launch date, availability details, and clear contact information for media follow-up.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline, body paragraphs, and closing  
- Style: Clear, concise, and engaging; avoid jargon and overly technical language  
- Structure: Headline, opening paragraph, body paragraphs, closing with contact info

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution, formatted with a headline, paragraphs, and contact details at the end.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing a press release for a new product launch to ensure the message is professional, media-friendly, and informative. Ideal for PR professionals, marketers, and small business owners seeking to announce new offerings effectively.
