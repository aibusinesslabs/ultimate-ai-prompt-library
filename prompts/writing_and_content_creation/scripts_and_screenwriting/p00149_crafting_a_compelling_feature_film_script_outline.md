### P-00149 - Crafting a Compelling Feature Film Script Outline

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `screenwriting` `feature_film` `story_structure` `character_development`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Develop a detailed and engaging feature film script outline that includes a clear three-act structure, well-defined character arcs, and key plot points to serve as a blueprint for full scriptwriting.

**Context:**  
You are an expert screenwriter tasked with creating a comprehensive outline for a feature-length film. The outline should balance narrative pacing, emotional impact, and character development to guide the screenplay drafting process effectively.

**Input:**  
- **Film Details**: {film_genre}, {film_tone} (genre and overall tone of the film)  
- **Main Characters**: {protagonist_description}, {antagonist_description} (brief traits and motivations)  
- **Core Conflict**: {central_conflict} (primary problem or tension driving the story)  
- **Setting & Themes**: {setting_description}, {key_themes} (time, place, and thematic elements)

**Instructions:**  
1. Define the three-act structure by outlining major plot points: setup, confrontation, and resolution.  
2. Develop detailed character arcs for the protagonist and antagonist, highlighting their motivations, transformations, and key emotional beats.  
3. Integrate the core conflict within each act to maintain narrative tension and drive story progression.  
4. Incorporate the setting and thematic elements organically to enrich the story world and deepen the narrative impact.  
5. Present the outline in a clear, organized format that can be easily expanded into a full script.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Structured outline with labeled acts and subsections  
- Style: Professional, concise, and engaging narrative voice suitable for screenwriters  
- Structure: Clear division into three acts, each with key plot points and character developments  

**Output Format:**  
A multi-paragraph structured outline with headings for Act 1, Act 2, and Act 3, including bullet points or short paragraphs detailing plot progression, character arcs, conflict escalation, and thematic integration.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for screenwriters, filmmakers, and creative writing coaches looking to create a solid foundation for a feature film script. It helps transform abstract ideas and character concepts into a coherent story framework that streamlines the scriptwriting process and enhances storytelling quality.
