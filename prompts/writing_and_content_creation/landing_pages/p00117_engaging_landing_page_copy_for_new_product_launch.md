### P-00117 - Engaging Landing Page Copy for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Landing Pages  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `landing_page` `copywriting` `product_launch` `marketing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create compelling and concise landing page copy that highlights the key benefits and features of a new product, persuading visitors to take action.

**Context:**  
You are a professional copywriter tasked with crafting engaging landing page content for a newly launched product aimed at converting visitors into customers by clearly communicating value and driving sign-ups or purchases.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description} (brief overview and unique selling points)  
- **Target Audience:** {audience_profile} (demographics and pain points)  
- **Call to Action:** {cta_text} (primary action for visitors to take)  
- **Brand Tone:** {brand_tone} (desired style and voice, e.g., friendly, professional)

**Instructions:**  
1. Write a compelling headline that grabs attention and reflects the product’s main benefit.  
2. Craft a concise introduction that clearly explains what the product is and how it solves the audience’s problem.  
3. Highlight 3-4 key features or benefits using persuasive language tailored to the target audience.  
4. Include a strong call to action encouraging visitors to engage with the product.  
5. Maintain the specified brand tone throughout the copy to ensure consistency.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Clear, easy-to-read paragraphs or bullet points where appropriate  
- Style: Professional but approachable, aligned with the provided brand tone  
- Structure: Headline, introduction, benefits/features, call to action

**Output Format:**  
A single coherent landing page copy block, formatted with a headline followed by body text and a clear call to action.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when launching a new product and needing persuasive landing page content that effectively communicates value and drives conversions. Ideal for marketers, copywriters, and small business owners seeking beginner-friendly, result-oriented copy to attract and engage their target customers.
