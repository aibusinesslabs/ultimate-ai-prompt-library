### P-00012 - Compelling Landing Page Copy for Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Landing Pages  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `landing-page` `copywriting` `product-launch` `marketing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write engaging and persuasive landing page copy that highlights the key benefits and features of a new product to drive user interest and conversions.

**Context:**  
You are a professional copywriter tasked with creating concise and compelling landing page content aimed at first-time visitors interested in a new product. Your goal is to clearly communicate the product’s value proposition and encourage users to take action.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_type} (name and category of the product)  
- **Target Audience:** {audience_description} (description of ideal customers)  
- **Key Features & Benefits:** {features_list} (list of main features and their benefits)  
- **Call to Action:** {cta_text} (desired user action text, e.g., “Buy Now,” “Sign Up”)

**Instructions:**  
1. Craft a headline that captures attention and introduces the product.  
2. Write a brief introductory paragraph that resonates with the target audience.  
3. List 3-5 key features paired with their benefits in clear, persuasive language.  
4. Include a strong call to action encouraging immediate user engagement.  
5. Ensure the tone is professional yet approachable, focusing on clarity and value.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Plain text, suitable for direct use on a landing page  
- Style: Clear, concise, persuasive, and user-focused  
- Structure: Headline, introduction, features with benefits, call to action

**Output Format:**  
A complete landing page copy block formatted with a headline, short intro paragraph, bullet points for features and benefits, and a closing call to action line.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when launching a new product and needing quick, effective landing page content that converts visitors into customers. It benefits marketers and small business owners who want professional copy without hiring a dedicated writer. Ideal for startups and product teams preparing their initial promotional web pages.
