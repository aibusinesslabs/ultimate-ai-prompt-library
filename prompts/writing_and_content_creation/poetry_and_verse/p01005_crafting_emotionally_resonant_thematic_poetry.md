### P-01005 - Crafting Emotionally Resonant Thematic Poetry

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative-writing` `emotion` `thematic`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose an original poem that vividly expresses a specific theme and evokes strong emotions through creative language and imagery.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled poet tasked with crafting a compelling poem that explores a given theme in a way that deeply resonates with readers. Your work should balance artistic expression with emotional clarity.

**Input:**  
- **Theme & Emotion:** {theme}, {emotion} (central subject and emotional tone to be conveyed)  
- **Perspective & Style:** {perspective}, {style} (narrative voice, e.g., first-person or third-person; poetic style, e.g., free verse, sonnet)  
- **Imagery & Symbolism:** {imagery_descriptions} (specific images or symbols to incorporate)  
- **Length & Tone:** {desired_length}, {tone} (desired poem length in words and overall mood, e.g., melancholic, hopeful)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin by interpreting the {theme} through the lens of the specified {emotion}, creating a clear emotional foundation.  
2. Write the poem from the {perspective} using the chosen {style}, ensuring consistent voice and structure.  
3. Integrate the provided {imagery_descriptions} naturally within the poem to enhance vividness and symbolic depth.  
4. Maintain the {tone} throughout, aligning language and rhythm to reinforce the mood.  
5. Ensure the poem meets the specified {desired_length} between 300 and 500 words.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Poem with appropriate line breaks and stanza separations  
- Style: Consistent with the selected poetic style and emotional tone  
- Structure: Follow the narrative perspective closely; imagery must be meaningful and relevant

**Output Format:**  
An original, thematically focused poem formatted with stanza breaks, adhering to the specified length, style, and emotional tone.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when creating heartfelt poetry tailored to express particular themes and emotions, ideal for writers, educators, or content creators aiming to produce evocative literary works that engage and move their audience.
