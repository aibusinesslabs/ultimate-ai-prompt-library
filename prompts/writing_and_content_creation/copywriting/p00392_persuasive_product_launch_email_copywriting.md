### P-00392 - Persuasive Product Launch Email Copywriting

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Copywriting  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `email marketing` `product launch` `persuasion` `conversion`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Craft a compelling and persuasive product launch email designed to engage potential customers, highlight unique selling points, and drive conversions for a new product.

**Context:**  
You are an expert copywriter specializing in email marketing campaigns for product launches. Your goal is to create an email that not only informs but excites the target audience and encourages immediate action, while maintaining brand voice and professionalism.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description} (key features and benefits)  
- **Audience Information:** {target_audience}, {audience_pain_points} (demographics and challenges)  
- **Brand Voice & Tone:** {brand_personality}, {tone_preferences} (e.g., friendly, authoritative, energetic)  
- **Call to Action:** {primary_cta}, {cta_link} (desired user action and link destination)

**Instructions:**  
1. Start with a captivating subject line and preheader that entice the recipient to open the email.  
2. Write an engaging introduction that connects with the audience’s pain points and introduces the product as the solution.  
3. Highlight the product’s unique features and benefits clearly and persuasively, using concise but impactful language.  
4. Incorporate the brand voice and tone consistently throughout the email to strengthen brand identity.  
5. End with a strong, clear call to action that encourages immediate engagement, including the {primary_cta} linked to {cta_link}.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Professional marketing email format including subject line, preheader, body, and CTA  
- Style: Persuasive, clear, and customer-focused with engaging, actionable language  
- Structure: Logical flow from attention to interest, desire, and action (AIDA model)

**Output Format:**  
Provide the complete product launch email copy including:  
- Subject Line  
- Preheader Text  
- Email Body (with introduction, product highlights, and closing)  
- Call to Action (hyperlinked text)

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing high-impact email campaigns for launching new products to maximize open rates, engagement, and conversions. Ideal for marketing professionals, copywriters, and business owners aiming to generate strong interest and sales through email outreach.
