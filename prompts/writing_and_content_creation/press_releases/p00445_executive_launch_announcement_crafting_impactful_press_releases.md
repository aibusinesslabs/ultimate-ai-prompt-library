### P-00445 - Executive Launch Announcement: Crafting Impactful Press Releases

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `corporate communication` `media relations`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a professional and compelling press release announcing the launch of a new product, designed to capture media attention, clearly communicate key benefits, and position the company as an industry leader.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned corporate communications specialist tasked with drafting a high-impact press release on behalf of a company launching its latest product. The release should balance informative and persuasive elements to engage journalists, stakeholders, and potential customers, while adhering to best practices in press release writing.

**Input:**  
- **Company & Product Details:** {{company_name}}, {{product_name}}, {{product_type}} (company and product identification, including product category)  
- **Launch Information:** {{launch_date}}, {{launch_event_location}} (date and venue of product launch)  
- **Product Features & Benefits:** {{key_features}}, {{unique_selling_points}} (detailed product attributes and competitive advantages)  
- **Quotes & Contact Info:** {{executive_quote}}, {{media_contact_name}}, {{media_contact_email}}, {{media_contact_phone}} (official executive statement and media liaison details)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that highlights the product launch and its significance.  
2. Write an engaging opening paragraph summarizing the announcement, including the who, what, when, where, and why.  
3. Elaborate on the product features and benefits, emphasizing what sets it apart in the market.  
4. Integrate the executive quote naturally to add credibility and a personal touch.  
5. Provide clear information about the launch event and invite media participation.  
6. Conclude with a boilerplate about the company and detailed media contact information.  
7. Ensure the language is clear, concise, and free of jargon, suitable for broad media distribution.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Standard press release structure with headline, dateline, body, quote, boilerplate, and contact info  
- Style: Professional, persuasive, and journalistic tone appropriate for press release distribution  
- Structure: Logical flow with clear sections; avoid fluff and redundancy  

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for immediate distribution, formatted with:  
- Headline  
- Dateline (City, State â€“ Date)  
- Body paragraphs  
- Quotation block  
- Boilerplate paragraph  
- Media contact information  

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for corporate communications teams or PR agencies preparing official announcements for new product launches. It ensures the creation of polished, media-ready press releases that effectively generate buzz and facilitate media coverage.
