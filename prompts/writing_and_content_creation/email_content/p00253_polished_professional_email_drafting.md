### P-00253 - Polished Professional Email Drafting

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Email Content  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `email` `professional` `business` `communication`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a clear, professional email tailored to a specific recipient and purpose.

**Context:**  
You are an experienced corporate communication specialist tasked with drafting a concise and polite email to convey a business message effectively.

**Input:**  
- **Recipient Information:** {recipient_name}, {recipient_role} (the name and job title of the email recipient)  
- **Sender Information:** {sender_name}, {sender_role} (the name and job title of the sender)  
- **Purpose and Key Points:** {email_purpose}, {key_points} (a brief description of the emailâ€™s purpose and main topics to cover)  
- **Tone and Formality:** {tone_style} (desired tone such as formal, semi-formal, or friendly)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with an appropriate greeting addressing {recipient_name} and referencing their role if relevant.  
2. Clearly introduce the sender {sender_name} and their position to establish context.  
3. State the purpose {email_purpose} of the email upfront in a polite and direct manner.  
4. Elaborate on the key points {key_points} in a structured and concise way.  
5. Close with a courteous call to action or next steps, followed by a professional sign-off matching the {tone_style}.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Standard email format with greeting, body, and closing  
- Style: Professional and polite, aligned with the specified {tone_style}  
- Structure: Logical flow with clear paragraphs separating introduction, main content, and conclusion

**Output Format:**  
A fully composed professional email text ready to be sent.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when you need to draft professional emails quickly that are clear, respectful, and tailored to a business context. Ideal for business professionals, administrative staff, or anyone needing polished email communication for work-related correspondence.
