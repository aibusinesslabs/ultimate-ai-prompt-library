### P-00195 - Crafting Emotionally Resonant Thematic Poetry

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative writing` `theme` `emotions`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose an original poem that deeply explores a specified theme and evokes targeted emotions through vivid imagery and carefully chosen poetic devices.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled poet aiming to create a meaningful and emotionally impactful piece. The poem should reflect the core theme while resonating with readers through its tone, style, and literary techniques.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Emotions**: {theme}, {primary_emotion}, {secondary_emotion} (central topic and two key emotions to convey)  
- **Tone and Style**: {tone_description}, {poetic_style} (desired mood and poetic form or style)  
- **Imagery and Devices**: {preferred_imagery}, {literary_devices} (visual/sensory elements and stylistic techniques to incorporate)  
- **Length and Structure**: {approximate_length}, {structural_requirements} (word count and poetic structure constraints)

**Instructions:**  
1. Interpret the {theme} and integrate the {primary_emotion} and {secondary_emotion} authentically throughout the poem.  
2. Establish the {tone_description} using language and rhythm appropriate to the {poetic_style} specified.  
3. Employ {preferred_imagery} to create vivid mental pictures and enrich the emotional experience.  
4. Incorporate the {literary_devices} (such as metaphor, alliteration, or enjambment) to enhance the poem’s artistic quality.  
5. Adhere to the {structural_requirements}, ensuring the poem’s length is within {approximate_length} words and conforms to the chosen format.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Poetic verse (stanzas and line breaks as appropriate)  
- Style: Evocative, emotionally engaging, aligned with specified tone and poetic style  
- Structure: Follow indicated structural rules (e.g., sonnet, free verse, haiku sequence) and maintain coherence throughout

**Output Format:**  
A polished poem presented in stanzas with proper line breaks, clearly reflecting the theme, emotions, and stylistic instructions. Include a brief title and optional subtitle.

#### Use Case:  
Ideal for poets, writers, educators, and creative professionals seeking to generate original poetry that effectively captures specific themes and emotional nuances. Useful for creative writing exercises, content creation, or thematic literary projects.
