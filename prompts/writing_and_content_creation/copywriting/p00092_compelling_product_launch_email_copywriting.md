### P-00092 - Compelling Product Launch Email Copywriting

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Copywriting  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `email marketing` `product launch` `copywriting` `conversion`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a persuasive and engaging product launch email copy that effectively introduces a new product to potential customers and drives conversions.

**Context:**  
You are a professional copywriter specializing in email marketing campaigns for product launches. Your goal is to craft a compelling message that highlights the product’s key benefits, creates excitement, and encourages recipients to take action.

**Input:**  
- **Product Information:** {product_name}, {product_features} (name and main features of the new product)  
- **Target Audience:** {audience_description} (demographics, interests, pain points)  
- **Offer Details:** {launch_offer} (special discounts, limited-time deals, or bonuses)  
- **Call to Action:** {cta} (desired action, e.g., “Buy Now,” “Learn More”)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a captivating subject line that piques curiosity and highlights the product’s core value.  
2. Introduce the product clearly and succinctly, emphasizing its unique features and benefits tailored to the target audience.  
3. Address the audience’s pain points or desires that the product solves or fulfills.  
4. Present the launch offer compellingly, creating urgency or exclusivity.  
5. End with a strong, clear call to action that motivates immediate response.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Email copy including subject line, greeting, body, and closing  
- Style: Professional yet approachable, persuasive, and customer-focused  
- Structure: Logical flow from introduction to offer to call to action; avoid jargon and maintain clarity

**Output Format:**  
A complete product launch email copy with:  
- Subject line  
- Greeting  
- Body paragraphs  
- Closing statement and signature line

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing email marketing campaigns for new product launches to ensure the message is clear, engaging, and conversion-oriented. Ideal for marketing teams, copywriters, and small business owners seeking to maximize launch impact through email outreach.
