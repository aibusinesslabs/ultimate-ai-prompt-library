### P-00827 - Persuasive Product Launch Email Copy

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Copywriting  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `email` `product_launch` `persuasive` `marketing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a persuasive product launch email designed to engage potential customers, highlight key benefits, and motivate recipients to take immediate action.

**Context:**  
You are an experienced copywriter specializing in email marketing campaigns for consumer products. Your goal is to craft a compelling message that captures attention, builds excitement, and drives sales for a newly launched product.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_features} (name and main features of the product)  
- **Target Audience:** {audience_description} (demographics, interests, pain points)  
- **Call to Action:** {cta_text} (desired action, e.g., "Buy Now," "Learn More")  
- **Offer Details:** {discount_info} (any discounts, limited-time offers, or bonuses)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a captivating subject line that entices the reader to open the email.  
2. Write an attention-grabbing opening paragraph that introduces {product_name} and resonates with {audience_description}.  
3. Clearly explain the key benefits and unique selling points using {product_features}, focusing on how they solve the audience’s pain points.  
4. Incorporate {discount_info} to create urgency and encourage immediate response.  
5. End with a strong call to action using {cta_text} and include a subtle reminder of the offer’s limited availability.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Professional email copy format (including subject line, greeting, body, closing)  
- Style: Persuasive, clear, and engaging tone that matches the target audience  
- Structure: Logical flow with distinct introduction, body, and conclusion sections

**Output Format:**  
A complete product launch email draft including:  
- Subject line  
- Salutation  
- Body paragraphs emphasizing benefits and offer  
- Clear call to action  
- Polite closing and signature placeholder

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing email campaigns for new product launches to ensure messaging is compelling and conversion-focused. Marketing teams, copywriters, and small business owners benefit from a structured approach to crafting persuasive emails tailored to specific audiences and offers.
