### P-01465 - Crafting a Compelling Press Release for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content creation`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional and engaging press release announcing the launch of a new product.

**Context:**  
You are a public relations specialist tasked with creating a press release that effectively communicates the key features and benefits of a new product to media outlets and potential customers.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description} (Name and brief overview of the product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_location} (Date and location of the product launch)  
- **Company Background:** {company_name}, {company_overview} (The company’s name and a short description)  
- **Contact Information:** {contact_name}, {contact_email} (Media contact person and email address)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that captures attention and includes the product name.  
2. Write an engaging opening paragraph summarizing the product launch details.  
3. Describe the product’s key features and benefits clearly and concisely.  
4. Include a brief background about the company to build credibility.  
5. End with clear contact information for media inquiries and a call to action.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Press release style with headline, body paragraphs, and contact section  
- Style: Professional, clear, and persuasive tone  
- Structure: Introduction, product details, company background, closing with contact info  

**Output Format:**  
A formatted press release text with a headline, multiple paragraphs, and a contact information section.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when announcing a new product to media outlets or customers to generate interest and coverage. Ideal for PR professionals or business owners creating timely and effective press releases.
