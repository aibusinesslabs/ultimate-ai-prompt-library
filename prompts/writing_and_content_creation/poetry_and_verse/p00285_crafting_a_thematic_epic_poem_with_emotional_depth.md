### P-00285 - Crafting a Thematic Epic Poem with Emotional Depth

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `epic poetry` `thematic writing` `emotional depth` `structured verse`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose an original epic poem that explores a complex theme, weaving emotional depth and vivid imagery throughout the narrative while adhering to a consistent poetic structure.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned poet specializing in epic and narrative poetry. Your expertise lies in blending thematic complexity with emotional resonance, crafting verses that not only tell a story but also evoke profound feelings. The poem should captivate readers by combining literary techniques such as metaphor, symbolism, and varied meter.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Emotional Tone:** {theme}, {emotional_tone} (e.g., resilience and hope, melancholy and loss)  
- **Narrative Elements:** {main_character}, {setting} (protagonist’s name and the poem’s time/place context)  
- **Poetic Structure:** {stanza_form}, {rhyme_scheme} (e.g., quatrains, sestets; ABAB, AABB)  
- **Imagery and Symbolism Preferences:** {imagery_focus}, {symbolism_elements} (nature, war, light/darkness, etc.)

**Instructions:**  
1. Develop an overarching narrative arc centered on {main_character} within the {setting}, integrating the {theme} throughout the poem.  
2. Employ the specified {stanza_form} and maintain the chosen {rhyme_scheme} consistently across all stanzas.  
3. Infuse the poem with vivid and evocative imagery aligned with {imagery_focus}, using {symbolism_elements} to deepen thematic meaning.  
4. Reflect the {emotional_tone} in the language and rhythm, ensuring emotional progression that resonates with readers.  
5. Revise the poem to ensure clarity, coherence, and a compelling emotional impact while respecting the constraints.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Written as continuous stanzas without prose interruptions  
- Style: Elevated, poetic language with a balance of narrative and lyrical qualities  
- Structure: Maintain uniform stanza length and rhyme scheme throughout; include a clear beginning, middle, and end  

**Output Format:**  
Return the complete epic poem formatted in stanzas, with line breaks preserved, and a title reflecting the central theme.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for writers, poets, educators, or creative professionals seeking to create a sophisticated epic poem for publication, performance, or teaching. It supports advanced poetic craftsmanship by emphasizing thematic cohesion and emotional nuance within a formal poetic structure.
