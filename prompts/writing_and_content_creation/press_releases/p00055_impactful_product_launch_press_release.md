### P-00055 - Impactful Product Launch Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `branding`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a compelling and professional press release announcing a new product launch, designed to generate media interest and inform target audiences.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned PR specialist crafting a press release for a company unveiling its latest product. The goal is to clearly communicate the product’s unique features, benefits, and market relevance while aligning with the company’s brand voice.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {{product_name}}, {{product_description}} (Name and key features of the product)  
- **Company Information:** {{company_name}}, {{company_background}} (Company name and brief background)  
- **Launch Specifics:** {{launch_date}}, {{launch_event_details}} (Date of launch and any event information)  
- **Target Audience & Market Impact:** {{target_audience}}, {{market_impact}} (Intended customers and expected influence on the market)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong, attention-grabbing headline incorporating the product name.  
2. Open with a concise introductory paragraph summarizing the product launch news.  
3. Detail the product features and benefits, highlighting what sets it apart from competitors.  
4. Include relevant company background that supports credibility and trust.  
5. Provide specifics about the launch date and any related events or availability information.  
6. Discuss the target audience and the anticipated impact on the market or industry.  
7. Close with a call-to-action or information on where to find more details.  
8. Maintain a clear, professional, and engaging tone throughout.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Press release format with headline, dateline, body, and boilerplate  
- Style: Formal, persuasive, and informative  
- Structure: Logical flow with clear separation of sections and no jargon  

**Output Format:**  
A complete press release text ready for distribution, including headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and company boilerplate.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing announcements for new product launches to ensure your press release is engaging, informative, and aligned with professional standards. Ideal for PR professionals, marketing teams, and business owners seeking to maximize media coverage and customer interest.
