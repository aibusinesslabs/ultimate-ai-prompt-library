### P-00385 - Impactful Corporate Announcement Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `press release` `corporate communication` `public relations` `branding`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Craft a professional and compelling press release announcing a significant corporate event that effectively communicates key details, generates media interest, and reinforces the companyâ€™s brand values.

**Context:**  
You are a senior communications specialist working for a mid-to-large-sized corporation preparing to announce a milestone event such as a merger, product launch, leadership change, or strategic partnership. Your goal is to deliver clear, engaging, and credible content that resonates with journalists, stakeholders, and the general public.

**Input:**  
- **Event Details:** {event_type}, {event_date}, {event_location} (type of event, scheduled date, and venue or context)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background}, {core_values} (name, brief history and mission/vision)  
- **Key Stakeholders & Quotes:** {executive_names}, {executive_titles}, {quotes} (names and titles of key figures with at least one relevant quote)  
- **Call to Action & Contact:** {next_steps}, {media_contact_name}, {media_contact_email}, {media_contact_phone} (desired audience response and media contact info)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that succinctly conveys the announcement and grabs attention.  
2. Write a concise subheadline that supports the headline with additional context or benefit highlights.  
3. Open with a lead paragraph summarizing the who, what, when, where, and why of the event.  
4. Expand on the details in the body: emphasize the significance, background information, and impact on the industry or community.  
5. Integrate relevant quotes from key stakeholders to add authenticity and perspective.  
6. Conclude with a clear call to action outlining next steps or how audiences can engage further.  
7. Include media contact information clearly at the end for follow-up inquiries.  
8. Maintain a professional, formal tone consistent with corporate communications best practices.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Standard press release structure including headline, dateline, body paragraphs, quotes, and boilerplate  
- Style: Formal, concise, authoritative, and engaging; avoid jargon and overly technical language  
- Structure: Logical flow with clear sections; use short paragraphs for readability  

**Output Format:**  
Provide the complete press release text formatted as a professional document, including:  
- Headline  
- Subheadline  
- Dateline (City, State, Date)  
- Body paragraphs with embedded quotes  
- Boilerplate paragraph about the company  
- Media contact details  

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing to announce important corporate developments requiring media distribution and stakeholder communication. Ideal for PR professionals, corporate communications teams, and marketing specialists seeking to produce polished, informative press releases that elevate brand reputation and maximize media coverage.
