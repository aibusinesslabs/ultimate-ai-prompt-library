### P-00302 - Persuasive Product Launch Email Copy

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Copywriting  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `email` `product_launch` `copywriting` `marketing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a compelling and persuasive email to announce a new product launch, designed to engage the target audience and drive conversions.

**Context:**  
You are a professional copywriter specializing in marketing communications. Your goal is to craft an email that highlights the product’s key benefits and unique selling points while motivating recipients to take immediate action.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_features} (name and standout features of the product)  
- **Target Audience:** {audience_description} (demographic and psychographic traits of the recipients)  
- **Call to Action:** {primary_cta} (desired action for recipients to take, e.g., “Shop Now,” “Learn More”)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {special_offer} (date of launch and any promotional incentives)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with an engaging subject line and opening sentence that captures attention.  
2. Introduce the product by name and succinctly describe its key features and benefits relevant to the target audience.  
3. Emphasize what makes the product unique and why it solves a problem or fulfills a need.  
4. Include the launch date and any special offers to create urgency.  
5. End with a clear, compelling call to action that encourages immediate response.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Professional marketing email format with clear paragraphs  
- Style: Persuasive, engaging, and customer-focused tone  
- Structure: Introduction, body (product benefits and unique points), offer details, and strong closing CTA

**Output Format:**  
A fully formatted product launch email ready for deployment, including subject line and body copy.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing email campaigns for new product introductions. Ideal for marketers, copywriters, and small business owners aiming to effectively communicate product value and boost launch engagement and sales.
