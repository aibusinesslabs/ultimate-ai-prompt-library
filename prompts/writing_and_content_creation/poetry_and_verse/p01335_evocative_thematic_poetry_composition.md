### P-01335 - Evocative Thematic Poetry Composition

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative_writing` `theme` `verse_structure`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose an original, evocative poem that explores a central theme through vivid imagery and emotional depth, adhering to a specified verse style.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled poet commissioned to create a meaningful poem that captures the essence of a given theme. The poem should engage readers by employing rich language and maintaining coherence within a defined poetic form.

**Input:**  
- **Theme & Emotion**: {theme}, {primary_emotion} (central topic and dominant emotional tone)  
- **Verse Style**: {poetic_form} (e.g., sonnet, free verse, haiku, villanelle)  
- **Imagery & Symbolism**: {key_symbols} (specific images or symbols to incorporate)  
- **Tone & Perspective**: {tone}, {narrative_perspective} (e.g., reflective, optimistic; first-person, third-person)

**Instructions:**  
1. Analyze the given theme and primary emotion to establish the poem’s core message.  
2. Select vocabulary and phrasing that evoke the {primary_emotion} effectively throughout the poem.  
3. Construct the poem strictly following the constraints and conventions of {poetic_form}.  
4. Integrate the {key_symbols} organically to enrich the thematic depth and visual texture.  
5. Ensure the tone and narrative perspective align consistently with {tone} and {narrative_perspective}.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300–500 words  
- Format: Written in stanzas appropriate to the {poetic_form}, with correct meter and rhyme scheme if applicable  
- Style: Use vivid, descriptive language with literary devices such as metaphor, simile, and symbolism  
- Structure: Maintain coherence and progression of ideas aligned with the chosen poetic form

**Output Format:**  
A polished poem in text format, labeled with the poem’s title, theme, and poetic form, divided into stanzas as per the style requirements.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for poets, creative writers, and educators seeking to generate thematic poetry that balances artistic expression with formal poetic conventions. It supports creative exploration while honing structural rigor, perfect for workshops, classroom assignments, or personal projects.
