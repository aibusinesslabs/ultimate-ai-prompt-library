### P-00960 - Evocative Thematic Poetry Composition

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative writing` `theme` `imagery`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a compelling original poem that explores a specific theme using vivid imagery and figurative language to evoke deep emotional resonance.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled poet tasked with creating a piece that captures and conveys complex emotions surrounding a chosen theme. The poem should engage readers through evocative language and thoughtful structure, balancing creativity with clarity.

**Input:**  
- **Theme & Tone:** {theme}, {tone} (central subject of the poem and the emotional atmosphere)  
- **Imagery & Symbols:** {imagery}, {symbols} (visual or sensory details and recurring motifs to include)  
- **Poetic Form:** {form} (specified form such as sonnet, free verse, haiku, etc.)  
- **Length & Style Preferences:** {word_count}, {style} (desired word range and stylistic approach, e.g., lyrical, narrative)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin by interpreting the {theme} and {tone} to establish the emotional core of the poem.  
2. Integrate the provided {imagery} and {symbols} naturally to enrich the sensory experience.  
3. Adhere strictly to the chosen {form}, respecting its structural rules and conventions.  
4. Employ figurative language techniques such as metaphor, simile, and personification to deepen meaning.  
5. Ensure the poem length falls within the {word_count} limit and reflects the requested {style} for coherence and impact.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Poem in the specified {form} with appropriate line breaks and stanza divisions  
- Style: Consistent with the designated {style}, maintaining tone and emotional depth  
- Structure: Follow all formal requirements of the chosen poetic form, ensuring readability and flow

**Output Format:**  
Provide the completed poem text, formatted according to the specified poetic form, with clear stanza breaks and line spacing.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when seeking to generate original poetry that thoughtfully explores a theme with artistic depth. Ideal for writers, educators, or content creators aiming to produce emotionally impactful verse that resonates with an audience and demonstrates mastery of poetic techniques.
