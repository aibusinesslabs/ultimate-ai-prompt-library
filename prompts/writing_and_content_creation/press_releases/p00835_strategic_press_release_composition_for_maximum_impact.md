### P-00835 - Strategic Press Release Composition for Maximum Impact

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `press release` `public relations` `media outreach` `corporate communication`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Craft a compelling, professional press release that effectively announces a significant company event or development, designed to capture media interest and engage target audiences.

**Context:**  
You are an experienced corporate communications specialist tasked with writing an advanced-level press release for a company’s major announcement. Your objective is to deliver a clear, persuasive message that highlights key information, supports brand positioning, and encourages media coverage and public engagement.

**Input:**  
- **Company Details:** {company_name}, {industry}, {company_background} (brief overview and positioning)  
- **Announcement Details:** {event_type}, {event_date}, {key_message} (description of the announcement and its significance)  
- **Quotes and Testimonials:** {executive_quote}, {customer_or_partner_quote} (relevant statements to add credibility and human interest)  
- **Call to Action & Contact Information:** {next_steps}, {media_contact_name}, {media_contact_email}, {media_contact_phone} (instructions for follow-up and media inquiries)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong, attention-grabbing headline that encapsulates the announcement’s essence.  
2. Write a concise, engaging lead paragraph summarizing the who, what, when, where, and why of the announcement.  
3. Develop the body with detailed information, integrating background context and emphasizing the announcement’s relevance to the industry or market.  
4. Incorporate the provided quotes naturally to add authority and emotional appeal.  
5. Conclude with a clear, actionable call to action and provide media contact details for follow-up inquiries.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Standard press release format including headline, dateline, body, quotes, boilerplate, and contact info  
- Style: Professional, clear, persuasive, and journalistic tone suitable for media distribution  
- Structure: Logical flow with distinct sections, ensuring readability and information hierarchy  

**Output Format:**  
A complete press release text formatted with:  
- Headline (bold)  
- Dateline (city, date)  
- Body paragraphs with integrated quotes  
- Boilerplate about the company  
- Media contact information section  

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when announcing major company milestones such as product launches, partnerships, funding rounds, or events. It benefits PR professionals, corporate communicators, and marketing teams aiming to generate high-impact media coverage and effectively inform stakeholders.
