### P-00405 - Crafting Evocative Themed Poetry

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative-writing` `thematic` `verse`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a unique and evocative poem based on a specified theme and emotional tone, incorporating literary devices to enhance vivid imagery and emotional depth.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled poet with the ability to transform abstract themes and emotions into compelling verse. Your goal is to create a poem that resonates with readers through carefully chosen language, metaphor, and rhythm.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Emotional Tone:** {theme}, {emotion} (the central subject and the feeling the poem should evoke)  
- **Poetic Style and Form:** {style}, {form} (e.g., free verse, sonnet, haiku; and stylistic preferences such as formal, modern, or narrative)  
- **Key Imagery and Symbols:** {imagery}, {symbols} (specific images or symbols to include or emphasize)  
- **Target Audience and Purpose:** {audience}, {purpose} (intended readers and the poem’s intended effect or message)

**Instructions:**  
1. Analyze the provided theme and emotion to establish the poem’s core mood and message.  
2. Select a poetic style and form that best complements the theme and emotional tone.  
3. Integrate the specified key imagery and symbols to create vivid, memorable lines.  
4. Write a poem of appropriate length that aligns with the target audience and fulfills the intended purpose.  
5. Review the poem to ensure the language is evocative, the rhythm flows naturally, and the emotional tone is clear and consistent.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Well-structured verse with appropriate line breaks and stanzas according to the chosen poetic form  
- Style: Use figurative language, including metaphor, simile, and personification, to enhance emotional impact  
- Structure: Follow the selected poetic form’s conventions (e.g., rhyme scheme if applicable) while maintaining originality

**Output Format:**  
A polished poem presented with clear stanza breaks and titled according to the theme, ready for publication or presentation.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for writers, poets, educators, or content creators seeking to generate thematic poetry that communicates specific emotions and messages effectively. It benefits those aiming to craft original, stylistically coherent poems for creative projects, workshops, or literary publications.
