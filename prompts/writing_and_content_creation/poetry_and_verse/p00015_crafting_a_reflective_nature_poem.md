### P-00015 - Crafting a Reflective Nature Poem

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `poetry` `nature` `creative-writing` `reflection`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose an original poem that reflects on a specific natural setting, capturing its mood and imagery through vivid language and sensory details.

**Context:**  
You are a beginner poet exploring themes of nature and reflection. Your goal is to create a poem that evokes emotions and paints a clear picture of the environment you choose, using simple but evocative language.

**Input:**  
- **Setting Details:** {location}, {time_of_day} (the natural place and time to inspire the poem)  
- **Emotional Tone:** {mood} (the feeling or atmosphere to convey, e.g., peaceful, melancholic)  
- **Sensory Elements:** {sights}, {sounds} (specific visual and auditory details to include)  
- **Poem Style:** {form} (poetic form preference, e.g., free verse, haiku, rhymed couplets)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin by visualizing the {location} during the {time_of_day} and connect with the mood {mood}.  
2. Incorporate the {sights} and {sounds} to create immersive sensory imagery.  
3. Choose the {form} to structure your poem according to your preference.  
4. Use simple but expressive language to convey your reflection on nature.  
5. Review and refine your poem to ensure clarity and emotional impact.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Prose text formatted as a poem with line breaks  
- Style: Beginner-friendly, descriptive, reflective  
- Structure: Follow the chosen {form} consistently throughout the poem  

**Output Format:**  
An original poem of 150-300 words, formatted with appropriate line breaks, capturing the specified natural setting, mood, and sensory details in the chosen poetic form.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for novice poets or creative writing students seeking to develop their descriptive and reflective writing skills through nature-themed poetry. It supports learning poetic structure while encouraging emotional and sensory expression.
