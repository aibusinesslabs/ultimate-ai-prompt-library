### P-00700 - Impactful Press Release Crafting for Product Launches

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a professional and engaging press release announcing a new product launch that captures media attention and effectively communicates the product’s key features and benefits.

**Context:**  
You are a corporate communications specialist tasked with creating a press release for a company's latest product. The goal is to inform journalists, stakeholders, and customers about the launch, emphasizing the product’s unique value proposition and market relevance.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_type} (name and category of the product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_location} (date and place of the product launch event)  
- **Company Background:** {company_name}, {company_description} (name and brief overview of the company)  
- **Key Features & Benefits:** {features_list}, {target_audience} (primary features and intended user base)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a compelling headline that highlights the product launch and its significance.  
2. Write a strong lead paragraph summarizing the who, what, when, where, and why of the launch.  
3. Detail the product’s key features and benefits, tailored to the identified target audience.  
4. Include a brief background of the company to establish credibility and context.  
5. Close with a call-to-action or information on how to learn more, attend the launch, or contact the company.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300–500 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and contact information  
- Style: Professional, clear, and persuasive tone suitable for media distribution  
- Structure: Logical flow with an attention-grabbing opening, informative middle, and a concise closing statement

**Output Format:**  
A full press release text ready for distribution, formatted with headline, dateline, body content, and a closing section including contact details.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for PR professionals, marketers, or content creators who need to generate polished press releases for new product launches. It ensures clear communication of essential details while maximizing media and public interest.
