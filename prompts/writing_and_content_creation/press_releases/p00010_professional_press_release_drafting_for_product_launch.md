### P-00010 - Professional Press Release Drafting for Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `professional writing` `media communication`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a clear and engaging press release announcing a new product launch, designed to attract media attention and inform the target audience.

**Context:**  
You are a professional communications specialist tasked with drafting an official press release for a company’s latest product launch. The goal is to highlight key features, benefits, and relevant details while maintaining a polished and concise tone.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_type} (name and category of the new product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_location} (date and place of the product launch event)  
- **Company Background:** {company_name}, {company_description} (company’s name and brief overview)  
- **Key Features:** {feature_list} (a list of 3-5 main product features or benefits)

**Instructions:**  
1. Introduce the product and announce its launch with {product_name} and {launch_date}.  
2. Provide a brief background on the company using {company_name} and {company_description}.  
3. Highlight the key features and benefits by elaborating on {feature_list}.  
4. Include details about the launch event location, {launch_location}, and any relevant participation info.  
5. Close with a call-to-action or contact information for media inquiries.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Press release format with a headline, subheadline, and body paragraphs  
- Style: Professional, clear, and engaging; avoid overly technical jargon  
- Structure: Start with a strong headline, followed by an informative lead paragraph, supporting details, and a concise closing statement

**Output Format:**  
A fully formatted press release text ready for distribution, including headline, subheadline, and body content.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing formal press releases to announce new product launches. It benefits marketing teams, PR professionals, and startup founders seeking a well-structured and impactful media announcement suitable for both digital and print distribution.
