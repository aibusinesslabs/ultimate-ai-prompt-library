### P-01405 - Crafting Impactful Corporate Press Releases

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `press release` `corporate communication` `public relations` `content strategy`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a professional, compelling press release announcing a significant corporate event or milestone that effectively communicates key information and engages target audiences.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned corporate communications specialist tasked with drafting a press release for a major announcement. Your goal is to ensure clarity, professionalism, and alignment with brand messaging while maximizing media interest and audience engagement.

**Input:**  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {industry}, {headquarters_location} (basic corporate details)  
- **Announcement Details:** {event_type}, {event_date}, {key_highlights} (summary of the announcement)  
- **Quotes and Statements:** {executive_name}, {executive_title}, {quote_content} (leadership commentary)  
- **Additional Information:** {background_info}, {call_to_action}, {media_contact} (supporting context and contact details)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that succinctly conveys the essence of the announcement.  
2. Write a compelling lead paragraph that answers who, what, when, where, why, and how.  
3. Elaborate on the key highlights and significance of the event with clear, engaging language.  
4. Integrate the executive quote naturally to add authority and a human perspective.  
5. Conclude with background information about the company, a clear call to action, and media contact details for follow-up.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Standard press release format including headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and boilerplate  
- Style: Formal, informative, and engaging with journalistic clarity  
- Structure: Logical flow with distinct sections (headline, lead, body, quote, boilerplate, contact)  

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution, formatted with clear headings and paragraphs, suitable for immediate publication or media use.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when a business needs to announce major developments such as product launches, mergers, awards, or executive appointments. It benefits PR professionals, marketing teams, and communications agencies aiming to create authoritative and media-friendly press releases that elevate brand reputation and inform stakeholders effectively.
