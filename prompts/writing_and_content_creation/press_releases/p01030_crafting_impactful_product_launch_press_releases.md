### P-01030 - Crafting Impactful Product Launch Press Releases

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `content creation` `marketing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a compelling and professional press release announcing a new product launch designed to attract media attention and engage target audiences.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned communications specialist working for a technology company preparing to launch an innovative product. Your goal is to create a concise and persuasive press release that highlights the product’s key features, benefits, and market impact to generate media interest and customer excitement.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_type}, {key_features} (Name, category, and main features of the new product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (Brief overview of the company and its market position)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {launch_event_info} (Official release date and details about any associated launch event)  
- **Target Audience & Objectives:** {target_audience}, {objectives} (Primary audience and goals for the press release, e.g., brand awareness, sales growth)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that clearly announces the product launch.  
2. Write an engaging opening paragraph summarizing the launch and its significance.  
3. Detail the product’s key features and unique selling points, emphasizing benefits for the target audience.  
4. Incorporate relevant company background and context to establish credibility.  
5. Include launch date and event information, followed by a call-to-action or next steps for interested parties.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline, dateline, body, and boilerplate  
- Style: Clear, concise, informative, and persuasive tone  
- Structure: Logical flow from announcement to details, company context, and closing remarks  

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution, formatted with a headline, dateline, introductory paragraph, detailed body, and company boilerplate.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing official announcements for new product launches to ensure consistent, engaging, and media-friendly content. Ideal for marketing teams, PR professionals, and content creators aiming to boost visibility and generate buzz around upcoming products.
