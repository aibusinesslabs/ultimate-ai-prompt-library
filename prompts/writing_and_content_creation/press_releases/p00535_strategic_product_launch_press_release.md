### P-00535 - Strategic Product Launch Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `branding`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Craft a polished, compelling press release announcing a new product launch that effectively communicates key features, market impact, and company vision to media outlets and stakeholders.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned corporate communications specialist tasked with creating a high-impact press release for a cutting-edge product launch. The release should capture the product’s unique value proposition, align with the company’s brand voice, and motivate journalists and industry influencers to cover the story.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_features}, {product_benefits} (core attributes and advantages of the new product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (brand history, mission, and positioning)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {target_audience}, {availability} (release timing, intended customers, and distribution channels)  
- **Supporting Details:** {quotes_from_executives}, {market_context}, {call_to_action} (endorsements, industry relevance, and next steps for readers)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline and subheadline that highlight the most newsworthy aspect of the launch.  
2. Write a concise opening paragraph summarizing the product launch and its significance.  
3. Elaborate on key product features and benefits in the body, integrating relevant company background to build credibility.  
4. Incorporate executive quotes to add authority and a personal touch.  
5. Conclude with market context and a clear call to action, such as directing readers to a website or upcoming event.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and contact information  
- Style: Formal, authoritative, and engaging, suitable for journalists and industry professionals  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to closing, using clear, concise language and active voice

**Output Format:**  
A fully formatted press release text ready for distribution, including headline, subheadline, dateline, body content, and closing contact details.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing for a major product launch that requires formal media communication. It benefits PR professionals, marketing teams, and startup founders aiming to maximize media coverage and stakeholder engagement with a well-crafted announcement.
