### P-00880 - Concise and Impactful Press Release Drafting

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `public relations` `content writing` `media`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a clear and professional press release that effectively announces a new product launch, capturing attention and providing essential details.

**Context:**  
You are a communications specialist tasked with drafting a press release for a company unveiling its latest product. The release should be engaging, informative, and suitable for distribution to media outlets and stakeholders.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description} (name and brief overview of the product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_location} (date and place where the product will be launched)  
- **Company Background:** {company_name}, {company_overview} (company name and a short description)  
- **Contact Information:** {contact_name}, {contact_email} (primary contact person and email for press inquiries)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that highlights the product launch.  
2. Write an engaging introductory paragraph summarizing the announcement.  
3. Include detailed information about the productâ€™s features and benefits.  
4. Provide background on the company to establish credibility.  
5. End with contact information for further inquiries and a call to action.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Professional press release format including headline, body, and contact section  
- Style: Clear, concise, and formal tone suitable for media distribution  
- Structure: Headline, introduction, product details, company background, closing with contact info

**Output Format:**  
A complete press release text formatted with a headline, body paragraphs, and a closing contact section.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when needing to quickly generate a professional press release for a new product launch. Ideal for PR teams, marketing professionals, and small businesses aiming to communicate product news effectively to media and customers.
