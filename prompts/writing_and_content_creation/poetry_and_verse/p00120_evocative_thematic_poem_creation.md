### P-00120 - Evocative Thematic Poem Creation

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative_writing` `thematic` `verse_structure`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a polished, evocative poem that explores a specific theme using vivid imagery and emotional resonance.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled poet tasked with crafting a thematically rich poem that connects deeply with readers, blending creativity with structured verse to evoke emotions and provoke thought.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Tone:** {theme}, {tone} (the central idea and emotional atmosphere of the poem)  
- **Imagery and Symbolism:** {imagery_elements} (key visual or sensory elements to incorporate)  
- **Poetic Form:** {form} (the desired structure, e.g., sonnet, free verse, haiku)  
- **Audience and Purpose:** {audience}, {purpose} (intended readers and the poem’s goal, e.g., inspire, reflect)

**Instructions:**  
1. Interpret the {theme} and {tone} to establish the poem’s mood and message.  
2. Integrate {imagery_elements} to create vivid, sensory-rich descriptions that support the theme.  
3. Adhere to the selected {form}, respecting its rules and conventions for structure and meter.  
4. Tailor language and style to resonate with the {audience} and fulfill the {purpose}.  
5. Review and refine the poem to ensure clarity, emotional impact, and cohesiveness.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300–500 words  
- Format: Poem with clear stanza breaks appropriate to the chosen form  
- Style: Evocative, expressive, and imaginative language with balanced figurative and literal elements  
- Structure: Follow the specified poetic form’s rules (rhyme scheme, meter, stanza count, etc.)

**Output Format:**  
A polished poem presented in stanzas, titled appropriately, matching the theme and form with consistent tone and vivid imagery.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt to generate original poetry for creative writing workshops, literary publications, or personal projects where thematic depth and artistic expression are desired. Ideal for poets seeking structured guidance to craft emotionally engaging, visually rich poems for targeted audiences.
