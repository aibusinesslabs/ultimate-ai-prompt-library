### P-00895 - Impactful Product Launch Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `PR writing` `content creation`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional press release announcing the launch of a new product, highlighting its key features, benefits, and market impact to generate media interest and customer engagement.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled PR specialist tasked with crafting a compelling and informative press release for a company introducing a new product to the market. The goal is to convey the product’s unique value proposition, appeal to target audiences, and support the company’s branding efforts.

**Input:**  
- **Company and Product Details:** {company_name}, {product_name}, {product_type} (basic identification and product category)  
- **Product Features and Benefits:** {key_features}, {customer_benefits} (main attributes and how they solve customer needs)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {availability} (product release timing and distribution channels)  
- **Quote from Leadership:** {executive_name}, {executive_title}, {executive_quote} (endorsement or statement from a company leader)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that clearly announces the product launch.  
2. Write an engaging opening paragraph summarizing the product and its significance.  
3. Detail the product’s key features and the benefits customers will gain.  
4. Include launch specifics such as release date and availability.  
5. Incorporate the leadership quote to add authority and personal perspective.  
6. Close with a brief company background and a call to action for readers or media.  
7. Ensure the tone is professional, clear, and persuasive throughout.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, dateline, body, and contact information placeholder  
- Style: Formal and journalistic, suitable for media distribution  
- Structure: Logical flow with distinct sections for introduction, details, quote, and conclusion

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution, including a headline, dateline, body paragraphs, quote section, and a closing paragraph with company info and contact prompt.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing official announcements for new product launches to ensure consistent, engaging communication that attracts media coverage and customer attention. Ideal for PR teams, marketing professionals, and content creators looking to streamline press release generation.
