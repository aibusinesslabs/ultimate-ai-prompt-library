### P-01065 - Evoking Emotion Through Thematic Epic Poetry

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `epic poetry` `thematic writing` `emotional depth` `structured verse`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose an original epic poem that deeply explores a central theme through vivid storytelling, rich imagery, and emotional resonance, demonstrating mastery of advanced poetic techniques.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned poet tasked with crafting a compelling epic poem that captures complex human experiences and emotions. The poem should balance narrative progression with lyrical beauty, appealing to readers who appreciate high-level poetic artistry and thematic depth.

**Input:**  
- **Theme & Emotional Tone:** {theme}, {emotion} (The central idea around which the poem is constructed, and the primary emotional atmosphere—e.g., loss, triumph, hope, despair)  
- **Narrative Elements:** {setting}, {main_character}, {conflict} (Key story components including where it takes place, protagonist details, and primary conflict driving the narrative)  
- **Poetic Style & Devices:** {meter}, {rhyme_scheme}, {literary_devices} (Preferred metrical pattern, rhyme scheme choices, and literary devices to incorporate such as metaphor, alliteration, symbolism)  
- **Length & Structure:** {word_count}, {stanza_structure} (Desired length in words between 500-750, and stanza format such as quatrains or tercets)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin by outlining the poem’s narrative arc focusing on the input theme and conflict.  
2. Develop the main character’s journey using vivid imagery and emotional language that aligns with the specified emotional tone.  
3. Employ the selected meter and rhyme scheme consistently throughout to create musicality and formal cohesion.  
4. Integrate the designated literary devices purposefully to enhance thematic depth and emotional impact.  
5. Structure the poem according to the specified stanza format, maintaining clarity and flow within the 500-750 word range.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Fully composed epic poem with consistent stanza breaks  
- Style: Elevated and evocative language suitable for advanced poetry readers; maintain emotional authenticity  
- Structure: Adhere strictly to the provided meter, rhyme scheme, and stanza structure; narrative must be coherent and immersive

**Output Format:**  
A single, polished epic poem that meets all thematic, stylistic, and structural specifications, delivered as plain text with stanza breaks clearly indicated.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for poets, literary students, or creative writers aiming to produce a sophisticated epic poem centered on a specific theme and emotional tone. It is especially useful for advanced writing workshops, portfolio building, or exploring thematic depth within structured poetic forms.
