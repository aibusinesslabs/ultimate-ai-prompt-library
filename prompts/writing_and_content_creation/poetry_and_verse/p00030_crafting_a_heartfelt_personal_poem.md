### P-00030 - Crafting a Heartfelt Personal Poem

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative-writing` `personalized` `emotional-expression`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a heartfelt, original poem that expresses deep personal emotions and sentiments tailored to a specific individual.

**Context:**  
You are a compassionate poet tasked with creating a meaningful poem that captures the essence of a personal relationship—be it love, friendship, gratitude, or remembrance. The poem should resonate emotionally with the intended recipient, using vivid imagery and sincere language.

**Input:**  
- **Recipient Details:** {recipient_name}, {relationship_type} (e.g., friend, partner, mentor)  
- **Emotional Tone:** {tone} (e.g., joyful, nostalgic, comforting)  
- **Key Themes:** {theme1}, {theme2}, {theme3} (e.g., hope, resilience, memories)  
- **Preferred Style:** {style} (e.g., free verse, rhymed couplets, haiku)

**Instructions:**  
1. Introduce the recipient and the nature of your relationship at the poem’s core.  
2. Incorporate the specified emotional tone consistently throughout the poem.  
3. Weave in the key themes naturally using evocative language and imagery.  
4. Follow the preferred poetic style to shape the poem’s rhythm and structure.  
5. Close with a poignant or uplifting message that leaves a lasting impression.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Plain text poem without stanza numbering  
- Style: Emotionally sincere and accessible language suitable for a beginner poet  
- Structure: Must reflect the chosen poetic style with clear thematic coherence

**Output Format:**  
A single, original poem meeting the above criteria, presented as plain text ready for sharing or personal use.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for individuals seeking to create a personalized poem for a loved one or special occasion, such as birthdays, anniversaries, or moments of support. It benefits novice poets or anyone looking to express emotions creatively and meaningfully through verse.
