### P-01345 - Crafting an Impactful Product Launch Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `media relations` `content writing`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional and engaging press release announcing a new product launch that effectively communicates key features, company vision, and call-to-action to media and potential customers.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled communications specialist tasked with creating a press release for a tech company introducing its latest innovative product to the market. The release should capture attention, build excitement, and encourage further engagement from press and consumers.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_features} (name and main features of the new product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_overview} (company name and brief background)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {event_info} (official launch date and any launch event details)  
- **Contact Information:** {contact_person}, {contact_email} (media contact name and email for follow-up)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a compelling headline that highlights the product launch.  
2. Write an introductory paragraph summarizing the product and its significance.  
3. Describe the key features and benefits of the product in clear, concise language.  
4. Include relevant background information about the company to establish credibility.  
5. Provide details about the launch date and any associated events or promotions.  
6. End with a strong call-to-action encouraging media coverage and consumer interest.  
7. Include the contact information for press inquiries at the bottom.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and contact info  
- Style: Professional, clear, and engaging tone suitable for media distribution  
- Structure: Logical flow with headline, introduction, body, conclusion, and contact section  

**Output Format:**  
A complete press release text ready for distribution, formatted with a headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and contact information.
