### P-00850 - Crafting Impactful Press Releases for New Product Launches

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `writing` `marketing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a clear and engaging press release announcing a new product launch that effectively communicates key information to media outlets and potential customers.

**Context:**  
You are a professional communications specialist tasked with creating a press release to generate excitement and awareness about a new product debut for a company’s marketing and PR campaign.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_type}, {key_features} (name, category, and main selling points)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (brand name and brief company overview)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {availability} (release date and where the product will be available)  
- **Contact Information:** {contact_person}, {contact_email} (media contact for follow-up inquiries)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a compelling headline that highlights the product launch.  
2. Write an engaging opening paragraph introducing the product and its significance.  
3. Describe the product’s key features and benefits clearly and concisely.  
4. Include relevant company background and the official launch date with availability.  
5. End with contact information for media inquiries and a call to action.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline, date line, body paragraphs, and contact section  
- Style: Formal, clear, and persuasive tone suitable for media distribution  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to product details to company info and closing contact

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution, including headline, dateline, body, and contact information.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when creating press releases for announcing new products to generate media interest and inform potential customers. It is ideal for marketing teams, PR professionals, and small businesses launching products who need a professional and effective communication tool.
