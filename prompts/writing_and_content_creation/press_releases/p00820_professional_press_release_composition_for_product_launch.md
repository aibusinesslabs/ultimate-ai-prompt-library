### P-00820 - Professional Press Release Composition for Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional press release announcing a new product launch, designed to generate media interest and inform the target audience effectively.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled PR specialist tasked with creating an engaging and informative press release for a company unveiling its latest product. The release should highlight key product features, benefits, and company background, tailored for publication in industry media outlets.

**Input:**  
- **Company & Product Details:** {company_name}, {product_name}, {product_description} (basic info and features)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_event_location} (when and where the launch occurs)  
- **Target Audience & Market:** {target_audience}, {market_positioning} (intended consumers and product’s competitive edge)  
- **Quotes & Contact Info:** {executive_quote}, {media_contact} (statement from a company leader and press contact details)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that captures the essence of the product launch.  
2. Write an engaging lead paragraph summarizing the key news points clearly and concisely.  
3. Elaborate on the product’s unique features and benefits, integrating {product_description} and {market_positioning}.  
4. Include the {launch_date} and {launch_event_location} to provide event specifics.  
5. Add a compelling quote from {executive_quote} to lend authority and human interest.  
6. Close with a brief company overview and include {media_contact} information for follow-up.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Formal press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and boilerplate  
- Style: Professional, clear, and persuasive tone suitable for media distribution  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to closing, with no jargon or promotional exaggeration  

**Output Format:**  
A well-structured press release text ready for publication, adhering to professional standards and including all provided inputs seamlessly.

#### Use Case:  
Ideal for PR professionals and marketers preparing announcements for new product launches. This prompt helps produce media-ready press releases that effectively communicate essential details, generate buzz, and support brand positioning in competitive markets.
