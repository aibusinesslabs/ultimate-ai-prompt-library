### P-01480 - Crafting a Compelling Product Launch Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `marketing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a clear, engaging, and professional press release announcing the launch of a new product.

**Context:**  
You are a professional PR writer tasked with creating a press release that introduces a new product to the market, highlights its key features, and encourages media coverage and customer interest.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_category} (name and category of the product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_location} (date and place of the product launch)  
- **Key Features:** {feature_list} (a brief list of the most important product features)  
- **Company Background:** {company_name}, {company_description} (information about the company releasing the product)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that captures attention and includes the product name.  
2. Write an introductory paragraph summarizing the launch event and product significance.  
3. Describe the key features and benefits of the product in a concise, engaging manner.  
4. Include a quote from a company representative to add credibility and a personal touch.  
5. Close with company background and a call to action for media or customers to learn more.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, date line, body paragraphs, and company info  
- Style: Professional, clear, and persuasive tone suitable for media outlets  
- Structure: Logical flow from announcement to details, then company info and contact

**Output Format:**  
A complete press release text formatted with a headline, date line, body paragraphs, and company information section.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt to quickly generate professional press releases for new product launches, ideal for PR professionals, marketing teams, and startup founders aiming to attract media attention and customer interest with minimal effort.
