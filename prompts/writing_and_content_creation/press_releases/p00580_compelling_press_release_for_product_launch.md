### P-00580 - Compelling Press Release for Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `brand messaging`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Craft a professional and engaging press release announcing the launch of a new product, designed to capture media interest and clearly communicate the product’s value proposition.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned PR specialist tasked with creating a press release for a company unveiling its latest product. The release should highlight key features, target audience benefits, and company background while maintaining a persuasive and authoritative tone.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description} (name and concise description of the new product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_location} (official date and venue or platform for launch)  
- **Company Background:** {company_name}, {company_overview} (brief about the company and its market position)  
- **Key Messages:** {unique_selling_points}, {target_audience} (main benefits of the product and intended customer segment)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that clearly announces the product launch.  
2. Write an engaging opening paragraph summarizing the who, what, when, where, and why.  
3. Elaborate on the product’s unique selling points and how they address the needs of the target audience.  
4. Include relevant background information about the company to establish credibility.  
5. Close with a call to action or next steps for interested parties, such as where to learn more or attend the launch event.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and boilerplate  
- Style: Formal yet approachable and persuasive; clear and concise language  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to introduction, body details, company info, and conclusion  

**Output Format:**  
A fully formatted press release text ready for distribution, including headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and a boilerplate section about the company.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing official communications to announce new products to the media and public. It benefits PR professionals, marketing teams, and brand managers aiming to generate buzz and coverage for product launches in a clear and compelling manner.
