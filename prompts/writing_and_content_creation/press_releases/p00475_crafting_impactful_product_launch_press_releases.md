### P-00475 - Crafting Impactful Product Launch Press Releases

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional and engaging press release announcing the launch of a new product, designed to attract media attention and inform the target audience.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned PR specialist tasked with creating a compelling press release for a company unveiling its latest product. The release should highlight key features, benefits, and the product’s market impact while maintaining a clear and persuasive tone.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_type}, {key_features} (core attributes and unique selling points)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (brief overview and reputation)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {availability}, {pricing} (release timing, market availability, and cost)  
- **Quotes & Contact:** {spokesperson_quote}, {contact_information} (official statement and PR contact info)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline featuring the product name and its main appeal.  
2. Write an engaging opening paragraph summarizing the launch and its significance.  
3. Detail the product’s key features and benefits clearly and persuasively.  
4. Incorporate the company background to establish credibility.  
5. Include the launch specifics: date, availability, and pricing.  
6. Add a relevant quote from the spokesperson that enhances the announcement’s impact.  
7. Close with clear contact information for media inquiries.  
8. Use professional, concise language suitable for journalists and media outlets.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and contact info  
- Style: Formal, informative, and persuasive  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to closing contact details, with distinct paragraphs for each section

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution, formatted with a headline, dateline, body paragraphs, spokesperson quote, and contact information.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing press releases for new product launches to ensure clear communication of essential information and maximize media pickup. Ideal for PR teams, marketing professionals, and content creators focused on product marketing and publicity.
