### P-00040 - Crafting a Compelling Press Release for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a professional and engaging press release announcing the launch of a new product.

**Context:**  
You are a PR specialist tasked with writing a press release that generates excitement and informs media outlets and potential customers about the new product. The release should clearly highlight key features, benefits, and availability.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description} (name and brief description of the product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {availability} (official launch date and where/how the product will be available)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (name and short background about the company)  
- **Contact Details:** {contact_name}, {contact_email} (media contact person and email address)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline featuring the product name and launch announcement.  
2. Write an introductory paragraph summarizing the launch news and why it matters.  
3. Provide detailed information about key product features and benefits.  
4. Include company background to build credibility and context.  
5. End with launch details and clear contact information for media inquiries.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline, body paragraphs, and contact info  
- Style: Clear, concise, and engaging with a formal tone  
- Structure: Logical flow from announcement to details to contact information

**Output Format:**  
A complete press release text ready for distribution, formatted with a headline, multiple paragraphs, and a contact section.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt to quickly generate effective press releases for new product launches that can be sent to media outlets, posted on company websites, or shared across social media to maximize public awareness and interest. Ideal for PR teams, marketers, and small business owners looking to create professional announcements with ease.
