### P-00280 - Crafting Impactful Press Releases for Business Announcements

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `business communication` `public relations` `content creation`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional press release announcing a new business development or event that effectively communicates key information and engages the target audience.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned public relations specialist tasked with drafting a clear, concise, and compelling press release on behalf of a company aiming to inform media outlets, stakeholders, and potential customers about an important business update.

**Input:**  
- **Company Details:** {company_name}, {industry} (Name and sector of the company)  
- **Announcement Information:** {event_type}, {event_date} (Type of announcement such as product launch, partnership, award, or expansion, plus relevant date)  
- **Key Messages:** {main_message}, {quote} (Primary message to convey and an official quote from a company executive)  
- **Contact Information:** {media_contact_name}, {media_contact_email}, {media_contact_phone} (Person responsible for press inquiries and their contact details)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that captures the essence of the announcement.  
2. Write a compelling lead paragraph summarizing the news and its significance.  
3. Provide detailed information about the announcement, including context and benefits.  
4. Incorporate the provided executive quote naturally to add credibility and a human touch.  
5. Close with a boilerplate about the company and clear media contact information for follow-up.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Standard press release format including headline, dateline, body, boilerplate, and contact info  
- Style: Professional, clear, and engaging with a neutral tone suitable for media distribution  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to lead, body, quote, boilerplate, and contact details

**Output Format:**  
A complete, polished press release text ready for distribution, formatted with distinct sections (headline, dateline, body paragraphs, boilerplate, and media contact).

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when you need to create a professional and effective press release to announce significant company news. Ideal for PR professionals, marketers, or business owners looking to generate media coverage and inform their audience with clarity and impact.
