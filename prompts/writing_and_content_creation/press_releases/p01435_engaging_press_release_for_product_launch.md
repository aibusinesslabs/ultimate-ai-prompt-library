### P-01435 - Engaging Press Release for Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `branding` `public relations`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional and engaging press release announcing the launch of a new product that effectively highlights its key features, benefits, and company vision.

**Context:**  
You are a PR specialist tasked with crafting a compelling press release for a tech company launching its latest innovative product. The release should build excitement, inform the media and potential customers, and reinforce the brand’s reputation as an industry leader.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_type}, {key_features} (Name, category, and standout features of the product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_mission} (Company name and mission statement or vision)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {availability} (Official launch date and where/how customers can purchase)  
- **Additional Information:** {quotes}, {contact_info} (Relevant executive quotes, spokesperson comments, and press contact details)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that captures attention and summarizes the announcement.  
2. Write an opening paragraph introducing the product and its significance.  
3. Describe the product’s key features and benefits in clear, concise language.  
4. Include relevant quotes from company representatives to add authority and authenticity.  
5. Provide launch details and instructions for obtaining more information or making purchases.  
6. End with a boilerplate about the company’s mission and background.  
7. Ensure all contact information for media inquiries is clearly presented.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Press release format including headline, dateline, body, quotes, boilerplate, and contact info  
- Style: Professional, clear, and engaging with active voice  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to conclusion with distinct paragraphs and proper journalism conventions

**Output Format:**  
A complete press release text formatted appropriately, ready for distribution, including headline, dateline, body content, executive quotes, company boilerplate, and media contact information.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when launching a new product to generate media-ready press releases that attract journalist attention and inform your target audience. Ideal for PR teams, marketing professionals, and startups preparing official announcements to maximize coverage and build brand awareness.
