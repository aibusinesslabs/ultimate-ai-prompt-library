### P-00205 - Crafting a Compelling Press Release for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `marketing` `public relations`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional press release announcing the launch of a new product, highlighting its key features and benefits to generate media interest and customer excitement.

**Context:**  
You are a public relations specialist tasked with creating a clear and engaging press release that effectively communicates the value of a new product to journalists and potential customers.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description} (name and brief overview of the product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_location} (date and place of the product launch)  
- **Target Audience:** {target_market} (primary customer group for the product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (company name and a short description)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline featuring the product name and launch event.  
2. Introduce the product and summarize its main features and benefits.  
3. Include the launch date and location, emphasizing the significance of the event.  
4. Describe the target audience and explain how the product meets their needs.  
5. Provide a brief overview of the companyâ€™s background and its expertise related to the product.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Formal press release format with headline, dateline, body, and boilerplate  
- Style: Clear, professional, and engaging language suitable for media outlets  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to introduction, body, and closing statement  

**Output Format:**  
A complete press release text formatted with a headline, dateline, opening paragraph, supporting details, and company boilerplate.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when launching a new product to quickly generate a polished press release that can be distributed to media outlets and posted on company websites. Ideal for PR professionals and marketing teams seeking an effective communication tool to announce product launches.
