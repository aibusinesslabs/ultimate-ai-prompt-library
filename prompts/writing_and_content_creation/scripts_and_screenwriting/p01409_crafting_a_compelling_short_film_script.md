**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `screenwriting` `short film` `character development` `dialogue`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**

**Context:**

- **Duration & Format**: {{duration_minutes}}, {{format}} (estimated runtime in minutes and screenplay formatting preferences)

1. Develop a three-act structure with a clear beginning, middle, and end suitable for a short film lasting approximately {{duration_minutes}} minutes.

- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Proper screenplay format (scene headings, action, dialogue) or specified {{format}}

**Output Format:**  
A fully formatted short film script excerpt (300-500 words) including scene headings, character names, dialogue, and action descriptions ready for review or submission.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when you need to generate a concise, compelling script for a short film project, especially for film festivals or portfolio building. It benefits screenwriters seeking structured guidance to balance character development and plot within short runtime constraints.
