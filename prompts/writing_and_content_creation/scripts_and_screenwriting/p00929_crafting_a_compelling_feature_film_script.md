### P-00929 - Crafting a Compelling Feature Film Script

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `screenwriting` `feature film` `character development` `plot structure`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Develop a polished, original feature film script treatment that includes a clear three-act structure, well-defined characters, and a captivating central conflict, setting the foundation for a full screenplay.

**Context:**  
You are an experienced screenwriter tasked with creating a detailed script treatment for a feature film. Your goal is to provide a concise yet vivid overview that outlines the narrative arc, major character motivations, and key plot points, suitable for pitching to producers or guiding the writing of a full screenplay.

**Input:**  
- **Story Details:** {genre}, {setting}, {time period} (the thematic and environmental context of the film)  
- **Characters:** {protagonist name and description}, {antagonist name and description}, {supporting characters descriptions} (primary characters with motivations and relationships)  
- **Plot Elements:** {central conflict}, {major plot twists}, {resolution concept} (core narrative elements shaping the story)  
- **Tone & Style:** {desired tone}, {visual style}, {target audience} (overall mood, aesthetic, and demographic focus)

**Instructions:**  
1. Summarize the story premise concisely, highlighting the unique hook within the selected genre and setting.  
2. Outline the three-act structure, specifying the key events in each act that drive the plot forward and escalate tension.  
3. Describe the protagonist’s journey, including motivations, obstacles, and growth, as well as the antagonist’s role and influence.  
4. Incorporate the major plot twists and how they impact the story’s direction and character dynamics.  
5. Conclude with the resolution concept that ties up the narrative and suggests the thematic message, ensuring alignment with the desired tone and style.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Narrative prose with clear section headings for premise, acts, characters, plot twists, and resolution  
- Style: Professional, engaging, and cinematic language that evokes visual imagery  
- Structure: Logical flow following the three-act screenplay paradigm and character development arcs  

**Output Format:**  
A structured script treatment document with distinct sections: Premise, Act I, Act II, Act III, Character Profiles, Plot Twists, and Resolution Summary, formatted as a polished narrative suitable for professional screenplay pitching.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for screenwriters, filmmakers, or content creators developing original feature film ideas who need a compelling foundation for their scripts. It supports pitching projects to producers or preparing a detailed guide before writing the full screenplay, ensuring clarity and engagement in early-stage development.
