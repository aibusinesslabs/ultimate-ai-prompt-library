### P-01150 - Crafting a Compelling Press Release for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `marketing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional press release announcing the launch of a new product.

**Context:**  
You are a communications specialist tasked with composing a clear and engaging press release that introduces a new product to the media and potential customers, highlighting its key features and benefits.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description} (name and brief description of the product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_location} (date and location of the product launch event or availability)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (company name and brief background)  
- **Contact Information:** {contact_name}, {contact_email} (media contact person and email for inquiries)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that captures attention and includes the product name.  
2. Write a compelling opening paragraph summarizing the product launch, including the launch date and location.  
3. Describe the productâ€™s main features and benefits clearly and concisely.  
4. Provide brief background information about the company to build credibility.  
5. End with contact information for media inquiries and a call to action.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and contact info  
- Style: Professional, informative, and engaging  
- Structure: Clear headline, introductory paragraph, detailed body, company info, and closing contact section

**Output Format:**  
A fully formatted press release text ready for distribution, including headline, dateline, body content, and contact details.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for small businesses or marketing teams preparing to announce new products to the media and public. It ensures consistent and professional messaging to increase visibility and generate interest during product launches.
