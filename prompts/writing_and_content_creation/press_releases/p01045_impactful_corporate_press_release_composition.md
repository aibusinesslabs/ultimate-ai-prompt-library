### P-01045 - Impactful Corporate Press Release Composition

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `press release` `corporate communication` `public relations` `media outreach`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a professional and compelling corporate press release that effectively announces a significant company milestone or event, designed to engage media outlets and target audiences.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned corporate communications specialist tasked with drafting an official press release for a reputable company. The goal is to clearly convey the importance of the event, highlight key details, and elicit interest from journalists, stakeholders, and the public.

**Input:**  
- **Company Details:** {company_name}, {industry}, {headquarters_location} (Basic background and positioning)  
- **Event Information:** {event_type}, {event_date}, {event_location}, {event_significance} (Details about the milestone or announcement)  
- **Key Quotes:** {executive_name}, {executive_title}, {quote_content} (A relevant quote from a company leader)  
- **Additional Context:** {target_audience}, {call_to_action}, {media_contact_info} (Supplementary information for media and readers)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong, attention-grabbing headline summarizing the main announcement.  
2. Write a concise but informative opening paragraph that includes the who, what, when, where, and why.  
3. Expand on the eventâ€™s significance, incorporating relevant industry context and company background.  
4. Integrate the executive quote naturally to add authority and personal insight.  
5. Conclude with a clear call to action and provide media contact details for follow-up inquiries.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: Between 500 and 750 words  
- Format: Use standard press release formatting including headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and boilerplate  
- Style: Formal, professional, and engaging with clear, jargon-free language  
- Structure: Logical flow from announcement to details, quote inclusion, and closing statements

**Output Format:**  
A fully formatted, polished press release text suitable for immediate distribution to media outlets.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing major corporate announcements such as product launches, partnership deals, or milestone achievements. Ideal for PR professionals, corporate communications teams, and marketing specialists seeking to draft impactful press releases that resonate with media and public audiences.
