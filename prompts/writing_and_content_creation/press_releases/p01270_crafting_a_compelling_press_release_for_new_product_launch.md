### P-01270 - Crafting a Compelling Press Release for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a clear and engaging press release announcing the launch of a new product, highlighting its key features, benefits, and availability.

**Context:**  
You are a professional communications specialist tasked with creating a press release that attracts media attention and informs potential customers about the new product.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description} (name and brief description of the product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {availability_details} (release date and where/how the product can be purchased)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (company name and a short background or mission statement)  
- **Contact Information:** {contact_person}, {contact_email} (media contact person and email address for inquiries)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that includes the product name and its main appeal.  
2. Write an introductory paragraph summarizing the launch and its significance.  
3. Detail the key features and benefits of the product in the body.  
4. Include relevant information about the launch date and how customers can obtain the product.  
5. End with a brief company background and provide clear contact details for media follow-up.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline, body, and contact info  
- Style: Formal, informative, and engaging  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to contact details, using short paragraphs  

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release ready for distribution, formatted with a headline, body paragraphs, and contact information section at the end.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for startups, businesses, and marketing teams preparing announcements for new product launches. It helps create professional press releases that effectively communicate key information to journalists and customers.
