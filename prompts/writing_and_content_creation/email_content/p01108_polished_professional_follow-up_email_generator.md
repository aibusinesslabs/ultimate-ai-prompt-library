### P-01108 - Polished Professional Follow-Up Email Generator

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Email Content  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `email` `professional` `follow-up` `communication`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a concise, professional follow-up email to a client or colleague to politely remind them of a previous conversation or request.

**Context:**  
You are a professional assistant tasked with crafting clear and courteous follow-up emails that maintain a positive tone and encourage a timely response without sounding pushy.

**Input:**  
- **Recipient Details**: {recipient_name}, {recipient_position} (name and title of the email recipient)  
- **Previous Interaction**: {previous_topic}, {previous_date} (subject and date of last communication)  
- **Purpose of Follow-Up**: {follow_up_reason} (reason for following up)  
- **Sender Details**: {sender_name}, {sender_position} (your name and title)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a polite greeting addressing {recipient_name} by name.  
2. Reference the previous interaction by mentioning {previous_topic} and {previous_date}.  
3. Clearly state the purpose of the follow-up using {follow_up_reason}.  
4. Express appreciation for their time and consideration.  
5. Close with a professional sign-off including {sender_name} and {sender_position}.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Email format with greeting, body, and closing  
- Style: Professional, polite, and concise  
- Structure: Introduction, reason for follow-up, courteous closing  

**Output Format:**  
A fully composed, ready-to-send follow-up email text adhering to the above instructions and constraints.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when you need to send a polite and professional reminder email after an initial conversation or request. Ideal for professionals in any industry looking to maintain courteous communication and improve response rates without seeming intrusive.
