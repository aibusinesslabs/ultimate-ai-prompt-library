### P-00690 - Crafting a Thematic Poem for Beginners

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative writing` `thematic` `beginner`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create an original poem centered around a specific theme, using vivid imagery and clear emotional expression appropriate for beginner-level poetry.

**Context:**  
You are an aspiring poet seeking to develop foundational skills in thematic poetry. The goal is to express ideas clearly and creatively within a concise format, focusing on emotional resonance and descriptive language.

**Input:**  
- **Theme & Emotion**: {theme}, {emotion} (the central topic and feeling the poem should convey)  
- **Form & Style**: {form}, {style} (poetic form such as free verse or rhyme, and style like simple or narrative)  
- **Imagery & Tone**: {imagery_keywords} (specific images or symbols to incorporate)  
- **Audience & Purpose**: {audience}, {purpose} (intended reader type and reason for the poem)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin by interpreting the given theme and emotion to form the central idea of the poem.  
2. Select the poem’s form and style as specified, ensuring consistency throughout the piece.  
3. Incorporate the provided imagery keywords to create vivid mental pictures that enhance the emotional impact.  
4. Write a poem between 150 and 300 words that connects with the intended audience and fulfills the poem’s purpose.  
5. Edit for clarity, emotional expression, and adherence to the chosen style and structure.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Plain text poem, with line breaks to reflect poetic structure  
- Style: Beginner-friendly language, emotionally engaging, and thematically coherent  
- Structure: Follow the chosen poetic form (e.g., rhyme scheme or free verse)

**Output Format:**  
A single, original poem formatted with appropriate line breaks that clearly reflects the theme, emotion, and style specified.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for beginner poets or creative writing students aiming to practice thematic poetry with guided inputs. It helps develop foundational skills in poetic form, emotional expression, and imagery through structured, hands-on creation.
