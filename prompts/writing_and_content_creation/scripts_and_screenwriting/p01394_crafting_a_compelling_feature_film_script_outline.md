### P-01394 - Crafting a Compelling Feature Film Script Outline

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `screenwriting` `film` `story-structure` `character-development`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a detailed and professional feature film script outline that includes a three-act structure, well-defined characters, key plot points, and thematic elements suitable for a compelling screenplay.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned screenwriter and story analyst tasked with developing a comprehensive outline that will serve as the blueprint for writing a full-length feature film script. Your expertise lies in structuring narratives with strong character arcs and coherent pacing aligned with industry standards.

**Input:**  
- **Story Essentials:** {genre}, {central_theme}, {setting_description} (genre of the film, main theme to explore, and a brief description of the storyâ€™s setting)  
- **Characters:** {protagonist_name}, {protagonist_goal}, {antagonist_name}, {antagonist_goal} (names and primary objectives of the protagonist and antagonist)  
- **Plot Points:** {inciting_incident}, {major_conflict}, {climax} (key events that drive the narrative forward)  
- **Tone and Style:** {tone_description}, {visual_style} (desired emotional tone and visual style for the script)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Develop a three-act outline that clearly defines the setup, confrontation, and resolution phases of the story.  
2. Introduce and describe the protagonist and antagonist, emphasizing their goals, motivations, and conflicts.  
3. Identify and detail major plot points including the inciting incident, major conflict, and climax, ensuring each advances the narrative logically.  
4. Integrate the central theme throughout the outline, demonstrating how it influences character decisions and story progression.  
5. Specify the intended tone and visual style, linking these elements to narrative choices and atmosphere to guide screenplay development.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Structured outline with clear headings for each act and section  
- Style: Professional, engaging, and clear with screenplay industry terminology  
- Structure: Logical flow following the three-act structure, including character arcs and thematic focus  

**Output Format:**  
A structured, professional feature film script outline divided into three acts, with sections for characters, plot points, theme integration, and tone/style descriptions, formatted as a clean, readable document suitable for presentation to producers or writers.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for screenwriters, script consultants, and film students who need to develop a coherent and compelling film outline as a foundation for writing or pitching a feature-length screenplay. It aids in clarifying story structure, character motivation, and thematic elements early in the creative process.
