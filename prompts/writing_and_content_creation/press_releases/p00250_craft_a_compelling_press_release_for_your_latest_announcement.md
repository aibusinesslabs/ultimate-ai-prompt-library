### P-00250 - Craft a Compelling Press Release for Your Latest Announcement

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `public relations` `content writing` `beginner`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional and engaging press release announcing a new product, service, or event for a company.

**Context:**  
You are a communications specialist tasked with crafting a clear and persuasive press release that highlights the key details and benefits of the announcement to attract media attention and inform the public.

**Input:**  
- **Company Details:** {company_name}, {company_location} (name and location of the company)  
- **Announcement Details:** {announcement_type}, {announcement_topic} (type of announcement such as product launch, event, or service, and its main focus)  
- **Key Features:** {feature_list} (three to five main features or benefits of the announcement)  
- **Contact Information:** {contact_name}, {contact_email}, {contact_phone} (media contact details for further inquiries)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that summarizes the announcement clearly.  
2. Write an introductory paragraph that provides the what, when, where, and why of the announcement.  
3. Detail the key features or benefits in the body, emphasizing value to the target audience.  
4. Include a quote from a company spokesperson to add credibility and a personal touch.  
5. End with the contact information and a standard company boilerplate paragraph.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Use standard press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and contact info  
- Style: Professional, concise, and informative with a positive tone  
- Structure: Clear headline, informative lead, detailed body, quote, and closing contact section  

**Output Format:**  
A complete, polished press release text ready for distribution, formatted with headline, dateline, body, quote, and contact information sections.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when you need to quickly generate a professional press release for a new business announcement, ensuring consistent messaging and media readiness. Ideal for startups, small businesses, or communications teams launching new products, services, or events.
