### P-01425 - Crafting a Thematic Narrative Poem

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `poetry` `narrative` `creative-writing` `beginner`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a narrative poem that tells a cohesive story centered around a specific theme, incorporating vivid imagery and emotional depth.

**Context:**  
You are an aspiring poet aiming to create an accessible yet engaging narrative poem. Your goal is to produce a beginner-friendly piece that effectively conveys a story through poetic devices while maintaining clarity and flow.

**Input:**  
- **Theme & Setting:** {theme}, {setting} (central idea and backdrop of the poem)  
- **Main Character:** {character_name}, {character_trait} (protagonist’s name and defining trait)  
- **Plot Elements:** {conflict}, {resolution} (primary challenge and how it is resolved)  
- **Tone & Style:** {tone}, {style} (desired emotional tone and writing style)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a compelling opening that introduces the setting and main character.  
2. Develop the narrative by describing the conflict using sensory details and imagery.  
3. Build emotional tension through the character’s experiences and reactions.  
4. Resolve the conflict clearly, highlighting growth or change in the character.  
5. Maintain a consistent tone and style throughout the poem to enhance readability and engagement.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Free verse or rhymed stanzas (choose one and remain consistent)  
- Style: Clear, accessible language suitable for beginner-level poetry  
- Structure: Narrative arc with introduction, conflict, and resolution

**Output Format:**  
A single narrative poem formatted in stanzas or free verse, with a title reflecting the theme, and clearly structured to convey a complete story within the word count.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for beginner poets, creative writing students, or content creators seeking to practice storytelling through poetry. It supports the development of narrative skills while encouraging creative expression within a manageable word limit.
