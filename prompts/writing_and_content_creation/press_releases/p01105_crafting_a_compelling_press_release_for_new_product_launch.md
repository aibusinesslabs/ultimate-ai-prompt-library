### P-01105 - Crafting a Compelling Press Release for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a clear and engaging press release announcing the launch of a new product.

**Context:**  
You are a communications specialist tasked with drafting a professional press release that highlights the key features and benefits of a new product for a broad audience, aiming to generate media interest and customer awareness.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description} (name and brief description of the product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_location} (date and place of the launch event)  
- **Company Background:** {company_name}, {company_overview} (company name and a short description)  
- **Contact Information:** {contact_person}, {contact_email} (media contact details)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline featuring the product name and launch event.  
2. Write an opening paragraph summarizing the announcement with key facts.  
3. Elaborate on the product features and unique selling points in the body.  
4. Include a brief company overview to establish credibility.  
5. End with contact information and a call-to-action for media inquiries.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150 to 300 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, body, and contact section  
- Style: Professional, clear, and engaging tone suitable for a general audience  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to closing contact details

**Output Format:**  
A finalized press release text ready for distribution, formatted with a headline, body paragraphs, and a contact information section.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for small businesses or startups preparing to announce a new product launch to media outlets and potential customers. It helps create a professional press release quickly and effectively, even for users with limited writing experience.
