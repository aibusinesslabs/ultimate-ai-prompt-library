### P-00235 - Crafting Impactful Press Releases for New Product Launches

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional and engaging press release announcing a new product launch, designed to generate media interest and attract potential customers.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled PR writer tasked with crafting a clear, concise, and compelling press release that highlights the unique features and benefits of a new product while maintaining the company’s brand voice.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_category}, {key_features} (Essential information about the product, its category, and standout features)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (Brief background of the company and its reputation)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {availability} (The official launch date and where/how the product will be available)  
- **Target Audience & Benefits:** {target_audience}, {customer_benefits} (Intended audience and key advantages the product delivers)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that captures attention and clearly states the product launch.  
2. Write a concise opening paragraph summarizing the product, launch date, and company.  
3. Elaborate on the product’s key features and how they address customer needs or solve problems.  
4. Include a quote from a company executive or product expert to add credibility and personality.  
5. Provide details about availability, pricing (if applicable), and where customers can learn more or purchase.  
6. End with a boilerplate paragraph about the company and contact information for media inquiries.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, quotes, and boilerplate  
- Style: Professional, clear, informative, and persuasive without jargon  
- Structure: Logical flow from announcement to details, benefits, quote, and closing information  

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution, formatted with a headline, dateline line, body paragraphs, a quoted statement, and a company boilerplate section.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing press releases to announce new product launches in a professional manner that appeals to journalists, industry influencers, and potential customers. Ideal for PR teams, marketing professionals, or content creators aiming to maximize media coverage and public interest.
