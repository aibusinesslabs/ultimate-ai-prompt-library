### P-01075 - Crafting Impactful Press Releases for Product Launches

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `media relations` `branding`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a compelling, professional press release announcing a new product launch that captures media attention, highlights key features, and communicates the brand’s unique value proposition.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned PR specialist tasked with drafting a press release for a company’s latest product. The release should appeal to journalists, potential customers, and stakeholders, positioning the product as innovative and essential in its market segment.

**Input:**  
- **Product Information:** {{product_name}}, {{product_type}}, {{key_features}} (name, category, and standout features)  
- **Company Details:** {{company_name}}, {{founder_or_ceo}}, {{brand_values}} (organization name, leadership, and core principles)  
- **Launch Specifics:** {{launch_date}}, {{launch_event_details}}, {{availability}} (date, event info, distribution channels)  
- **Target Audience & Media Angle:** {{target_market}}, {{industry_trends}}, {{competitive_advantage}} (intended customers, relevant market context, unique selling points)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that is newsworthy and includes the product name.  
2. Write an engaging lead paragraph summarizing the announcement and its significance.  
3. Elaborate on the product’s features, benefits, and how it addresses customer needs or solves a problem.  
4. Incorporate relevant quotes from company leadership to add credibility and human interest.  
5. Provide clear details about the launch date, event, and product availability, ending with a call to action for media or consumers.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and boilerplate  
- Style: Professional, concise, persuasive, and journalistic tone suitable for wide media distribution  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to conclusion, use of short paragraphs and clear language  

**Output Format:**  
A polished, ready-to-distribute press release text that can be directly shared with media outlets and posted on company channels.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt to efficiently generate high-quality press releases for new product launches, helping PR teams and marketers save time while ensuring messaging is clear, compelling, and aligned with brand identity. Ideal for startups, product managers, and communications professionals preparing for media outreach campaigns.
