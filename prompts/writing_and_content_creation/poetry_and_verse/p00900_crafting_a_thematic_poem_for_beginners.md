### P-00900 - Crafting a Thematic Poem for Beginners

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative-writing` `beginner` `thematic`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a short poem that explores a specific theme, incorporating given keywords and adhering to a beginner-friendly poetic style.

**Context:**  
You are a novice poet aiming to express emotions and ideas through simple yet meaningful verse. Your goal is to craft a poem that resonates with readers while practicing foundational poetic techniques.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Keywords:** {theme}, {keyword1}, {keyword2} (central idea and two important words to include)  
- **Tone:** {tone} (emotional tone, e.g., hopeful, melancholic)  
- **Poem Type:** {poem_type} (e.g., free verse, haiku, rhymed quatrain)  
- **Target Audience:** {audience} (intended readers, e.g., children, general audience)

**Instructions:**  
1. Understand the theme and incorporate both keywords naturally within the poem.  
2. Maintain the specified tone throughout the poem to evoke the desired emotion.  
3. Follow the structural conventions of the chosen poem type.  
4. Use clear and simple language appropriate for a beginner poet and the target audience.  
5. Ensure the poem is engaging and coherent, reflecting the theme effectively.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Text only, poem format with appropriate line breaks  
- Style: Beginner-friendly, emotionally resonant, accessible vocabulary  
- Structure: Adhere to the chosen poem typeâ€™s format

**Output Format:**  
A neatly formatted poem that includes the theme and keywords, with clear line breaks and a consistent tone, suitable for the specified audience.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for beginner poets seeking guided practice in writing themed poetry. It helps build confidence in crafting expressive verses while learning to balance structure, tone, and vocabulary for diverse audiences.
