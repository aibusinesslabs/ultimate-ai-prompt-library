### P-01244 - Crafting a Compelling Feature Film Screenplay

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `screenplay` `feature_film` `character_development` `plot_structure`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Develop a detailed, professionally structured screenplay for a feature-length film that effectively integrates complex characters, a compelling narrative arc, and cinematic dialogue.

**Context:**  
You are an experienced screenwriter tasked with creating a polished and engaging screenplay for a feature film. Your expertise includes crafting nuanced characters, structuring multi-act narratives, and writing dialogue that resonates with audiences while advancing the plot.

**Input:**  
- **Character Profiles & Relationships:** {{main_character}}, {{supporting_characters}}, {{character_arcs}} (detailed descriptions of protagonist, key supporting roles, and their development throughout the story)  
- **Plot Outline & Genre:** {{genre}}, {{plot_summary}}, {{key_conflicts}} (overall genre, brief story synopsis, and major conflicts driving the narrative)  
- **Setting & Tone:** {{primary_setting}}, {{time_period}}, {{tone}} (main locations, historical or contemporary period, and emotional atmosphere)  
- **Screenplay Specifications:** {{desired_length}}, {{format_style}} (target runtime or page count, and preferred screenplay formatting conventions, e.g., industry-standard formatting)

**Instructions:**  
1. Construct a three-act structure screenplay that incorporates the provided plot outline, ensuring clear setup, confrontation, and resolution phases.  
2. Develop rich, multidimensional characters with distinct voices and motivations, reflecting the character profiles and arcs given.  
3. Write cinematic, natural dialogue that reveals character, advances the story, and maintains pacing suitable for feature film.  
4. Integrate the specified setting and tone consistently throughout scenes to create immersive world-building and atmosphere.  
5. Format the screenplay following industry standards, including scene headings, action lines, character cues, and dialogue formatting, while adhering to the desired length.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Industry-standard screenplay format (sluglines, action, dialogue, parentheticals)  
- Style: Professional, engaging, cinematic, and character-driven  
- Structure: Strict three-act narrative structure with clear plot progression and character development

**Output Format:**  
A complete screenplay excerpt or condensed version featuring at least one major scene per act, formatted according to professional screenplay standards, ready for submission or pitching.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when developing high-quality feature film screenplays for professional writers, screenwriting students, or creative teams seeking a detailed and structured narrative foundation. Ideal for creating compelling scripts that meet industry expectations and storytelling excellence.
