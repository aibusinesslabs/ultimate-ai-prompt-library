### P-01135 - Crafting a Concise Press Release for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `branding` `public relations`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a clear and engaging press release announcing a new product launch.

**Context:**  
You are a communications specialist tasked with creating a professional press release that effectively introduces a new product to the media and target audience, highlighting key features and company background.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description} (name and brief description of the new product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_location} (date and place of the product launch)  
- **Company Background:** {company_name}, {company_overview} (companyâ€™s name and a short description)  
- **Contact Information:** {contact_person}, {contact_email} (person and email for media inquiries)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a compelling headline featuring the product name.  
2. Introduce the product and its main benefits in the opening paragraph.  
3. Provide details about the launch date and location.  
4. Include a brief overview of the company to establish credibility.  
5. End with contact information for media follow-up.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Formal press release style with clear paragraphs  
- Style: Professional, concise, and informative tone  
- Structure: Headline, introduction, product details, company info, contact section

**Output Format:**  
A polished, ready-to-publish press release text adhering to the specified structure and style.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for PR professionals or small business owners preparing to announce a new product. It ensures the press release is concise, clear, and media-friendly, suitable for distribution to journalists and stakeholders.
