### P-01230 - Evoking Emotion: Crafting a Thematic Poem

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative writing` `emotion` `theme`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a short poem that evokes a specific emotion through vivid imagery and thematic focus.

**Context:**  
You are a beginner poet aiming to explore how language and imagery can convey emotions effectively. Your goal is to write a poem that resonates emotionally with readers by centering on a chosen theme.

**Input:**  
- **Theme & Emotion:** {theme}, {emotion} (the central topic and feeling to be expressed)  
- **Imagery Elements:** {image1}, {image2} (two vivid sensory images related to the theme)  
- **Tone:** {tone} (desired tone or mood, e.g., hopeful, melancholic, joyful)  
- **Poem Style:** {style} (style preference, e.g., free verse, rhymed couplets, haiku)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin by reflecting on the given {theme} and {emotion} to establish the poem’s emotional core.  
2. Incorporate the provided {image1} and {image2} to create vivid sensory details that reinforce the theme.  
3. Maintain the specified {tone} throughout the poem to ensure a consistent mood.  
4. Follow the {style} guidelines to structure your poem appropriately.  
5. Review and refine the poem to ensure clarity and emotional impact.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Plain text poem  
- Style: Emotionally expressive and clear language suitable for beginners  
- Structure: Adhere to the selected poem style’s conventions

**Output Format:**  
A polished poem, formatted as a plain text block, reflecting the specified theme, emotion, imagery, tone, and style.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt to help beginner poets practice crafting emotionally impactful poems around specific themes and imagery. Ideal for creative writing classes, personal exploration, or poetry workshops focused on emotional expression.
