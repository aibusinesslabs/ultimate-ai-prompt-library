### P-00694 - Crafting a Compelling Short Story Outline

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Creative Writing  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `creative-writing` `storytelling` `outline` `fiction`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a detailed outline for a short story that incorporates the given characters, setting, conflict, and theme.

**Context:**  
You are a professional fiction writer specializing in short stories. Your goal is to develop a structured story outline that clearly defines the plot progression, character arcs, and thematic elements to guide the writing process.

**Input:**  
- **Characters & Setting:** {{characters}}, {{setting}} (brief descriptions of main characters and story location/time period)  
- **Conflict:** {{conflict}} (central problem or challenge facing the protagonist)  
- **Theme:** {{theme}} (underlying message or moral of the story)  
- **Tone & Genre:** {{tone}}, {{genre}} (overall mood and literary genre)

**Instructions:**  
1. Introduce the main characters and setting, establishing the story’s initial context.  
2. Describe the inciting incident that introduces the central conflict.  
3. Outline the key plot points or events that escalate the conflict.  
4. Detail the climax where the protagonist faces the conflict’s peak challenge.  
5. Conclude with the resolution and how the theme is reflected in the story’s outcome.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Organized outline with bullet points or numbered sections  
- Style: Clear, concise, and professional with creative flair  
- Structure: Logical progression from introduction to resolution, emphasizing character development and thematic coherence

**Output Format:**  
A structured short story outline formatted with headings and bullet points or numbered steps, suitable for guiding story writing.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when planning a short story to ensure a well-organized narrative structure. It benefits writers seeking clarity on plot development and thematic integration before drafting their story. Ideal for creative writing workshops, author brainstorming sessions, or personal writing projects.
