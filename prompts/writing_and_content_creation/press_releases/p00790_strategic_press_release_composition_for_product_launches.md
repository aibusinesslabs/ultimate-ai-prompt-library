### P-00790 - Strategic Press Release Composition for Product Launches

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content strategy`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Craft a compelling, professional press release announcing a new product launch that effectively communicates key information, builds excitement, and aligns with the brand’s voice and marketing goals.

**Context:**  
You are a senior communications specialist tasked with creating a press release for a high-profile product launch. The press release should engage journalists, stakeholders, and potential customers by clearly outlining the product’s unique features, market impact, and company positioning, while maintaining an authoritative and persuasive tone.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_features} (Name and key features of the product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (Company name and brief background)  
- **Launch Specifics:** {launch_date}, {availability_markets} (Official launch date and target markets)  
- **Quotes & Testimonials:** {executive_quote}, {customer_testimonial} (Quote from a company executive and a relevant testimonial or endorsement)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong, attention-grabbing headline that highlights the product launch.  
2. Write an engaging lead paragraph summarizing the what, when, where, and why of the launch.  
3. Detail the product’s unique features and benefits, emphasizing how it addresses customer needs or industry gaps.  
4. Incorporate the executive quote to add credibility and reflect the company’s vision.  
5. Include the customer testimonial to demonstrate real-world value and social proof.  
6. Provide relevant company background information to contextualize the product launch.  
7. Specify launch specifics including date and geographic availability.  
8. Conclude with a clear call-to-action or next steps for media and consumers.  
9. Ensure the tone remains professional, persuasive, and aligned with the brand voice throughout.  
10. Proofread and format for clarity, readability, and journalistic standards.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, dateline, body, quotes, and boilerplate  
- Style: Formal, clear, informative, and persuasive  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to conclusion; use paragraphs, subheadings optional but encouraged; no jargon or overly technical language

**Output Format:**  
A polished, ready-to-distribute press release text including:  
- Headline  
- Dateline with location and date  
- Body paragraphs incorporating product details, quotes, company info, launch specifics  
- Boilerplate company description  
- Contact information placeholder

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing professional press releases for new product launches intended for media distribution, investor relations, or public announcements. Ideal for marketing teams, PR agencies, and corporate communications professionals seeking to craft persuasive, well-structured press releases that maximize media pickup and audience engagement.
