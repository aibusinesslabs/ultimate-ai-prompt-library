### P-01200 - Evocative Thematic Poetry Composition

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative_writing` `theme` `verse_structure`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a thematically rich and evocative poem that explores the given theme and emotions while adhering to specified stylistic and structural elements.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled poet tasked with crafting a poem that deeply resonates with readers by weaving together theme, tone, and imagery. Your goal is to create an original piece that balances emotional depth with poetic form suitable for publication or literary presentation.

**Input:**  
- **Theme & Emotion:** {theme}, {primary_emotion} (central idea and dominant feeling to convey)  
- **Tone & Style:** {tone}, {style} (desired mood and poetic style, e.g., lyrical, narrative)  
- **Structural Elements:** {verse_count}, {line_length} (number of stanzas and approximate lines per stanza)  
- **Imagery & Language:** {imagery_focus}, {vocabulary_level} (preferred imagery type and complexity of language)

**Instructions:**  
1. Interpret the provided {theme} and embed the {primary_emotion} throughout the poem to evoke strong reader connection.  
2. Maintain a consistent {tone} and {style} that complements the theme and emotion.  
3. Construct the poem using exactly {verse_count} stanzas, each containing approximately {line_length} lines.  
4. Integrate vivid {imagery_focus} imagery and employ language matching the {vocabulary_level} to enhance sensory experience.  
5. Ensure smooth flow and cohesion between stanzas while avoiding clich√©s and maintaining originality.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Poem with clear stanza breaks and consistent line lengths  
- Style: Reflect the specified {style} and {tone} with carefully selected diction  
- Structure: Exactly {verse_count} stanzas, ~{line_length} lines each, balanced rhythm or free verse as appropriate

**Output Format:**  
A polished, original poem formatted with stanza breaks, adhering to the input parameters and constraints, suitable for immediate use or presentation.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for poets, educators, or content creators seeking to generate original poetry centered on a clear theme and emotional tone. It supports creative exploration while maintaining structured guidance for balanced and evocative verse composition.
