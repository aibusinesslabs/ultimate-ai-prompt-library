### P-01300 - Impactful Product Launch Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `professional writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Craft a compelling and professional press release announcing a new product launch that effectively communicates the product’s unique value proposition, key features, and company vision to media outlets and target audiences.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned PR specialist tasked with creating a polished press release for a company introducing an innovative product to the market. Your goal is to generate excitement and credibility while maintaining journalistic integrity and brand voice.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description} (name and concise explanation of the product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_location} (official launch date and event/location details)  
- **Company Background:** {company_name}, {company_overview} (company name and brief background or mission statement)  
- **Key Messages:** {target_audience}, {unique_selling_points}, {quotes_from_executives} (intended audience, product highlights, and executive quotes)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that captures attention and includes the product name.  
2. Write a compelling lead paragraph summarizing the launch event, product significance, and timing.  
3. Elaborate on the product’s unique features and benefits, addressing the target audience’s needs and interests.  
4. Incorporate relevant quotes from company executives to add authority and a personal touch.  
5. Conclude with company background information, contact details, and a call to action for media or interested parties.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, dateline, body, boilerplate, and contact section  
- Style: Professional, engaging, clear, and concise with a journalistic tone  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to call to action, using short paragraphs and bullet points where appropriate

**Output Format:**  
A complete press release text ready for distribution, formatted with headline, dateline, body sections, boilerplate, and media contact information.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt to generate press releases that announce new products, helping PR teams, marketers, or startup founders communicate effectively with journalists and target customers. Ideal for product launches requiring impactful messaging and professional presentation to maximize media coverage.
