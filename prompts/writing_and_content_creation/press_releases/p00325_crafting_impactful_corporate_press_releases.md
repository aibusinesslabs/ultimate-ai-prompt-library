### P-00325 - Crafting Impactful Corporate Press Releases

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `corporate communication` `public relations` `media`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a compelling and professional press release announcing a new corporate initiative, event, or product launch that effectively communicates key information and generates media interest.

**Context:**  
You are a corporate communications specialist tasked with drafting a press release for your company. The goal is to clearly present the news, highlight its significance, and engage journalists and stakeholders while maintaining the brandâ€™s professional tone.

**Input:**  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {industry}, {headquarters_location} (basic company profile)  
- **Announcement Details:** {announcement_type}, {key_features}, {launch_date} (description of the news)  
- **Quotes and Statements:** {executive_name}, {executive_title}, {quote} (relevant leadership commentary)  
- **Additional Information:** {contact_info}, {website_url} (media contact details and online resources)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that captures the essence of the announcement.  
2. Write a concise lead paragraph summarizing the most critical facts: who, what, when, where, and why.  
3. Expand in the body paragraphs on the significance and benefits of the announcement, incorporating key features and context.  
4. Include a relevant, authentic quote from the designated executive to add authority and perspective.  
5. Close with a boilerplate about the company and clear media contact information for follow-ups.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, dateline, body, quote, boilerplate, and contact info  
- Style: Formal, clear, engaging, and free of jargon  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to closing statement, with distinct paragraphs  

**Output Format:**  
A professionally formatted press release document ready for distribution to media outlets, adhering to the structure and style guidelines outlined.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing official announcements for media distribution to maximize clarity and impact. It benefits PR professionals, corporate communicators, and marketing teams aiming to effectively share news and attract media coverage. Ideal for product launches, corporate milestones, or event announcements.
