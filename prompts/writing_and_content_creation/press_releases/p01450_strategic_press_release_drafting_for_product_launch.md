### P-01450 - Strategic Press Release Drafting for Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Craft a compelling and professional press release announcing the launch of a new product to generate media interest and consumer excitement.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled communications specialist tasked with creating a press release that highlights the key features and benefits of a new product, conveys the brand’s messaging clearly, and motivates journalists and the public to engage with the product launch.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description}, {unique_selling_points} (core features and value propositions)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_event_details} (when and how the product will be introduced)  
- **Company Background:** {company_name}, {company_mission} (brand identity and positioning)  
- **Contact Information:** {media_contact_name}, {media_contact_email}, {media_contact_phone} (for follow-up inquiries)

**Instructions:**  
1. Open with a strong headline that captures attention and summarizes the announcement.  
2. Write an engaging introductory paragraph that clearly states the product launch news.  
3. Elaborate on the product’s unique selling points and benefits in the body paragraphs.  
4. Include relevant launch event details and any calls to action for media or consumers.  
5. Conclude with a brief company overview and provide accurate contact information for media inquiries.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline, dateline, body, boilerplate, and contact section  
- Style: Clear, concise, formal, and persuasive tone suitable for journalists and industry professionals  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to contact details, ensuring readability and engagement

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution, formatted with appropriate sections: headline, dateline, introduction, body, boilerplate, and media contact information.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for PR professionals, marketers, and content creators preparing to announce a new product launch. It helps produce a high-quality press release that can be shared with media outlets to maximize exposure and generate buzz around the product.
