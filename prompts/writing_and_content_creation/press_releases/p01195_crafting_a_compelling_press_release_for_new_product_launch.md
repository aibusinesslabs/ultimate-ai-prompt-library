### P-01195 - Crafting a Compelling Press Release for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `content writing` `public relations`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a clear, engaging press release announcing a new product launch to generate media interest and inform the target audience.

**Context:**  
You are a professional PR writer tasked with creating an effective press release for a company unveiling its latest product. The goal is to inform journalists, stakeholders, and potential customers with relevant details that highlight the productâ€™s unique benefits and launch information.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description} (name and brief overview of the product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (company name and a short background)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {availability} (date of launch and where/how the product will be available)  
- **Key Benefits:** {unique_features}, {target_audience} (main selling points and intended users)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that grabs attention and includes the product name.  
2. Write an introductory paragraph summarizing the product launch and its significance.  
3. Provide detailed information about the product features and benefits tailored to the target audience.  
4. Include company background to establish credibility.  
5. End with launch details, availability, and a call-to-action for media or customers.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Professional press release structure (headline, dateline, body paragraphs, contact info optional)  
- Style: Clear, concise, and persuasive with a formal tone  
- Structure: Logical flow from introduction to detailed content, ending with launch specifics

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution, formatted with a headline and body paragraphs within the specified word count.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing a press release for a new product launch to ensure clear communication and media engagement. Ideal for PR teams, marketing professionals, and small business owners seeking professional-quality announcements without extensive writing experience.
