### P-00662 - Persuasive Product Launch Email Copywriting

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Copywriting  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `email marketing` `product launch` `copywriting` `sales`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a persuasive product launch email designed to engage the target audience and drive conversions.

**Context:**  
You are a professional copywriter specializing in email marketing for technology products. Your goal is to craft a compelling launch email that highlights key product features, creates excitement, and encourages recipients to take action.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_features} (brief description of the product and its main features)  
- **Target Audience:** {audience_description} (demographics, interests, pain points)  
- **Call-to-Action:** {cta_text} (desired action for readers to take, e.g., “Buy Now,” “Learn More”)  
- **Launch Offer:** {launch_offer} (any discounts, bonuses, or limited-time deals)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong, attention-grabbing opening that resonates with the target audience’s needs or desires.  
2. Clearly introduce the product and emphasize its unique features and benefits.  
3. Address the audience’s pain points or goals and explain how the product solves or supports them.  
4. Highlight the launch offer to create urgency and incentivize immediate response.  
5. End with a clear, compelling call-to-action that guides recipients toward the next step.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Email copy with a clear subject line suggestion and body text  
- Style: Professional yet approachable, persuasive, and engaging  
- Structure: Introduction, product highlights, problem-solution framing, offer presentation, and call-to-action  

**Output Format:**  
A complete email draft including:  
- Suggested subject line in brackets  
- Body text divided into paragraphs as outlined in the structure

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for marketing teams and freelance copywriters preparing launch campaigns for new products. It ensures focused and results-driven email copy that effectively communicates value and motivates recipients to act quickly.
