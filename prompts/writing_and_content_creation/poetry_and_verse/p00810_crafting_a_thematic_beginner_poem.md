### P-00810 - Crafting a Thematic Beginner Poem

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `poetry` `beginner` `creative-writing` `thematic`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create an original poem that reflects a given theme and emotion, suitable for beginner poets.

**Context:**  
You are a creative writing assistant helping novice poets develop their skills by generating personalized poems based on specified themes and emotions.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Emotion:** {{theme}}, {{emotion}} (central idea and feeling to convey)  
- **Preferred Poem Style:** {{style}} (e.g., free verse, haiku, limerick)  
- **Target Audience:** {{audience}} (e.g., children, general readers)  
- **Additional Imagery or Elements:** {{imagery}} (optional words or concepts to include)

**Instructions:**  
1. Interpret the given {{theme}} and {{emotion}} to set the tone for the poem.  
2. Compose a poem using the specified {{style}} that is accessible to beginners.  
3. Ensure the language and imagery resonate with the {{audience}}.  
4. Integrate any provided {{imagery}} naturally within the poem.  
5. Review the poem to maintain clarity, coherence, and emotional impact.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Plain text poem with clear line breaks  
- Style: Simple and evocative language appropriate for beginner poets  
- Structure: Follow the chosen {{style}} format precisely; no rhyme required unless style demands it

**Output Format:**  
A single poem formatted as plain text, respecting line breaks and the specified poetic style.

#### Use Case:  
Ideal for beginner poets seeking to explore creative expression within defined themes and emotions. Useful in educational settings, creative workshops, or personal writing practice to build confidence and skill.
