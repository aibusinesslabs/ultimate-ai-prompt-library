### P-00134 - Crafting a Compelling Short Film Script

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `screenwriting` `shortfilm` `scriptwriting` `creativewriting`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a concise and engaging short film script that clearly presents a central conflict and resolution within a limited timeframe.

**Context:**  
You are a novice screenwriter tasked with drafting a short film script. The script should be suitable for a beginner-level writer aiming to develop foundational storytelling and formatting skills for screenwriting.

**Input:**  
- **Characters and Setting:** {{main_character}}, {{supporting_character}}, {{location}} (names and primary setting details)  
- **Plot Elements:** {{central_conflict}}, {{resolution}} (brief description of the main problem and how it is resolved)  
- **Tone and Genre:** {{tone}}, {{genre}} (desired emotional tone and genre of the script)  
- **Duration:** {{approximate_length}} (target length of the short film in minutes)

**Instructions:**  
1. Introduce the main and supporting characters quickly and establish the setting within the first scene.  
2. Clearly present the central conflict in an engaging way that drives the story forward.  
3. Develop the plot with at least two scenes that build tension or deepen character interaction.  
4. Resolve the conflict in a satisfying manner by the end of the script.  
5. Format the script using standard screenplay conventions (scene headings, character names, dialogue, and action descriptions).

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Proper screenplay format for short films  
- Style: Clear, concise, beginner-friendly, and engaging  
- Structure: At least three scenes (including introduction, conflict development, and resolution)

**Output Format:**  
A fully formatted short film script draft composed of scene headings, character dialogues, and action lines, within the specified word range.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for beginner writers seeking to practice short film scriptwriting by focusing on character-driven conflict and resolution. It supports film students, aspiring screenwriters, and creative writing enthusiasts developing foundational skills in script formatting and storytelling.
