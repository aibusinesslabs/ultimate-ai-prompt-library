### P-00805 - Crafting Impactful Press Releases for Product Launches

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a professional and compelling press release announcing a new product launch that effectively communicates key information and generates media interest.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned PR specialist tasked with drafting a press release for a company launching a new product. The release must highlight the productâ€™s unique features, company background, and relevant launch details to attract journalists, potential customers, and industry stakeholders.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {{product_name}}, {{product_features}} (name and key features of the product)  
- **Company Information:** {{company_name}}, {{company_background}} (company name and brief background)  
- **Launch Details:** {{launch_date}}, {{launch_event_info}} (date of launch and any related event information)  
- **Contact Information:** {{contact_name}}, {{contact_email}}, {{contact_phone}} (PR contact person details)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong, engaging headline that includes the product name and its main benefit.  
2. Write an introductory paragraph summarizing the product launch, including the launch date and company name.  
3. Elaborate on the product features and explain what sets it apart from competitors.  
4. Include a brief background of the company to establish credibility and context.  
5. Provide details about the launch event if applicable, and end with clear contact information for media inquiries.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Formal press release layout with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and contact info  
- Style: Professional, clear, and persuasive tone suitable for media distribution  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to conclusion, using short paragraphs and concise sentences

**Output Format:**  
A complete, formatted press release text ready for distribution to media outlets, adhering to standard press release conventions.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when launching a new product to craft polished press releases that attract media coverage and generate public interest. Ideal for PR professionals, marketing teams, and content creators seeking to deliver concise and impactful announcements.
