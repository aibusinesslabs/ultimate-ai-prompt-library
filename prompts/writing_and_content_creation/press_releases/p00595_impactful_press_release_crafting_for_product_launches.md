### P-00595 - Impactful Press Release Crafting for Product Launches

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a compelling and professional press release announcing a new product launch that effectively communicates key information and generates media interest.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled communications specialist tasked with creating a press release for a company launching a new product. The release should clearly present the product’s features, benefits, and launch details while maintaining a tone that is informative and engaging for media outlets and potential customers.

**Input:**  
- **Company and Product Details:** {company_name}, {product_name} (name of the organization and the new product being launched)  
- **Product Features and Benefits:** {product_features}, {product_benefits} (key attributes of the product and how it adds value)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {availability} (official release date and where/how customers can purchase or access the product)  
- **Quotes and Contact Information:** {executive_quote}, {media_contact} (a quote from a company representative and contact details for press inquiries)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that highlights the product launch in a clear and engaging manner.  
2. Write a concise opening paragraph summarizing the announcement, including the product name, company, and launch date.  
3. Detail the product’s main features and benefits in the body, explaining why it stands out in the market.  
4. Incorporate the executive quote to add a human perspective and credibility to the announcement.  
5. Conclude with launch availability details and provide clear media contact information for follow-up questions.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and contact info  
- Style: Professional, clear, and engaging; avoid jargon and overly technical language  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to contact info, maintaining coherence and readability

**Output Format:**  
A formatted press release text including headline, dateline, body paragraphs, executive quote, and media contact section.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing official communications to announce new product launches to journalists, media outlets, and the public. It benefits PR professionals, marketing teams, and startups aiming to maximize visibility and media coverage for their new offerings. Ideal for crafting polished press releases that balance information and promotional appeal.
