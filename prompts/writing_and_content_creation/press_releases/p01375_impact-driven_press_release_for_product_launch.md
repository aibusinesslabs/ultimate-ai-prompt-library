### P-01375 - Impact-Driven Press Release for Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `media relations` `branding`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a compelling, professional press release announcing a new product launch that effectively communicates the product’s unique value, company vision, and market impact, targeting media outlets and industry stakeholders.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled corporate communications specialist tasked with drafting a high-impact press release for a company unveiling its latest product. Your goal is to craft a document that aligns with journalistic standards, maximizes media pick-up potential, and enhances brand reputation in a competitive market.

**Input:**  
- **Company & Product Details:** {company_name}, {product_name}, {product_category}, {launch_date} (basic information about the company and the product being launched)  
- **Key Features & Benefits:** {feature_list}, {unique_selling_points}, {target_audience} (detailed product attributes and advantages for the intended customers)  
- **Quotes & Statements:** {ceo_quote}, {customer_testimonial} (authentic and persuasive quotes from leadership and early adopters)  
- **Additional Information:** {pricing}, {availability}, {distribution_channels}, {contact_info} (logistical details and media contact information)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Open with a strong headline that highlights the product launch and its significance.  
2. Write a compelling introductory paragraph summarizing the news and capturing reader interest.  
3. Elaborate on the product’s key features and benefits, emphasizing what differentiates it in the market.  
4. Incorporate relevant quotes to add credibility and human interest, maintaining a professional tone.  
5. Provide details on pricing, availability, and distribution channels to inform buyers and media.  
6. Include a boilerplate section about the company’s mission and background to establish context.  
7. Conclude with clear media contact information for follow-up inquiries.  
8. Ensure the press release maintains clarity, journalistic integrity, and a persuasive narrative flow.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline, dateline, body, boilerplate, and contact sections  
- Style: Formal, concise, and engaging; suitable for journalists and industry professionals  
- Structure: Organized into clear paragraphs, using active voice and avoiding jargon without explanation  

**Output Format:**  
A fully formatted press release text ready for immediate distribution, including headline, dateline, body copy, boilerplate, and media contact information.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing to announce a new product to the public and media, ensuring your messaging is clear, persuasive, and professional. It benefits PR professionals, marketing teams, and startup founders aiming to maximize visibility and credibility during critical launch phases.
