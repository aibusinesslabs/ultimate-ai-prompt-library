### P-01408 - Professional Follow-Up Email Drafting

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Email Content  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `email` `follow-up` `professional` `communication`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Draft a clear and professional follow-up email to a contact after a recent meeting or call.

**Context:**  
You are a professional looking to maintain engagement with a client or colleague by sending a polite and concise follow-up email that reinforces key points and encourages next steps.

**Input:**  
- **Recipient Information:** {recipient_name}, {recipient_position} (name and role of the recipient)  
- **Meeting Details:** {meeting_date}, {meeting_topic} (date and subject of the recent interaction)  
- **Sender Information:** {sender_name}, {sender_position} (your name and professional title)  
- **Call to Action:** {next_step} (desired next action or request to include)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a polite greeting addressing the recipient by name.  
2. Reference the recent meeting or call by mentioning the date and topic.  
3. Summarize key points or mutual agreements discussed during the interaction.  
4. Clearly state the next step or action you would like the recipient to take.  
5. Close the email professionally with your name and position.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Professional email format (greeting, body, closing)  
- Style: Polite, concise, and professional tone  
- Structure: Introduction, summary, call to action, and closing  

**Output Format:**  
A polished, ready-to-send follow-up email text tailored to the provided inputs.  

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when you need to send a courteous follow-up after meetings or calls to nurture professional relationships and ensure clarity on next steps. Ideal for business development, client management, and networking communications.
