### P-00685 - Strategic Press Release Composition for Product Launch Success

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content strategy`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Craft a compelling and strategically structured press release announcing a new product launch, designed to capture media attention and engage target audiences effectively.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned PR specialist tasked with producing a polished press release for a company unveiling its latest product. The release must highlight key product features, company insights, and market relevance while maintaining journalistic appeal and brand voice consistency.

**Input:**  
- **Product & Company Details:** {product_name}, {product_description}, {company_name}, {company_background} (core information about the product and its maker)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_event_details} (timing and context of the launch)  
- **Target Audience & Market Positioning:** {target_audience}, {unique_selling_points} (intended customers and differentiators)  
- **Quotes & Contact Info:** {executive_quote}, {media_contact_name}, {media_contact_email}, {media_contact_phone} (key spokesperson statement and media liaison contacts)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a captivating headline that clearly states the product launch and its significance.  
2. Write an engaging opening paragraph summarizing the who, what, when, where, and why of the launch.  
3. Elaborate on the productâ€™s features, benefits, and how it stands out in the market, integrating relevant company background to build credibility.  
4. Include a compelling quote from a company executive to add authoritative insight and humanize the announcement.  
5. Conclude with event details, call-to-action for media engagement, and provide clear media contact information.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, quote section, and boilerplate  
- Style: Formal, concise, persuasive, and media-friendly tone  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to conclusion, ensuring clarity and journalistic standards

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution, including headline, dateline, body content, executive quote, boilerplate, and media contact block.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing professional press releases for new product launches to ensure comprehensive coverage of essential details and maximize media pickup. Ideal for PR teams, marketing professionals, and communications consultants aiming to create high-impact announcements that resonate with journalists and target customers alike.
