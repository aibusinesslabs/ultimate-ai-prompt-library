### P-01334 - Crafting a Compelling Feature Film Script Outline

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `screenwriting` `film` `storytelling` `scriptoutline`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Develop a detailed and engaging feature film script outline that clearly defines the narrative structure, key plot points, character arcs, and thematic elements to guide the screenplay writing process.

**Context:**  
You are an experienced professional screenwriter tasked with creating a comprehensive outline for a feature-length film. Your outline should serve as a blueprint that balances cinematic storytelling with strong character development and thematic depth tailored for an advanced screenwriting project.

**Input:**  
- **Film Concept and Genre**: {film_title}, {genre} (Title of the film and its primary genre)  
- **Main Characters and Arcs**: {protagonist_name}, {antagonist_name}, {supporting_characters} (Key characters with brief descriptions and intended character arcs)  
- **Setting and Time Period**: {setting_description}, {time_period} (Primary location(s) and era where the story takes place)  
- **Core Themes and Tone**: {themes}, {tone} (Underlying messages and mood/style of the film)

**Instructions:**  
1. Summarize the film’s premise in one compelling sentence that hooks the reader.  
2. Outline the three-act structure, detailing the inciting incident, major turning points, climax, and resolution.  
3. Define each main character’s journey, highlighting their motivations, conflicts, and transformation throughout the story.  
4. Integrate the setting and time period to enhance narrative context and mood, specifying how these elements influence plot and character decisions.  
5. Incorporate the core themes and tone consistently across the outline to ensure a cohesive and impactful storytelling experience.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Structured outline with clear headings for each act and character arc  
- Style: Professional, concise, and evocative, suitable for industry stakeholders  
- Structure: Logical flow following the three-act screenplay paradigm with explicit emphasis on narrative beats and character development

**Output Format:**  
A well-organized feature film script outline presented as a multi-section document, including:  
- Film Premise  
- Act I: Setup  
- Act II: Confrontation  
- Act III: Resolution  
- Character Arcs Summary  
- Themes and Tone Integration

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for screenwriters, filmmakers, and content creators who need to develop a strong foundational outline before drafting a full screenplay. It benefits professionals seeking a structured and creative framework to pitch, plan, or refine feature-length narratives effectively.
