### P-00059 - Crafting a Compelling Feature Film Script Outline

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `screenplay` `feature_film` `story_structure` `character_development`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a detailed, professional outline for a feature-length film screenplay that integrates complex character arcs, a three-act structure, and thematic depth.

**Context:**  
You are an experienced screenwriter developing a feature film script outline intended for pitch presentations or first draft development. The outline should clearly define key plot points, character motivations, conflicts, and resolutions while maintaining narrative coherence and thematic resonance.

**Input:**  
- **Story Details:** {genre}, {setting}, {primary_theme} (filmâ€™s genre, location/timeframe, and central theme)  
- **Characters:** {protagonist_description}, {antagonist_description}, {supporting_characters} (brief profiles including motivations and key traits)  
- **Plot Elements:** {inciting_incident}, {major_conflicts}, {climax} (core narrative drivers and pivotal moments)  
- **Tone & Style:** {desired_tone}, {narrative_style} (emotional tone and storytelling approach, e.g., dark, comedic, nonlinear)

**Instructions:**  
1. Outline the screenplay using a classic three-act structure: setup, confrontation, and resolution.  
2. Define clear character arcs for the protagonist and antagonist, showing their development through the acts.  
3. Incorporate the inciting incident early to propel the story and establish stakes.  
4. Map out major conflicts and turning points that escalate tension leading to a climax.  
5. Emphasize the central theme throughout the outline, ensuring it is reflected in plot and character decisions.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Structured outline with bullet points or numbered acts and scenes  
- Style: Professional, concise, and engaging with clear narrative logic  
- Structure: Must follow three-act screenplay framework and include character arcs and thematic integration

**Output Format:**  
A structured screenplay outline divided into three acts, with bullet points detailing key scenes, character developments, conflicts, and thematic notes.

#### Use Case:  
Ideal for screenwriters seeking to develop or pitch a feature film concept, providing a clear blueprint for scripting. This prompt benefits writers aiming to organize complex story elements cohesively while emphasizing character and theme for a compelling narrative foundation.
