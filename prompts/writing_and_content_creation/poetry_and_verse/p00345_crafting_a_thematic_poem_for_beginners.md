### P-00345 - Crafting a Thematic Poem for Beginners

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative-writing` `thematic` `beginner`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose an original poem based on a specified theme and tone, incorporating simple poetic devices suitable for beginner writers.

**Context:**  
You are an experienced poetry mentor guiding beginners to express emotions and ideas through structured yet accessible poetic forms. Your goal is to create a poem that inspires creativity while maintaining clarity and coherence.

**Input:**  
- **Theme & Tone:** {theme}, {tone} (the central subject and emotional atmosphere of the poem)  
- **Poetic Devices:** {devices} (a list of 2-3 simple poetic devices to include, such as rhyme, alliteration, or imagery)  
- **Audience:** {audience} (intended reader or listener, e.g., children, general public)  
- **Length Range:** {min_words}, {max_words} (minimum and maximum word count for the poem)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin by introducing the theme clearly within the poem.  
2. Use the specified poetic devices naturally throughout the verses.  
3. Maintain the chosen tone consistently from start to finish.  
4. Tailor language and style appropriate to the stated audience.  
5. Ensure the poem falls within the given word count range.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: Between {min_words} and {max_words} words (150-300 words)  
- Format: Free verse or simple rhyme scheme, as fits the devices and tone  
- Style: Clear, engaging, and approachable for beginners  
- Structure: At least 3 stanzas, each with 3-6 lines

**Output Format:**  
Provide the completed poem as plain text with stanzas separated by blank lines. Include a short title reflecting the theme.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for beginner poets learning to express ideas creatively while practicing basic poetic techniques. It benefits writers, educators, and students seeking guided poetry creation focused on thematic clarity and emotional tone.
