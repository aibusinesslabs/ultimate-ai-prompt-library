### P-00014 - Crafting a Compelling Feature Film Script Outline

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `screenwriting` `film` `storytelling` `script_outline`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Develop a detailed, professional feature film script outline that clearly establishes the narrative arc, character development, and key scenes to guide full scriptwriting.

**Context:**  
You are an expert screenwriter tasked with creating a comprehensive outline for a feature-length film. The outline should reflect strong storytelling principles, including well-defined acts, character motivations, and pivotal plot points, to assist screenwriters and producers in visualizing the story’s flow and emotional beats.

**Input:**  
- **Story Details:** {{genre}}, {{theme}}, {{setting}} (the film’s genre, core theme, and primary setting)  
- **Main Characters:** {{protagonist}}, {{antagonist}}, {{supporting_characters}} (brief descriptions and roles of principal characters)  
- **Plot Points:** {{inciting_incident}}, {{climax}}, {{resolution}} (key turning points driving the story’s progression)  
- **Tone & Style:** {{tone}}, {{style}} (desired mood and narrative style, e.g., dark, comedic, nonlinear)

**Instructions:**  
1. Outline the story structure using the three-act format: Setup, Confrontation, and Resolution.  
2. Define the protagonist’s goals, conflicts, and evolution throughout the narrative.  
3. Incorporate the antagonist’s role and how their actions create obstacles.  
4. Highlight major plot points including inciting incident, midpoint twists, climax, and resolution.  
5. Describe key scenes and sequences that advance the plot and develop characters, ensuring alignment with the chosen tone and style.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Clear, numbered sections with headings for each act and character  
- Style: Professional, concise, engaging, and suitable for industry use  
- Structure: Logical flow that can be used as a blueprint for full script development  

**Output Format:**  
A well-organized, detailed feature film script outline divided into three acts, including character arcs, key plot points, and scene descriptions formatted for easy reference by screenwriters and producers.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when developing a new feature film concept to create a comprehensive narrative framework before writing the full script. It benefits screenwriters, producers, and creative teams seeking a clear, actionable story roadmap to facilitate collaboration and streamline the production process.
