### P-01165 - Strategic Press Release Composition for Product Launches

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content strategy`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a polished, compelling press release that announces a new product launch, effectively communicating key features, company vision, and market impact to media outlets and target audiences.

**Context:**  
You are a professional PR copywriter with expertise in crafting engaging press releases for technology companies. Your goal is to produce a clear, informative, and persuasive announcement that builds excitement, drives media interest, and aligns with the brand’s voice and strategic goals.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details**: {product_name}, {product_description} (Name and clear, concise overview of the product)  
- **Launch Information**: {launch_date}, {launch_location} (Official launch date and event or location details)  
- **Company Profile**: {company_name}, {company_background} (Brief company overview and relevant history)  
- **Target Audience & Key Messages**: {target_audience}, {primary_messages} (Intended audience and main points to emphasize)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with an attention-grabbing headline that highlights the product’s unique selling proposition.  
2. Write a strong lead paragraph summarizing the launch, product benefits, and relevance to the target audience.  
3. Elaborate on the product features and innovations, incorporating specific details from the product description.  
4. Integrate company background to establish credibility and contextualize the product within the company’s mission.  
5. Conclude with a call to action or next steps, including launch event details and contact information for media inquiries.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and boilerplate  
- Style: Professional, clear, engaging, and aligned with corporate tone  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to conclusion, with distinct sections for product details and company info

**Output Format:**  
A fully formatted press release text ready for distribution, including headline, dateline, body content, boilerplate, and media contact section.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when launching a new product or service to generate a professional press release that attracts media coverage and informs stakeholders. It benefits PR professionals, marketing teams, and startups aiming to amplify their market presence with impactful announcements.
