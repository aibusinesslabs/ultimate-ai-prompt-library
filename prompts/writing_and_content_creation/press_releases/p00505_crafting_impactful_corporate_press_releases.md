### P-00505 - Crafting Impactful Corporate Press Releases

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `corporate communication` `public relations` `brand messaging`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a professional and engaging corporate press release that effectively announces a new product launch, ensuring clarity, credibility, and alignment with the company’s brand voice.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned PR specialist tasked with drafting a press release for a reputable company unveiling its latest product. The release should capture media interest, inform stakeholders, and promote positive brand perception.

**Input:**  
- **Company Details**: {company_name}, {industry}, {brand_tone} (The company’s name, operating industry, and preferred brand tone such as formal, friendly, or innovative)  
- **Product Information**: {product_name}, {product_description}, {key_features} (Name of the product, a brief overview, and three to five main features or benefits)  
- **Launch Details**: {launch_date}, {availability}, {pricing} (Official release date, initial market availability, and price points)  
- **Quotes & Contact**: {executive_quote}, {media_contact_info} (A quote from a company executive about the product and contact details for media inquiries)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a compelling headline that highlights the product launch.  
2. Write a concise introductory paragraph summarizing the announcement and its significance.  
3. Elaborate on the product description and key features in a clear, persuasive manner.  
4. Include the official launch details, emphasizing availability and pricing.  
5. Incorporate the executive quote to add authority and human interest.  
6. End with a call-to-action or next steps for readers and provide media contact information.  
7. Maintain consistent brand tone and professional language throughout.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and contact section  
- Style: Professional, clear, and engaging with no jargon or overly technical terms  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to conclusion, use short paragraphs and bullet points where appropriate  

**Output Format:**  
A fully formatted press release text ready for distribution, including headline, dateline, body, executive quote, and media contact information.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for PR professionals, marketing teams, and content creators preparing official announcements about new products. It ensures consistent messaging and maximizes media engagement during product launches or corporate communications.
