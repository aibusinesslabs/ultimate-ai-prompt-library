### P-00775 - Crafting a Compelling Press Release for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a clear and engaging press release announcing the launch of a new product, highlighting its key features and benefits to attract media attention and potential customers.

**Context:**  
You are a professional PR writer tasked with creating a press release that effectively communicates the value of a new product for a company’s marketing campaign.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_type}, {key_features} (name, category, and main features of the product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_mission} (company’s name and a brief mission statement)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {availability_channels} (official launch date and where the product will be available)  
- **Quotes:** {executive_name}, {executive_title}, {quote} (a short quote from a company executive about the product)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that includes the product name and the launch announcement.  
2. Introduce the company and its mission to provide context.  
3. Describe the new product, emphasizing its key features and benefits.  
4. Include the official launch date and availability information.  
5. Add a relevant and engaging executive quote to personalize the announcement.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and contact info placeholder  
- Style: Clear, concise, and engaging with a formal tone suitable for media distribution  
- Structure: Logical flow starting from headline to closing quote, no bullet points

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution, formatted with a headline, dateline, body, and ending with the executive quote.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for marketing teams and PR professionals who need to quickly generate effective press releases announcing new products. It helps create consistent, media-ready content that can boost visibility and attract customer interest during product launches.
