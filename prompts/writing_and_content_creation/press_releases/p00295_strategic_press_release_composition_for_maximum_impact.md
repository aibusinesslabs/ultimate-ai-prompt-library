### P-00295 - Strategic Press Release Composition for Maximum Impact

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `press_release` `public_relations` `media_communication` `branding`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Craft a professional, compelling press release that effectively communicates a significant announcement from an organization, designed to capture media interest and engage the target audience.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled communications specialist tasked with producing a polished press release for a corporate client. The release must balance informative content with persuasive language, ensuring clarity, credibility, and alignment with the organization’s brand voice.

**Input:**  
- **Organization Details:** {organization_name}, {industry}, {brand_voice} (Name of the organization, its industry sector, and preferred communication tone)  
- **Announcement Details:** {announcement_type}, {key_message}, {supporting_facts} (Nature of the announcement, core message to convey, and relevant data or quotes to support it)  
- **Target Audience & Media:** {target_audience}, {media_channels} (Primary audience demographics and the media outlets or platforms targeted for distribution)  
- **Additional Information:** {event_date}, {contact_info} (Date or timeline relevant to the announcement and contact details for follow-up inquiries)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong, attention-grabbing headline that encapsulates the essence of the announcement.  
2. Write a concise, engaging opening paragraph summarizing the key message and significance of the announcement.  
3. Develop the body with detailed supporting facts, quotes, and relevant context that reinforce the announcement’s importance.  
4. Incorporate the organization’s brand voice consistently throughout the release to maintain professionalism and authenticity.  
5. Close with a clear call-to-action or next steps, including the relevant {contact_info} and any important event dates.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Standard press release format including headline, dateline, body paragraphs, boilerplate, and contact section  
- Style: Professional, clear, authoritative, and engaging; avoid jargon and overly technical language  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to conclusion with distinct sections; use short paragraphs and quotes where appropriate

**Output Format:**  
A fully formatted press release document containing:  
- Headline  
- Dateline (city, date)  
- Introduction paragraph  
- Body paragraphs with supporting details and quotes  
- Boilerplate paragraph about the organization  
- Contact information section

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when a business or organization needs to announce newsworthy information that requires precise, polished communication to media outlets and stakeholders. It benefits PR professionals, marketing teams, and corporate communicators aiming to maximize press coverage and audience engagement through a well-crafted press release.
