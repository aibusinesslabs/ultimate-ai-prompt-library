### P-01060 - Crafting a Compelling Product Launch Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional and engaging press release announcing a new product launch.

**Context:**  
You are a PR specialist tasked with creating a press release that highlights the key features and benefits of a new product to generate media interest and attract potential customers.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_type} (name and category of the product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_location} (official launch date and event/location if applicable)  
- **Key Features:** {feature_list} (list of the product’s main features or selling points)  
- **Company Background:** {company_name}, {company_mission} (the company’s name and a brief mission statement or tagline)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline announcing the product launch.  
2. Write an engaging opening paragraph that includes the product name, launch date, and location.  
3. Highlight 3-4 key features or benefits of the product in a clear and persuasive manner.  
4. Include a brief paragraph about the company and its mission to build credibility.  
5. Close with a call to action or information on where readers can find more details.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Professional press release style with a clear headline and structured paragraphs  
- Style: Formal yet accessible language suitable for media outlets and potential customers  
- Structure: Headline, introduction, features, company info, closing statement  

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution, including headline and body copy.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when needing to create clear, concise, and professional press releases for new product launches. Ideal for PR teams, marketing professionals, and small business owners looking to announce products effectively to the media and public.
