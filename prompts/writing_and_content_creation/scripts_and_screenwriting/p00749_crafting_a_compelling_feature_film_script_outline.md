### P-00749 - Crafting a Compelling Feature Film Script Outline

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `screenwriting` `feature_film` `story_structure` `character_development`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Develop a detailed and compelling feature film script outline that incorporates a strong three-act structure, well-defined characters, and thematic depth.

**Context:**  
You are an experienced screenwriter and story consultant tasked with creating a professional script outline for a feature-length film. The outline should serve as a blueprint that guides the full screenplay development, balancing narrative pacing, character arcs, and thematic elements.

**Input:**  
- **Story Overview and Genre:** {{title}}, {{genre}}, {{logline}} (A brief description of the film concept and its primary genre)  
- **Main Characters:** {{protagonist}}, {{antagonist}}, {{supporting_characters}} (Character names, roles, and brief personality traits)  
- **Setting and Tone:** {{setting}}, {{time_period}}, {{tone}} (Description of the storyâ€™s world, era, and overall mood)  
- **Themes and Key Plot Points:** {{themes}}, {{major_conflicts}}, {{climax}}, {{resolution}} (Core thematic messages and major narrative beats)

**Instructions:**  
1. Create a clear and concise three-act structure breakdown, identifying major plot points and their placement within each act.  
2. Develop detailed character arcs for the protagonist and antagonist, illustrating their growth and motivations across the story.  
3. Integrate the setting and tone consistently throughout the outline to enhance atmosphere and narrative immersion.  
4. Emphasize the thematic elements by linking them to character decisions and plot developments.  
5. Format the outline with headings for each act, sections for key scenes or sequences, and bullet points for important narrative details.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Professional script outline format with clear headings and bullet points  
- Style: Formal, narrative-driven, and engaging with emphasis on clarity and cohesion  
- Structure: Three-act structure with explicit inclusion of character arcs, themes, and major plot points

**Output Format:**  
A structured feature film script outline document, organized by acts and scenes, including character arc summaries, thematic integration, and key plot details presented in a professional and readable format.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when developing initial script treatments or preparing pitch materials for feature films. It benefits screenwriters, script consultants, and creative producers who need a detailed and structured outline to guide screenplay writing or collaborative story development. Ideal for projects in development stages requiring clarity and depth in story planning.
