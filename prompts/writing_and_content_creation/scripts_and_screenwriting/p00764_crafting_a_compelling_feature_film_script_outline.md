### P-00764 - Crafting a Compelling Feature Film Script Outline

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `screenwriting` `script_outline` `feature_film` `story_structure`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Develop a detailed, professional outline for a feature film script that effectively establishes the story arc, character development, and key plot points, serving as a strong foundation for full screenplay writing.

**Context:**  
You are an experienced screenwriter tasked with creating a comprehensive feature film script outline. The outline must reflect mastery of classic and contemporary storytelling techniques, balancing narrative pacing, character motivation, and thematic depth suitable for a cinematic production.

**Input:**  
- **Film Details:** {film_title}, {genre}, {setting} (Basic information about the film’s title, primary genre, and the story’s setting)  
- **Main Characters:** {protagonist_description}, {antagonist_description}, {supporting_characters_brief} (Descriptions of the main hero, villain, and relevant supporting cast)  
- **Central Conflict and Theme:** {central_conflict}, {primary_theme} (The main conflict driving the story and its underlying thematic message)  
- **Tone and Style Preferences:** {desired_tone}, {narrative_style} (The emotional tone and storytelling style preferred for the script)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a logline that succinctly captures the essence of the film’s story and stakes.  
2. Outline the three-act structure clearly, detailing key plot points such as inciting incident, midpoint, climax, and resolution.  
3. Define each main character’s arc, motivations, and transformations throughout the story.  
4. Integrate the central conflict and theme consistently across acts, ensuring they resonate through major events and character decisions.  
5. Incorporate the specified tone and narrative style in the outline’s descriptions and pacing notes to guide the screenplay’s development.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Use clear headings and bullet points for structure; maintain professional screenplay development terminology  
- Style: Formal, concise, cinematic, and engaging  
- Structure: Follow a logical progression from general story overview to detailed act breakdown, character arcs, and thematic integration

**Output Format:**  
A structured feature film script outline document including:  
- Logline (1-2 sentences)  
- Three-act breakdown with key scenes and plot points  
- Character arcs summary for protagonist, antagonist, and main supporting roles  
- Explanation of how the central conflict and theme are woven into the narrative  
- Notes on tone and narrative style application throughout the story

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for professional screenwriters, filmmakers, and content creators preparing to develop a full-length screenplay. It helps translate raw story concepts into a coherent and compelling narrative framework, ensuring strong storytelling foundations before scriptwriting begins.
