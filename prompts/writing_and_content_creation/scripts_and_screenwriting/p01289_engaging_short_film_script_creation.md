### P-01289 - Engaging Short Film Script Creation

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `screenwriting` `shortfilm` `creativewriting` `dialogue`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a concise and engaging short film script based on the provided scenario and characters.

**Context:**  
You are a beginner screenwriter tasked with creating a compelling short film script that introduces characters, develops a simple conflict, and concludes with a meaningful resolution.

**Input:**  
- **Scenario & Setting:** {scenario_description}, {setting_description} (brief description of the story premise and location)  
- **Characters:** {character_names}, {character_traits} (names and key personality traits of main characters)  
- **Conflict:** {conflict_description} (central problem or challenge driving the story)  
- **Tone & Style:** {tone_description} (desired mood, e.g., humorous, dramatic, inspirational)

**Instructions:**  
1. Create a script outline including a beginning, middle, and end.  
2. Develop natural dialogue that reflects each characterâ€™s personality.  
3. Incorporate the conflict clearly and show its impact on the characters.  
4. Resolve the conflict in a way that fits the tone and provides closure.  
5. Ensure the script length is appropriate for a 5-minute short film.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Script format including character names and dialogue lines  
- Style: Clear, engaging, and accessible for beginner screenwriters  
- Structure: Introduction, conflict development, climax, and resolution

**Output Format:**  
Provide a formatted short film script with character names in uppercase followed by dialogue, stage directions in parentheses, and clear scene indications.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for beginners or creative writing students looking to practice short scriptwriting. It helps generate a structured, engaging screenplay for short films, class projects, or story pitching.
