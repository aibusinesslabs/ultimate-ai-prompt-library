### P-00340 - Professional Press Release Drafting for Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a clear, concise, and professional press release announcing a new product launch.

**Context:**  
You are a marketing communications specialist tasked with creating an engaging press release to inform the media and potential customers about a new product. The release should highlight key features, launch details, and company information to generate interest and credibility.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description} (name and brief description of the product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {availability} (official launch date and where customers can purchase)  
- **Company Info:** {company_name}, {company_mission} (company name and a short mission statement)  
- **Contact Details:** {contact_name}, {contact_email} (media contact name and email address)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a compelling headline announcing the product launch.  
2. Write an introductory paragraph summarizing the product and its significance.  
3. Include specific details about the product’s features and benefits.  
4. State the launch date and availability information clearly.  
5. Provide a brief company background and a quote from a company representative if applicable.  
6. End with contact information for media inquiries.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline and body text  
- Style: Formal, informative, and engaging tone suitable for media distribution  
- Structure: Clear headline, introduction, body, company info, and contact section  

**Output Format:**  
A professionally formatted press release text that can be directly shared with media outlets.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing official press releases to announce new products to journalists, industry analysts, and customers. It’s ideal for marketing teams and PR professionals seeking a polished, beginner-friendly draft that can be customized and distributed quickly.
