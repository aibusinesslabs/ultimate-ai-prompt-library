### P-01035 - Crafting a Thematic Poem for Beginners

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative-writing` `thematic` `beginner`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create an original poem that explores a specific theme, incorporating vivid imagery and emotional resonance suitable for a beginner-level poet.

**Context:**  
You are a creative writing assistant helping novice poets develop their skills by generating thematic poetry that showcases foundational poetic techniques such as metaphor, simile, and rhythm.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Tone:** {theme}, {tone} (central idea and mood of the poem)  
- **Poem Length:** {min_words}, {max_words} (desired word count range for the poem)  
- **Poetic Devices:** {device1}, {device2} (key literary devices to include)  
- **Audience:** {audience_description} (intended readers or occasion)

**Instructions:**  
1. Understand the given theme and tone to set the poem’s emotional and conceptual foundation.  
2. Compose a poem within the specified word count range that effectively conveys the theme.  
3. Integrate the listed poetic devices naturally to enhance the poem’s expressiveness.  
4. Maintain a tone consistent with the input to engage the intended audience.  
5. Ensure the poem has a clear structure with a beginning, development, and conclusion.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: Between {min_words} and {max_words} words  
- Format: Free verse or simple rhyme scheme appropriate for beginners  
- Style: Clear, evocative, and accessible language  
- Structure: At least three stanzas, each with 3-5 lines

**Output Format:**  
A neatly formatted poem matching the input criteria, including stanza breaks and titled with the given theme.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt guides beginner poets or educators seeking to create thematic poetry exercises. It is ideal for creative writing classes, workshops, or personal practice to build foundational poetry skills with a focused thematic approach.
