### P-00175 - Crafting Impactful Press Releases for Product Launches

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a professional and compelling press release announcing a new product launch that effectively highlights key features, benefits, and company information to attract media attention and engage the target audience.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned public relations specialist tasked with drafting a press release for a company launching a new product. The release should convey excitement, provide essential information, and follow industry-standard formatting to ensure clear communication and maximum impact.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {{product_name}}, {{product_features}}, {{unique_selling_points}} (key attributes and advantages of the product)  
- **Company Information:** {{company_name}}, {{company_background}} (brief overview of the company and its mission)  
- **Launch Information:** {{launch_date}}, {{availability}} (release date and how/where the product can be purchased)  
- **Contact Details:** {{media_contact_name}}, {{media_contact_email}}, {{media_contact_phone}} (information for press inquiries)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with an engaging headline that clearly states the product launch.  
2. Write an introductory paragraph summarizing the announcement and its significance.  
3. Detail the productâ€™s main features and unique selling points in subsequent paragraphs.  
4. Include relevant background about the company to build credibility and context.  
5. Conclude with launch date, availability details, and clear media contact information for follow-up.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Professional press release style with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and contact section  
- Style: Clear, concise, persuasive, and journalistic tone  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to conclusion with no jargon or overly technical language  

**Output Format:**  
A complete press release text document formatted with:  
- Headline (bold)  
- Dateline (city and date)  
- Body paragraphs  
- Contact information section  

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when you need to quickly generate a polished press release for new product launches, ensuring consistent messaging and professional presentation. Ideal for PR professionals, marketing teams, and startups preparing announcements for media distribution and public awareness.
