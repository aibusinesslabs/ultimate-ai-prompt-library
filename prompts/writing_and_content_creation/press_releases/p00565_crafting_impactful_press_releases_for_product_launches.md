### P-00565 - Crafting Impactful Press Releases for Product Launches

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `content writing` `public relations`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a concise and professional press release announcing a new product launch that captures attention and provides key information clearly.

**Context:**  
You are a public relations specialist tasked with creating an engaging press release for a company’s latest product. The goal is to inform media outlets and potential customers with a clear, persuasive message that highlights the product’s value and launch details.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_features} (name and key features of the product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (company name and brief background)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {availability} (official launch date and availability status)  
- **Contact Details:** {contact_person}, {contact_email} (media contact name and email address)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline featuring the product name and launch announcement.  
2. Write an engaging opening paragraph summarizing the product and its significance.  
3. Include details about the product’s features and benefits in the body.  
4. Provide information on the launch date and where the product will be available.  
5. End with the company background and contact information for media inquiries.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline, body paragraphs, and contact section  
- Style: Clear, formal, and persuasive language appropriate for media and public audiences  
- Structure: Headline, introduction, product details, launch info, company info, contact details  

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution to media outlets, formatted with a headline and well-structured paragraphs.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when launching a new product and needing a professional press release to share with journalists, bloggers, and customers. Ideal for PR teams, marketing professionals, and small business owners aiming to create clear and effective announcements.
