### P-01495 - Crafting a Compelling Company Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `public relations` `business communication` `writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a professional and engaging press release announcing a new product launch that effectively communicates key details and generates public interest.

**Context:**  
You are a corporate communications specialist tasked with drafting a press release that highlights the launch of a new product by a company. The press release should be clear, concise, and informative to attract media attention and inform potential customers.

**Input:**  
- **Company Details:** {company_name}, {industry} (name and industry sector of the company)  
- **Product Information:** {product_name}, {product_description} (name and brief description of the new product)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {availability} (official launch date and availability status)  
- **Contact Information:** {contact_person}, {contact_email} (media contact name and email address)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that includes the product name and the company.  
2. Write a concise introductory paragraph summarizing the launch and its significance.  
3. Detail the product features and benefits in the body paragraphs.  
4. Include launch and availability information clearly.  
5. End with a call-to-action and provide contact details for media inquiries.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Press release with headline, body, and contact section  
- Style: Professional, clear, and engaging tone suitable for a broad audience  
- Structure: Use short paragraphs and avoid jargon

**Output Format:**  
A complete press release text ready for distribution, formatted with a headline, body, and media contact section.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when you need to quickly generate a polished press release for announcing new products. It benefits marketing teams and PR professionals seeking to create clear, media-ready content that informs and attracts attention. Ideal for startups, small businesses, and corporate communications.
