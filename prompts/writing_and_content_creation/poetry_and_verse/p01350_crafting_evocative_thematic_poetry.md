### P-01350 - Crafting Evocative Thematic Poetry

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative_writing` `theme` `verse_structure`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a thematically rich and evocative poem that explores a specific subject or emotion, demonstrating creative use of language and poetic devices.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled poet tasked with creating a poem that captures a defined theme or emotion. Your goal is to engage readers through vivid imagery, thoughtful word choice, and a coherent structure that enhances the poem’s emotional impact.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Emotion:** {theme}, {emotion} (central subject and feeling to be conveyed)  
- **Tone and Style:** {tone}, {style} (desired mood and poetic style, e.g., melancholic, hopeful, free verse, sonnet)  
- **Key Imagery and Symbols:** {imagery}, {symbols} (specific images or symbols to include)  
- **Target Audience and Purpose:** {audience}, {purpose} (intended readers and the poem’s intended effect or message)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Identify and focus on the central theme and emotion provided.  
2. Incorporate the specified tone and style consistently throughout the poem.  
3. Weave the key imagery and symbols naturally into the poem to deepen the reader’s experience.  
4. Ensure the poem resonates with the target audience and fulfills the stated purpose.  
5. Edit for clarity, rhythm, and emotional resonance while adhering to the chosen poetic form.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300–500 words  
- Format: Use stanza breaks appropriate to the chosen poetic style  
- Style: Maintain the instructed tone and style consistently  
- Structure: Follow the specified poetic form or free verse as indicated  

**Output Format:**  
A polished, original poem formatted with stanza breaks, titled according to the theme or subject, suitable for presentation or publication.  

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for poets, educators, and creative writers seeking to produce meaningful poetry tailored to specific themes and emotions. It supports developing expressive writing skills and creating content for literary publications, workshops, or personal projects.
