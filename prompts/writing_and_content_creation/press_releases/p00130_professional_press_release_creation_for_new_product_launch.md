### P-00130 - Professional Press Release Creation for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `marketing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional and engaging press release announcing the launch of a new product, designed to capture media interest and clearly communicate key details.

**Context:**  
You are a public relations specialist tasked with crafting a concise press release for a company’s new product launch. The release should highlight the product’s unique features, target audience, and launch details in a clear, compelling manner suitable for distribution to media outlets.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description} (name and brief description)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (company name and relevant background)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {availability} (official launch date and availability information)  
- **Contact Information:** {contact_name}, {contact_email} (media contact person and email)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline incorporating the product name and launch event.  
2. Write an engaging introductory paragraph summarizing the announcement and its significance.  
3. Elaborate on the product’s key features and benefits aligned with the target audience.  
4. Include relevant company background to establish credibility and context.  
5. Provide launch date, availability details, and clear contact information for media inquiries.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150–300 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, body paragraphs, and contact info  
- Style: Professional, clear, and succinct language suitable for media distribution  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to introduction, product details, company info, and contact details

**Output Format:**  
A fully composed press release text ready for publication, formatted with a headline, body paragraphs, and a closing contact section.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for marketing teams or PR professionals who need to quickly generate polished press releases for new product launches. It ensures consistent messaging and professional presentation for media circulation and brand promotion.
