### P-00910 - Strategic Press Release Crafting for Product Launches

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `media outreach`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a compelling, professional press release announcing a new product launch that effectively communicates key features, market positioning, and company vision to attract media attention and drive customer interest.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned PR specialist writing on behalf of a technology company preparing to launch an innovative product. The goal is to generate buzz and secure media coverage by crafting a clear, engaging, and informative press release that aligns with the company’s brand voice and strategic marketing objectives.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_features} (name and primary innovative features of the product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_mission} (company’s name and overarching mission statement)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {target_audience} (official release date and intended customer demographics)  
- **Additional Context:** {market_positioning}, {key_quotes} (how the product is positioned in the market and relevant executive or expert quotes)

**Instructions:**  
1. Start with a strong, attention-grabbing headline that highlights the product’s uniqueness and appeal.  
2. Write a concise opening paragraph summarizing the product launch, its significance, and timing.  
3. Elaborate on the product’s key features and benefits, emphasizing innovation and customer value.  
4. Incorporate the company’s mission and market positioning to contextualize the product within the brand’s strategy.  
5. Include at least two relevant quotes from company executives or industry experts to add credibility and human interest.  
6. Clearly state the launch date and call to action, encouraging media and customer engagement.  
7. End with a brief company boilerplate providing background information and contact details for press inquiries.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, dateline, body copy, quotes, boilerplate, and contact section  
- Style: Formal, persuasive, and engaging tone suitable for media professionals and potential customers  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to closing, with clear segmentation of sections and professional language

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release document ready for distribution, formatted with a bold headline, dateline, well-structured paragraphs, quoted material, and a closing boilerplate section including contact information.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing to announce a new product launch to journalists, partners, and customers to maximize visibility and impact. Ideal for PR teams, marketing professionals, and brand managers seeking a high-quality, media-ready press release that highlights innovation and aligns with strategic messaging.
