### P-00715 - Crafting an Engaging Press Release for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional and engaging press release announcing a new product launch that captures media and customer attention.

**Context:**  
You are a PR specialist tasked with drafting a clear and compelling press release for a company's latest product. The goal is to highlight key features, benefits, and launch details while maintaining a professional tone suitable for journalists and potential customers.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_type}, {key_features} (name, category, and main features of the product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (company name and brief background)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {availability} (release date and where/how to purchase)  
- **Quotes and Contact:** {quote_from_executive}, {media_contact_info} (a short executive quote and media contact details)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline featuring the product name and launch announcement.  
2. Introduce the company and provide relevant background information.  
3. Describe the new product, emphasizing its unique features and benefits.  
4. Include the official launch date and availability information.  
5. Add a relevant quote from a company executive to add credibility and human interest.  
6. Conclude with media contact information for follow-up inquiries.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with clear paragraphs  
- Style: Formal, concise, and informative  
- Structure: Headline, introduction, product description, launch info, quote, closing statement with contact details  

**Output Format:**  
A complete press release text ready for distribution, formatted in standard press release style with a headline and organized paragraphs.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing press releases for new product launches to ensure clear communication of essential details. Ideal for PR professionals, marketing teams, and business owners seeking to attract media coverage and customer interest efficiently.
