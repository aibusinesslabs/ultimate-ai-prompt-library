### P-00462 - Engaging Landing Page Copy for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Landing Pages  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `landing page` `copywriting` `product launch` `marketing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a compelling and clear landing page copy that highlights the key benefits and features of a new product, designed to convert visitors into customers.

**Context:**  
You are a professional copywriter specializing in digital marketing and landing pages. Your goal is to craft persuasive text that appeals to the target audience, clearly communicates value, and encourages conversions.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_features} (name and main features of the product)  
- **Target Audience:** {audience_description} (demographics and pain points)  
- **Call to Action:** {cta_text} (primary action you want visitors to take)  
- **Brand Voice:** {tone_style} (e.g., friendly, professional, energetic)

**Instructions:**  
1. Write a captivating headline that immediately grabs attention.  
2. Introduce the product with a brief but engaging summary.  
3. Highlight 3-5 key features or benefits tailored to the target audienceâ€™s needs.  
4. Include a clear and persuasive call to action using the provided text.  
5. Keep the tone consistent with the specified brand voice throughout.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Continuous, web-ready copy without bullet points  
- Style: Conversational and approachable, suitable for beginners  
- Structure: Headline, introduction, features/benefits, call to action  

**Output Format:**  
A complete landing page copy text block ready for direct use on a webpage.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when launching a new product and you need professional, beginner-friendly landing page copy that converts. Ideal for marketers, entrepreneurs, and small business owners seeking effective web copy to increase sales and engagement.
