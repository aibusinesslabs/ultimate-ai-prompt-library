### P-01090 - Strategic Press Release Crafting for Major Announcements

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `press release` `public relations` `content strategy` `corporate communications`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a professional, engaging, and strategically structured press release announcing a major corporate event or development to maximize media pickup and stakeholder engagement.

**Context:**  
You are a senior communications specialist tasked with creating an impactful press release for a well-established company. The announcement involves critical information that needs precise messaging, clear benefits, and an authoritative tone to appeal to both journalists and the company’s target audience.

**Input:**  
- **Company Details & Announcement:** {company_name}, {event_description} (Name of the company and detailed description of the event or development)  
- **Key Messages & Quotes:** {key_benefits}, {executive_quote} (Core benefits of the announcement and a compelling quote from a senior executive)  
- **Target Audience & Media Info:** {target_audience}, {preferred_media_outlets} (Primary audience segments and preferred media channels for distribution)  
- **Additional Context & Call to Action:** {background_info}, {cta} (Relevant background information and a clear call to action for readers)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that captures the essence and significance of the announcement.  
2. Write a concise subheadline that supports the headline by summarizing the main benefit or impact.  
3. Craft an opening paragraph that answers the who, what, when, where, why, and how of the announcement, ensuring clarity and immediacy.  
4. Develop subsequent paragraphs to elaborate on key benefits, provide relevant background, and incorporate the executive quote naturally to add authority and personalization.  
5. Close with a compelling call to action directing readers on next steps, such as visiting a website, attending an event, or contacting the company for more information.  
6. Ensure the tone remains professional, confident, and aligned with the company’s brand voice throughout.  
7. Proofread for factual accuracy, grammatical precision, and adherence to journalistic style standards.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Standard press release format including headline, subheadline, dateline, body paragraphs, boilerplate, and contact info  
- Style: Formal, authoritative, and engaging, suitable for corporate communications and media distribution  
- Structure: Logical flow with clear segmentation of headline, lead, body, quote integration, and call to action; use active voice and avoid jargon

**Output Format:**  
Provide the complete press release text formatted with:  
- Headline (bold, centered)  
- Subheadline (italicized, centered)  
- Dateline (city, date)  
- Body paragraphs (left aligned)  
- Executive quote attributed with name and title  
- Boilerplate paragraph about the company  
- Contact information for media inquiries

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when a company needs to announce significant corporate news such as product launches, partnerships, mergers, or executive changes. It benefits communications teams and PR agencies aiming to create polished, media-ready press releases that effectively convey important information and engage key stakeholders.
