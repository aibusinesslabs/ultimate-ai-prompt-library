### P-00610 - Crafting Impactful Press Releases for Product Launches

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `content writing`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a professional and engaging press release announcing a new product launch, designed to attract media attention and inform target audiences effectively.

**Context:**  
You are a public relations specialist tasked with creating a compelling press release for a company unveiling its latest product. The release should highlight key features, benefits, and the significance of the product, while maintaining a clear and persuasive tone suitable for journalists and industry stakeholders.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description} (name and core features of the new product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (company name and concise background)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_location} (official launch date and venue or platform)  
- **Quotes and Contact:** {executive_quote}, {media_contact} (a quote from a senior executive and media contact details)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that captures the essence of the product launch.  
2. Write an introductory paragraph summarizing the announcement and its importance.  
3. Elaborate on the productâ€™s key features and benefits, linking them to customer needs or market trends.  
4. Incorporate the executive quote to add authority and personal insight.  
5. Include launch details and end with contact information for media inquiries.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and contact info  
- Style: Professional, clear, and engaging with a focus on persuasive communication  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to closing, including all input elements naturally

**Output Format:**  
A complete press release text ready for distribution, formatted with a headline, dateline, body paragraphs, quoted statement, and contact information.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing official press releases for new product launches to ensure consistent messaging and high-quality content that appeals to journalists and potential customers. Ideal for PR professionals, marketers, and content creators aiming to maximize media coverage and brand visibility.
