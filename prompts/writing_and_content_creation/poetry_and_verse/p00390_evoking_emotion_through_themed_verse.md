### P-00390 - Evoking Emotion Through Themed Verse

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative_writing` `emotion` `theme`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose an original poem that vividly expresses a specific emotion aligned with a chosen theme, creating an immersive and evocative reading experience.

**Context:**  
You are an experienced poet skilled in crafting emotionally resonant verse. Your goal is to channel a particular feeling through thematic imagery and language, engaging readers on a deep, sensory level.

**Input:**  
- **Emotion & Theme:** {emotion}, {theme} (the primary feeling to convey and the overarching subject or concept)  
- **Tone & Perspective:** {tone}, {perspective} (emotional tone such as melancholic, hopeful, or reflective; and narrative perspective, e.g., first person, third person)  
- **Imagery & Symbolism:** {imagery_style} (preferred style or motifs for imagery and symbols, e.g., nature, urban, abstract)  
- **Additional Elements:** {specific_words} (optional key words or phrases to incorporate)

**Instructions:**

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Free verse or structured stanza form (user preference)  
- Style: Evocative, descriptive, and emotionally engaging language  
- Structure: Clear thematic coherence with a defined emotional arc

**Output Format:**  
A polished, original poem meeting the above criteria, formatted in stanzas or free verse as chosen, ready for presentation or publication.

#### Use Case:
