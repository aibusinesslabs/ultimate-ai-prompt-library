### P-00855 - Crafting Emotionally Resonant Thematic Poetry

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative-writing` `theme` `emotional-tone`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose an original poem that creatively explores a given theme while capturing a specified emotional tone and incorporating targeted stylistic elements.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled poet tasked with producing a nuanced piece that blends theme and emotion seamlessly. Your goal is to evoke vivid imagery and deep feelings through carefully chosen language and poetic devices, tailored to the specified parameters.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Emotion:** {theme}, {emotionalTone} (central topic and mood to convey)  
- **Stylistic Preferences:** {style} (poetic style such as free verse, sonnet, haiku, etc.)  
- **Imagery and Symbolism:** {imageryKeywords} (key images or symbols to weave into the poem)  
- **Length and Tone Details:** {wordCount}, {toneDetails} (desired length and any additional tone nuances)

**Instructions:**  
1. Analyze the {theme} and interpret how to best express it through poetry.  
2. Integrate the {emotionalTone} consistently throughout the poem to evoke intended feelings.  
3. Employ the {style} as the structural and rhythmic framework for the poem.  
4. Incorporate the {imageryKeywords} naturally to enrich symbolic depth and vividness.  
5. Ensure the poem aligns with the specified {wordCount} and reflects the {toneDetails} nuances.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: Between 300 and 500 words  
- Format: Poem, adhering to the specified poetic style and structure  
- Style: Must reflect the emotional tone and thematic depth requested  
- Structure: Follow the conventions of the chosen poetic form without compromising creativity

**Output Format:**  
A complete, polished poem formatted according to the selected style, clearly embodying the theme, emotional tone, and imagery specified, within the given word range.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for writers, educators, and creative professionals seeking to generate thoughtful, original poetry tailored to specific thematic and emotional goals. It supports creative exploration in literary workshops, content creation, or personal artistic development.
