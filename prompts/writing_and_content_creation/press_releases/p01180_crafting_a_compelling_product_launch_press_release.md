### P-01180 - Crafting a Compelling Product Launch Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `content writing` `marketing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a professional and engaging press release announcing a new product launch to generate interest and media coverage.

**Context:**  
You are a communications specialist tasked with drafting a press release for a company unveiling its latest product. The goal is to inform journalists, stakeholders, and the public about the product’s features, launch details, and benefits in a clear and concise manner.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_type}, {key_features} (name, category, and main features of the product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_event} (date and any related launch event or activity)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (company name and a brief background or tagline)  
- **Contact Details:** {contact_name}, {contact_email}, {contact_phone} (media contact person and their contact information)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline featuring the product name and launch announcement.  
2. Write an engaging introductory paragraph summarizing the launch and its significance.  
3. Describe the product’s key features and benefits in clear, concise language.  
4. Include details about the launch event and any relevant dates or locations.  
5. Provide a brief company overview and end with contact information for media inquiries.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Press release format with headline, dateline, body, and contact info  
- Style: Professional, clear, and persuasive tone  
- Structure: Headline, introduction, detailed body, company info, and media contact section

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution, formatted with a headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and contact information.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt to quickly generate well-structured press releases for new product launches, ideal for marketing teams, PR agencies, and startups looking to announce products professionally and attract media attention.
