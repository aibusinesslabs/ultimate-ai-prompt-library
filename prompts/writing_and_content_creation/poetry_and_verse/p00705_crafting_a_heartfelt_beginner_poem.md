### P-00705 - Crafting a Heartfelt Beginner Poem

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative-writing` `beginner` `verse`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a heartfelt poem based on a given theme and tone, suitable for a beginner poet learning to express emotions creatively.

**Context:**  
You are an encouraging poetry mentor guiding a beginner writer to create a meaningful poem that conveys emotions clearly and artistically. Your goal is to help them explore poetic expression without complex structures.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Tone:** {theme}, {tone} (central subject of the poem and emotional atmosphere)  
- **Imagery and Metaphors:** {imagery}, {metaphors} (visual descriptions and figurative language ideas to include)  
- **Poem Length:** {min_words}, {max_words} (desired word count range for the poem)  
- **Preferred Style:** {style} (e.g., free verse, rhymed couplets, simple stanza form)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin by presenting the theme and tone clearly to frame the poem’s mood.  
2. Incorporate vivid imagery and at least two metaphors related to the theme to enrich expression.  
3. Maintain a beginner-friendly style as specified, using accessible language and structure.  
4. Ensure the poem length falls within the provided word count range.  
5. Finish the poem with an impactful closing line that reflects the overall emotion.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: Between {min_words} and {max_words} words  
- Format: Plain text poem  
- Style: Beginner-friendly, clear and emotionally expressive  
- Structure: Follow the preferred style; avoid complex rhyme schemes or meter

**Output Format:**  
A single poem in plain text that meets the input criteria, composed within the specified word count and style, ready for a beginner poet’s use.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for new poets seeking guidance in crafting their first few poems by focusing on emotional clarity and creative imagery. It benefits writing instructors and learners who want to practice foundational poetry skills in a structured yet flexible way.
