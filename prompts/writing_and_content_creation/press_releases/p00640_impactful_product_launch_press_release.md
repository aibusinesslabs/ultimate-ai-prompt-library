### P-00640 - Impactful Product Launch Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `media communication` `branding`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional press release announcing a new product launch that highlights its key features, market benefits, and company vision to attract media attention and potential customers.

**Context:**  
You are a communications specialist crafting a press release for a technology company introducing an innovative product. Your goal is to create a clear, engaging, and informative announcement that aligns with the company’s brand voice and excites the target audience.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_features} (name and main features of the new product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_mission} (company’s name and mission statement)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {availability} (official launch date and product availability)  
- **Target Audience & Market:** {target_audience}, {market_position} (intended customers and product’s competitive advantage)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a compelling headline that includes the product name and launch announcement.  
2. Write an introductory paragraph summarizing the product and its significance.  
3. Detail the key features and benefits of the product, emphasizing what sets it apart in the market.  
4. Incorporate company background and mission to reinforce credibility and brand values.  
5. Include launch date, availability information, and a call-to-action encouraging media or customer engagement.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Professional press release structure with headline, dateline, body, and contact information placeholder  
- Style: Formal, clear, and persuasive tone suitable for media distribution  
- Structure: Logical flow from announcement to details, concluding with actionable next steps  

**Output Format:**  
A complete press release text, formatted with a headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and ending with a placeholder for media contact information.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing a press release to announce a new product launch, especially for technology companies aiming to generate buzz and attract media coverage. Ideal for marketing teams, PR professionals, and business owners seeking a polished, persuasive announcement aligned with brand messaging.
