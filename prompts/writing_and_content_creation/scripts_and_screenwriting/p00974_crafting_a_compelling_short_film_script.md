### P-00974 - Crafting a Compelling Short Film Script

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `screenwriting` `shortfilm` `dialogue` `characterdevelopment`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a concise and engaging short film script that introduces main characters, sets up a central conflict, and concludes with a meaningful resolution.

**Context:**  
You are a novice screenwriter tasked with drafting a short film script aimed at beginners. The script should be suitable for a 5-7 minute film and focus on clear character motivation and natural dialogue.

**Input:**  
- **Characters & Setting:** {main_character_name}, {supporting_character_name}, {location} (names of two characters and the primary setting)  
- **Conflict:** {central_conflict} (brief description of the main problem or challenge)  
- **Tone & Genre:** {tone}, {genre} (emotional tone and genre style, e.g., dramatic, comedic)  
- **Resolution:** {resolution_type} (type of ending desired, e.g., hopeful, ambiguous, twist)

**Instructions:**  
1. Introduce the main and supporting characters with brief but distinct descriptions.  
2. Establish the setting clearly to ground the story.  
3. Present the central conflict through natural dialogue or action within the first third of the script.  
4. Develop the charactersâ€™ interaction and tension around the conflict.  
5. Conclude with the {resolution_type} ending, ensuring the story feels complete yet concise.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Script format with scene headings, character names, and dialogue lines  
- Style: Clear, natural dialogue suitable for beginner screenwriters  
- Structure: Three-act structure (setup, confrontation, resolution) condensed for a short film

**Output Format:**  
A short film script formatted with scene headings, character names aligned above dialogue, and concise stage directions if necessary.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for beginner screenwriters looking to practice creating focused, efficient short film scripts. It supports learning basic screenplay formatting, character development, and conflict-driven storytelling within a manageable word count.
