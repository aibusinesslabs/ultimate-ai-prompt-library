### P-00029 - Crafting a Compelling Feature Film Screenplay

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `screenplay` `feature_film` `character_development` `plot_structure`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Develop a polished and engaging feature film screenplay draft that effectively balances character development, plot progression, and thematic depth.

**Context:**  
You are a professional screenwriter tasked with creating a captivating 90-120 minute feature film screenplay. The story must reflect strong character arcs, coherent plot development with clear acts, and emotionally resonant dialogue that fits the chosen genre and tone.

**Input:**  
- **Story Elements:** {genre}, {theme}, {setting} (genre of the film, central theme, and primary setting/location)  
- **Characters:** {protagonist_description}, {antagonist_description}, {supporting_characters} (key characters with motivations and relationships)  
- **Plot Outline:** {logline}, {key_story_beats} (a concise logline and a list of major plot points or turning points)  
- **Tone & Style:** {tone}, {dialogue_style} (desired emotional tone and style of dialogue, e.g., naturalistic, stylized)

**Instructions:**  
1. Construct a clear three-act structure incorporating the provided plot outline and story elements.  
2. Develop detailed character arcs for the protagonist and antagonist, ensuring motivations and conflicts evolve logically.  
3. Write dynamic and context-appropriate scenes that reveal character depth and advance the plot, including dialogue aligned with the specified style.  
4. Integrate thematic elements seamlessly throughout the screenplay to enhance emotional resonance and message.  
5. Format the screenplay draft following industry standards (e.g., correct scene headings, action descriptions, and dialogue formatting).

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Professional screenplay format with proper scene headers and dialogue layout  
- Style: Consistent with the chosen genre and tone; dialogue must feel authentic and purposeful  
- Structure: Must follow a coherent three-act narrative with clear plot progression and character development

**Output Format:**  
A formatted screenplay draft excerpt (approximately 3-5 scenes) demonstrating plot progression, character interaction, and thematic integration in professional screenplay format.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for experienced screenwriters seeking to draft a feature film screenplay or screenplay excerpts with strong narrative and character focus. It benefits professional writers, script consultants, and advanced creatives refining their craft or preparing submissions for production or competitions.
