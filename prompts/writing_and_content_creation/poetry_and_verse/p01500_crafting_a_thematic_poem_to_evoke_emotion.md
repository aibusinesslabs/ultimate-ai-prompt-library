### P-01500 - Crafting a Thematic Poem to Evoke Emotion

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative-writing` `emotion` `theme`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write an original poem that captures the essence of a chosen theme and evokes a specific emotion in the reader.

**Context:**  
You are a creative poet tasked with composing a short, expressive poem. Your goal is to use vivid imagery, rhythm, and thoughtful word choice to bring a clear theme to life while stirring an emotional response.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Emotion:** {theme}, {emotion} (The central idea of the poem and the feeling it should convey)  
- **Tone and Style:** {tone}, {style} (The desired mood, such as hopeful or melancholic, and poetic style, e.g., free verse or rhymed)  
- **Target Audience:** {audience} (The group or individual the poem is intended for, e.g., children, adults, or a general audience)  
- **Additional Elements:** {imagery_keywords} (Optional key images or symbols to include in the poem)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin by reflecting on the {theme} and the {emotion} to guide your creative process.  
2. Choose a {tone} and {style} that best enhance the emotional impact for the {audience}.  
3. Incorporate relevant imagery or symbols from {imagery_keywords} to enrich the poem’s sensory experience.  
4. Write a poem between 150 and 300 words that clearly conveys the {theme} and evokes the {emotion}.  
5. Review and revise the poem to ensure it flows well and connects emotionally with the intended {audience}.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150–300 words  
- Format: Poetic form (free verse or rhyme, as specified)  
- Style: Expressive, clear, and accessible language suitable for the {audience}  
- Structure: A coherent poem with a beginning, development, and conclusion or closing image  

**Output Format:**  
Provide the completed poem as plain text without additional commentary or explanation.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for beginner poets and creative writers looking to practice thematic and emotional expression in poetry. It supports educators, students, and content creators who want to develop engaging, emotionally resonant verse tailored to specific audiences and styles.
