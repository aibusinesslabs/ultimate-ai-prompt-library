### P-00145 - Impactful Product Launch Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `branding` `media relations`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional press release announcing the launch of a new product, highlighting its unique features, benefits, and market impact.

**Context:**  
You are a seasoned PR specialist tasked with creating a compelling press release for a company unveiling its latest product. Your goal is to inform media outlets, potential customers, and stakeholders with clarity and persuasive language that aligns with the brand’s voice and values.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_features} (name and key features of the product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background} (company’s name and brief background)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {availability} (official launch date and market availability)  
- **Market Impact:** {target_audience}, {unique_selling_points} (intended customer base and product differentiators)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that captures attention and summarizes the news.  
2. Craft an engaging opening paragraph that introduces the product and its significance.  
3. Describe the product’s main features and benefits clearly and concisely.  
4. Incorporate relevant company background to build credibility and context.  
5. Conclude with launch details, availability, and a call-to-action or contact information.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Standard press release structure with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and boilerplate  
- Style: Professional, informative, and persuasive tone consistent with corporate communications  
- Structure: Logical flow from announcement to detailed information, ending with company boilerplate  

**Output Format:**  
A well-structured press release text ready for distribution, formatted with a headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and company boilerplate.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing a formal announcement for a new product launch to maximize media pickup and customer interest. Ideal for marketing teams, PR agencies, and brand managers aiming to communicate product innovations clearly and professionally.
