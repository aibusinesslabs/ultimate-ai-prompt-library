### P-00520 - Impactful Product Launch Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `media communication`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a professional and engaging press release announcing the launch of a new product, designed to capture media attention and inform target audiences effectively.

**Context:**  
You are a public relations specialist tasked with drafting a press release for a company unveiling a new product. The release should highlight key features, market relevance, and the company’s vision, aiming to generate interest from journalists, customers, and stakeholders.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_type} (name and category of the product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_location} (date and place of product launch)  
- **Company Profile:** {company_name}, {company_overview} (company’s name and brief description)  
- **Key Selling Points:** {product_features}, {target_audience} (main features and intended customers)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a compelling headline that includes the product name and launch announcement.  
2. Write a concise opening paragraph summarizing the product launch, including the who, what, when, and where.  
3. Detail the product’s key features and benefits in the following paragraphs, emphasizing innovation and customer value.  
4. Include a brief company background to establish credibility and context.  
5. End with a call to action or information on where to find additional details or contact the company.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and boilerplate  
- Style: Professional, clear, and engaging tone suitable for media outlets  
- Structure: Logical flow from announcement to details, company info, and closing statement

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution, formatted with a headline, dateline, body, and company boilerplate.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing a formal press release to announce new product launches. It benefits PR professionals, marketing teams, and business owners aiming to create media-ready content that effectively communicates product details and company positioning to journalists and consumers.
