### P-00407 - Persuasive Product Launch Email Copywriting

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Copywriting  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `email marketing` `product launch` `sales copy` `persuasion`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a compelling and persuasive product launch email aimed at converting prospects into customers.

**Context:**  
You are a professional copywriter specializing in crafting email campaigns for product launches. Your goal is to engage the target audience, highlight product benefits, and drive sales through clear, persuasive language tailored to the recipient’s needs.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_features} (key features and unique selling points)  
- **Target Audience:** {audience_description} (demographics, pain points, interests)  
- **Offer Details:** {launch_offer} (discounts, bonuses, or limited-time deals)  
- **Brand Voice:** {brand_tone} (formal, casual, enthusiastic, professional)

**Instructions:**  
1. Open with a strong, attention-grabbing headline that reflects the launch excitement.  
2. Introduce the product briefly, focusing on key benefits aligned with the audience’s needs.  
3. Highlight unique features and how they solve specific pain points for the recipient.  
4. Present the launch offer clearly and create urgency with a call-to-action.  
5. Close with a friendly, persuasive sign-off that encourages immediate engagement.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Email copy with headline, body, and call-to-action  
- Style: Persuasive, clear, and engaging; consistent with provided brand voice  
- Structure: Logical flow from hook to offer to call-to-action without jargon

**Output Format:**  
A complete product launch email draft including:  
- Headline  
- Body text with benefits and offer details  
- Clear call-to-action statement  
- Professional closing

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for marketers and copywriters preparing for a new product introduction via email campaigns. It helps generate focused, conversion-driven emails tailored to specific audiences and brand identities to maximize launch success.
