### P-00550 - Crafting an Impactful Product Launch Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `branding`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional and engaging press release announcing the launch of a new product.

**Context:**  
You are a PR specialist tasked with creating a clear, concise, and persuasive press release to generate media interest and inform the target audience about the new product.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_type} (name and category of the new product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_location} (date and place of the product launch)  
- **Key Features:** {feature_list} (a brief list of the product’s main features or benefits)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name} (name of the company releasing the product)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a compelling headline that highlights the product launch.  
2. Write a strong opening paragraph summarizing the launch event and key product details.  
3. Describe the product’s main features and benefits in a clear and engaging manner.  
4. Include a quote from a company representative to add credibility and personality.  
5. Close with relevant contact information and a call to action for interested parties.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Formal press release format with headline, date line, body, and contact info  
- Style: Professional, clear, and persuasive language suitable for media outlets  
- Structure: Logical flow from announcement to details to closing statement

**Output Format:**  
A complete press release text ready for distribution, formatted with:  
- Headline  
- Date line  
- Body paragraphs  
- Contact information section

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for marketing teams, PR professionals, and small business owners who need to create effective product launch announcements quickly. It ensures the press release is informative, media-friendly, and aligned with professional standards.
