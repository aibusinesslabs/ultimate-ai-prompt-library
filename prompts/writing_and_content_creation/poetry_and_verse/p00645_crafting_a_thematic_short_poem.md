### P-00645 - Crafting a Thematic Short Poem

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative writing` `theme` `verse`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a short poem that captures a specific theme and tone using vivid imagery and simple language.

**Context:**  
You are a beginner poet aiming to express a clear theme through concise, evocative verse. Your goal is to engage readers with accessible language while maintaining emotional depth.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Tone:** {theme}, {tone} (the central idea and mood for the poem)  
- **Imagery Elements:** {imagery1}, {imagery2} (two vivid images or symbols to include)  
- **Preferred Poem Style:** {style} (e.g., free verse, rhymed couplets, haiku)  
- **Audience:** {audience} (intended readers, e.g., children, adults, general audience)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin by reflecting on the {theme} and {tone} to set the poem’s mood.  
2. Incorporate the {imagery1} and {imagery2} creatively to enhance sensory appeal.  
3. Follow the {style} structure for the poem’s format and rhythm.  
4. Use clear and simple language suitable for the {audience}.  
5. Ensure the poem evokes emotion and delivers the theme effectively within the word limit.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Poem with appropriate line breaks according to chosen style  
- Style: Evocative yet accessible language, matching the specified tone  
- Structure: Adhere to the selected poem style’s conventions (e.g., rhyme scheme if applicable)

**Output Format:**  
A single poem formatted as per the chosen style, incorporating all specified elements, between 150 and 300 words.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for beginner poets looking to practice theme-driven poetry with guidance on imagery and style. It benefits creative writing educators, students, and content creators seeking to produce concise, meaningful verse tailored to specific audiences and moods.
