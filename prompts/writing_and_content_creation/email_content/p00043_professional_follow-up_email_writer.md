### P-00043 - Professional Follow-Up Email Writer

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Email Content  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `email` `follow-up` `professional` `customer-relations`  

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a professional and polite follow-up email to a client or colleague to check on the status of a previous request or meeting.

**Context:**  
You are a professional communicator tasked with writing a clear and courteous follow-up email that encourages a timely response without sounding pushy or impatient.

**Input:**  
- **Recipient Details:** {recipient_name}, {recipient_role} (the person receiving the email and their position)  
- **Previous Interaction:** {previous_topic}, {previous_date} (subject and date of last communication)  
- **Purpose of Follow-Up:** {request_summary} (brief summary of what you are following up on)  
- **Sender Details:** {sender_name}, {sender_role} (your name and position)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Start with a polite greeting addressing {recipient_name}.  
2. Reference the previous interaction on {previous_date} regarding {previous_topic}.  
3. Clearly and briefly restate the purpose of your follow-up using {request_summary}.  
4. Express understanding of their busy schedule and gently encourage a response.  
5. Close the email professionally with your {sender_name} and {sender_role}.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Formal business email format with greeting and closing  
- Style: Polite, professional, concise, and clear  
- Structure: Introduction, reference to previous communication, follow-up purpose, call to action, closing  

**Output Format:**  
A complete, polished follow-up email ready to be sent, formatted with subject line, greeting, body paragraphs, and professional closing signature.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when you need to send a courteous follow-up email to clients, colleagues, or stakeholders after a meeting or request. It helps maintain professionalism and encourages timely responses, ideal for business communication and relationship management.
