### P-01125 - Crafting a Thematic Poem with Emotional Depth

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative writing` `emotions` `theme`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a thematic poem that effectively conveys a specific emotion using vivid imagery and expressive language.

**Context:**  
You are a beginner poet aiming to create a short, impactful poem that evokes a particular feeling while adhering to a clear theme.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Emotion**: {theme}, {emotion} (the central topic and feeling to convey)  
- **Tone and Style**: {tone}, {style} (desired mood and poetic style, e.g., lyrical, narrative)  
- **Imagery and Symbolism**: {imagery}, {symbols} (key images or symbols to incorporate)  
- **Length and Format**: {word_count}, {format} (word count between 150-300 words and preferred poem structure, e.g., free verse, rhymed)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin by defining the central theme and the primary emotion your poem should express.  
2. Incorporate vivid imagery and relevant symbols that reinforce the theme and emotion.  
3. Maintain the chosen tone and style consistently throughout the poem.  
4. Structure the poem according to the specified format, ensuring a natural flow and readability.  
5. Keep the poem within the 150-300 word limit, focusing on clarity and emotional impact.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Follow the specified poem structure (e.g., free verse, sonnet)  
- Style: Reflect the chosen tone (e.g., melancholic, hopeful) using expressive and accessible language  
- Structure: Use imagery and symbolism to enhance emotional resonance without overcomplicating the poem

**Output Format:**  
A polished, original poem matching the given theme, emotion, tone, style, and format, presented as plain text.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for beginner poets or creative writers seeking to practice crafting emotionally resonant poetry around a clear theme. It helps develop skills in imagery, tone control, and structural awareness, suitable for creative writing exercises or personal projects.
