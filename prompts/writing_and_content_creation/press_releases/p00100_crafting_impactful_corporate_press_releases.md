### P-00100 - Crafting Impactful Corporate Press Releases

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `corporate communications` `branding` `public relations`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional and engaging press release announcing a significant company update, designed to attract media attention and inform stakeholders effectively.

**Context:**  
You are a corporate communications specialist tasked with creating a clear, concise, and persuasive press release for a mid-sized company. The goal is to communicate key information while maintaining the companyâ€™s brand voice and adhering to standard PR conventions.

**Input:**  
- **Company Details:** {company_name}, {industry}, {location} (Basic company profile)  
- **Announcement Details:** {announcement_type}, {key_points}, {date_of_announcement} (Nature and specifics of the update)  
- **Quotes:** {executive_name}, {executive_title}, {quote_text} (Relevant executive quote to add credibility)  
- **Contact Information:** {contact_name}, {contact_email}, {contact_phone} (For media inquiries)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a compelling headline summarizing the announcement.  
2. Craft a strong opening paragraph outlining the who, what, when, where, and why.  
3. Expand on the announcement with detailed yet concise key points in the body.  
4. Include the provided executive quote to humanize and reinforce the message.  
5. End with a boilerplate company description and clear media contact information.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, dateline, body, quote, boilerplate, and contact info  
- Style: Professional, objective, and engaging tone consistent with corporate communications  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to conclusion, easy to scan by journalists

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text formatted with:  
- Headline (bold)  
- Dateline (city, date)  
- Body paragraphs  
- Executive quote in quotation marks, attributed properly  
- Boilerplate paragraph about the company  
- Contact information labeled clearly

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing official company announcements such as product launches, leadership changes, partnerships, or milestone achievements. Ideal for PR professionals, marketers, and corporate communicators seeking a structured and effective press release draft that balances professionalism with readability.
