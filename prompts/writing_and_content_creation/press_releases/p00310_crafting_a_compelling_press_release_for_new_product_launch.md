### P-00310 - Crafting a Compelling Press Release for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `content creation` `marketing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a concise and professional press release announcing a new product launch that effectively highlights its key features and benefits.

**Context:**  
You are a communications specialist tasked with creating an engaging press release to generate media interest and inform the public about a new product from a company.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description} (name and a brief overview of the product)  
- **Launch Information:** {launch_date}, {launch_location} (date and place of the launch event)  
- **Company Background:** {company_name}, {company_overview} (company name and a short description)  
- **Contact Information:** {contact_name}, {contact_email} (media contact person and email address)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong, attention-grabbing headline that includes the product name.  
2. Write an introductory paragraph summarizing the launch and its significance.  
3. Describe the productâ€™s key features and benefits clearly and concisely.  
4. Include relevant company background to provide credibility and context.  
5. Close with launch event details and contact information for media inquiries.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Formal press release format with headline, body paragraphs, and contact info  
- Style: Professional, clear, and persuasive language suitable for media outlets  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to introduction, body, and closing contact details  

**Output Format:**  
A complete press release text formatted with a headline, three to four body paragraphs, and a contact information section at the end.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt to quickly generate polished press releases for new product launches, ideal for marketing teams or PR professionals looking to create media-ready announcements that effectively communicate key details and spark interest.
