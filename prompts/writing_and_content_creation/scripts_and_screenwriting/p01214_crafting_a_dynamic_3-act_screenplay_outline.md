### P-01214 - Crafting a Dynamic 3-Act Screenplay Outline

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `screenwriting` `story-structure` `3-act` `plot-development`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Develop a detailed 3-act screenplay outline that clearly defines the key plot points, character arcs, and dramatic turning points for a compelling story.

**Context:**  
You are an experienced screenwriter creating an organized and engaging screenplay outline to serve as a blueprint for writing a full script. Your goal is to structure the narrative following the classic 3-act format, emphasizing pacing and character development.

**Input:**  
- **Story Basics:** {title}, {genre} (The working title and genre of the screenplay)  
- **Main Characters:** {protagonist}, {antagonist} (Brief descriptions and motivations of the lead characters)  
- **Setting & Tone:** {setting}, {tone} (Where the story takes place and the overall mood)  
- **Core Conflict:** {conflict} (The central problem or challenge driving the narrative)

**Instructions:**  
1. Define Act 1 by introducing the protagonist, setting, and initial situation; establish the inciting incident that propels the story forward.  
2. Outline Act 2 by detailing rising action, key obstacles, subplots, and character development leading to the mid-point climax.  
3. Structure Act 3 with the climax, resolution of the core conflict, and the protagonistâ€™s transformation or final state.  
4. Highlight at least three major plot points or turning points that distinctly separate each act.  
5. Ensure the outline balances narrative progression with character arcs and thematic elements.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Clear, organized prose with labeled sections for each act  
- Style: Professional, concise, and engaging with strong narrative clarity  
- Structure: Follow the 3-act format explicitly with distinct act divisions and plot point annotations

**Output Format:**  
A structured screenplay outline divided into Act 1, Act 2, and Act 3 sections, each containing a summary of key plot points, character developments, and dramatic turning points.

#### Use Case:  
Ideal for screenwriters and storytellers seeking to create a solid foundation for their scripts, this prompt helps generate a clear, professional 3-act outline. It is especially useful during the planning phase to visualize story flow and refine narrative elements before writing the full screenplay.
