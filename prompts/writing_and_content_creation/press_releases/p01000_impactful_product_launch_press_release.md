### P-01000 - Impactful Product Launch Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `marketing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a polished and engaging press release announcing the launch of a new product, designed to capture media attention and generate buzz.

**Context:**  
You are a professional PR writer tasked with creating a compelling press release for a company’s newest product. The release should highlight key features, benefits, and the product’s unique value proposition while maintaining a formal and persuasive tone.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details**: {product_name}, {product_description} (name and overview of the new product)  
- **Launch Information**: {launch_date}, {availability} (official release date and market availability)  
- **Company Background**: {company_name}, {company_mission} (company name and a brief mission statement)  
- **Key Benefits & Features**: {feature_list}, {target_audience} (major features and intended customer base)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that clearly announces the product launch.  
2. Introduce the product and company in the opening paragraph, including the launch date and availability.  
3. Elaborate on the product’s key features and benefits, emphasizing what makes it unique and valuable.  
4. Include a quote from a company executive or product manager to add authenticity and insight.  
5. Close with a call to action and contact information for media inquiries.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, dateline, body paragraphs, and contact info  
- Style: Professional, clear, and persuasive language  
- Structure: Logical flow from announcement to details, quote, and closing statement  

**Output Format:**  
A fully formatted press release text ready for distribution, including headline, dateline, body, quote, and media contact details.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when you need to create a professional press release for a new product launch that will be sent to media outlets and stakeholders. Ideal for PR professionals, marketing teams, or business owners aiming to generate excitement and coverage around a new product introduction.
