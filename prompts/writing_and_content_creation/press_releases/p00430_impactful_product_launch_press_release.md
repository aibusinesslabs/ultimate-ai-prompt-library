### P-00430 - Impactful Product Launch Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `media communication`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Craft a professional and engaging press release announcing a new product launch that captures media interest and clearly communicates the product’s unique benefits.

**Context:**  
You are a communications specialist working for a company preparing to launch a new product. Your goal is to create a compelling press release that will be distributed to media outlets, industry influencers, and potential customers to maximize awareness and generate buzz.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {{product_name}}, {{product_description}} (name and key features of the new product)  
- **Launch Information:** {{launch_date}}, {{launch_location}} (date and venue or platform for the launch event)  
- **Company Background:** {{company_name}}, {{company_overview}} (brief information about the company releasing the product)  
- **Quotes and Testimonials:** {{quote_from_executive}}, {{customer_testimonial}} (a quote from a company executive and a relevant customer or expert endorsement)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that highlights the product launch and its significance.  
2. Write a concise introductory paragraph summarizing the launch event and the product’s primary benefit.  
3. Provide detailed information about the product’s features and how it stands out in the market.  
4. Incorporate the provided quotes to add credibility and a human element to the release.  
5. Conclude with a call to action and contact information for media inquiries.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Standard press release format with headline, dateline, body, and boilerplate  
- Style: Professional, clear, and persuasive tone suitable for media and business audiences  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to introduction, body details, quotes, and closing statement

**Output Format:**  
A polished press release text ready for distribution, formatted with appropriate headings and paragraphs.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when a company is preparing to announce a new product launch to the public and media. It benefits PR professionals, marketing teams, and small business owners who need a structured, effective press release to drive awareness and engagement ahead of a launch event.
