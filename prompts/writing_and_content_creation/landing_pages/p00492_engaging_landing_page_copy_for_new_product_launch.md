### P-00492 - Engaging Landing Page Copy for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Landing Pages  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `landing page` `copywriting` `product launch` `conversion`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a compelling and concise landing page copy that highlights the key benefits and features of a new product to maximize visitor engagement and conversions.

**Context:**  
You are a professional copywriter tasked with crafting persuasive landing page content for a recently launched product aimed at potential customers unfamiliar with the brand.

**Input:**  
- **Product Information:** {product_name}, {product_features} (name and key features of the product)  
- **Target Audience:** {audience_description} (brief description of intended customers)  
- **Primary Benefit:** {primary_benefit} (main advantage or value proposition)  
- **Call to Action:** {cta_text} (desired user action, e.g., “Buy Now,” “Learn More”)

**Instructions:**  
1. Write a captivating headline incorporating {product_name} that grabs attention instantly.  
2. Summarize {primary_benefit} clearly within the first two sentences.  
3. Highlight 3-5 key {product_features} in an easy-to-read format.  
4. Address the {audience_description} directly, using language that resonates with their needs.  
5. End with a strong, persuasive {cta_text} encouraging immediate action.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: Between 150 and 300 words  
- Format: Use short paragraphs and bullet points where appropriate  
- Style: Professional, clear, and persuasive, with a friendly tone  
- Structure: Headline, benefit summary, features list, audience connection, and call to action

**Output Format:**  
A single, well-structured landing page copy text suitable for direct publishing.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt to quickly generate effective landing page content for startups or marketers launching new products. It benefits copywriters and marketing teams needing beginner-friendly and conversion-focused copy that can be adapted across various product categories.
