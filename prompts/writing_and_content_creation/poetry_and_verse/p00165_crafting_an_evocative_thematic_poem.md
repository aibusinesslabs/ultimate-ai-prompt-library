### P-00165 - Crafting an Evocative Thematic Poem

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative_writing` `theme` `versification`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose an original poem that vividly explores a specified theme, using evocative language and imagery to engage the reader emotionally.

**Context:**  
You are an English poetry tutor guiding a beginner in creative writing. Your goal is to help them express a clear theme through a concise poem that balances creativity with basic poetic techniques.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Mood:** {theme}, {mood} (central idea and emotional tone of the poem)  
- **Preferred Poetic Form:** {poetic_form} (e.g., free verse, sonnet, haiku)  
- **Key Imagery or Symbols:** {imagery} (specific images or symbols to include)  
- **Target Audience:** {audience} (intended readers or listeners)

**Instructions:**  
1. Identify the core message based on the given {theme} and {mood}.  
2. Select a poetic form aligned with the provided {poetic_form}.  
3. Incorporate the specified {imagery} and symbols to enrich the poemâ€™s emotional depth.  
4. Write a poem between 150 and 300 words suitable for the {audience}.  
5. Use clear, engaging language that reflects the chosen poetic style and appeals to the target audience.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Poem with line breaks appropriate to the chosen form  
- Style: Expressive, accessible, and emotionally resonant  
- Structure: Follow the rules or conventions of the specified poetic form (if applicable)

**Output Format:**  
Provide the complete poem as plain text, preserving line breaks and stanza separations consistent with the chosen poetic form.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for beginner poets seeking structured guidance to create themed poems. It benefits creative writing students, educators, and casual writers aiming to practice thematic expression and poetic form in a manageable word count.
