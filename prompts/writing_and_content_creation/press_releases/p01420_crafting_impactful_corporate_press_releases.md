### P-01420 - Crafting Impactful Corporate Press Releases

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `press release` `corporate communications` `brand messaging` `public relations`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a polished, engaging, and newsworthy corporate press release that effectively announces a significant company development to media outlets and stakeholders.

**Context:**  
You are a professional communications specialist tasked with drafting a press release for a major announcement on behalf of a company. The goal is to clearly convey key information, capture attention, and align with the company’s brand voice while adhering to journalistic standards.

**Input:**  
- **Company Details**: {company_name}, {industry}, {headquarters_location} (Basic company profile and positioning)  
- **Announcement Details**: {announcement_type}, {key_features}, {significance} (Type of announcement, major points, and why it matters)  
- **Quotes**: {executive_name}, {executive_title}, {quote_text} (At least one authoritative quote to add credibility and human perspective)  
- **Distribution Info**: {target_audience}, {release_date}, {contact_info} (Intended audience, timing, and media contact details)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong, attention-grabbing headline summarizing the announcement.  
2. Write a compelling lead paragraph that succinctly answers the who, what, when, where, why, and how.  
3. Expand with supporting paragraphs detailing the announcement’s background, benefits, and implications.  
4. Integrate the provided executive quote naturally to enhance credibility and emotional appeal.  
5. Conclude with boilerplate company information and clear media contact details for follow-up.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Professional press release format with headline, dateline, body, and contact section  
- Style: Formal, clear, and engaging; suitable for journalists and industry stakeholders  
- Structure: Logical flow from headline to contact info, with well-organized paragraphs and no jargon overload  

**Output Format:**  
A finalized press release text formatted for immediate distribution, including:  
- Headline  
- Dateline (City, Date)  
- Body (multiple paragraphs)  
- Boilerplate company background  
- Media contact information  

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing official company announcements such as product launches, mergers, leadership changes, or significant milestones. Corporate communications teams, PR agencies, and marketing professionals will benefit from generating consistent, high-quality press releases tailored to media distribution.
