### P-00642 - Engaging Landing Page Copy for New Product Launch

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Landing Pages  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `landing page` `copywriting` `product launch` `marketing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a compelling and concise landing page copy that highlights the key features and benefits of a new product to attract and convert potential customers.

**Context:**  
You are a professional copywriter tasked with crafting persuasive landing page content for a new product launch targeting a general audience interested in innovative solutions.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_description} (name and brief description of the product)  
- **Target Audience:** {audience_profile} (description of ideal customer demographics and interests)  
- **Key Features:** {feature_list} (bullet points of main product features)  
- **Call to Action:** {cta_text} (primary action you want visitors to take on the page)

**Instructions:**  
1. Write a captivating headline that clearly introduces the product and its value.  
2. Summarize the product description in an engaging way that resonates with the target audience.  
3. Highlight the top 3–5 key features, emphasizing benefits rather than just technical specs.  
4. Include a persuasive call to action encouraging visitors to take the desired step (e.g., buy now, sign up).  
5. Ensure the tone is friendly, clear, and easy to understand for a broad audience.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150–300 words  
- Format: Plain text, suitable for direct use on a landing page  
- Style: Professional yet approachable, persuasive, and customer-focused  
- Structure: Headline, introductory paragraph, feature highlights, call to action

**Output Format:**  
A single, well-structured landing page copy text block matching the specifications and ready for deployment.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when launching a new product and you need clear, effective landing page copy that quickly informs visitors and encourages conversions. Ideal for marketers, small business owners, and content creators seeking beginner-friendly, high-impact copywriting assistance.
