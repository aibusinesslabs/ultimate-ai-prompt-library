### P-01215 - Crafting Evocative Thematic Poetry

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Poetry & Verse  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `poetry` `creative_writing` `theme` `verse`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Compose a polished, evocative poem that explores a specific theme through vivid imagery and emotional depth.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled poet crafting original verse that conveys nuanced emotions and thematic resonance. Your goal is to engage readers by blending creative language with a clear thematic focus.

**Input:**  
- **Theme and Tone**: {theme}, {tone} (central subject and emotional atmosphere)  
- **Form and Length**: {poetic_form}, {desired_length} (type of poem, e.g., sonnet or free verse, and target word count)  
- **Imagery and Devices**: {key_imagery}, {literary_devices} (important visual elements and stylistic techniques to include)  
- **Audience and Purpose**: {target_audience}, {intended_effect} (who the poem is for and the emotional or intellectual response desired)

**Instructions:**  
1. Identify the core message and mood based on the provided {theme} and {tone}.  
2. Select an appropriate {poetic_form} that complements the thematic and tonal requirements.  
3. Incorporate the specified {key_imagery} and {literary_devices} to enrich sensory experience and poetic quality.  
4. Tailor language and structure to resonate with the {target_audience} and achieve the {intended_effect}.  
5. Refine the poem to meet the {desired_length} while maintaining coherence, emotional impact, and artistic integrity.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Poetic stanza structure consistent with the chosen {poetic_form}  
- Style: Evocative, metaphorically rich, and emotionally engaging  
- Structure: Cohesive thematic development with clear progression or narrative flow  

**Output Format:**  
A completed poem formatted according to the specified {poetic_form}, presented in stanzas with appropriate line breaks and punctuation, totaling between 300 and 500 words.

#### Use Case:  
This prompt is ideal for creative writers, poets, educators, and content creators seeking to produce original thematic poetry for publications, workshops, or personal enrichment. It guides the generation of thoughtful, well-crafted verse tailored to specific emotional and stylistic goals.
