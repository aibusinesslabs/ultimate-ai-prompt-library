### P-00734 - Crafting a Compelling Short Film Script Outline

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Scripts & Screenwriting  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Tags:** `scriptwriting` `shortfilm` `storystructure` `creativewriting`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Develop a detailed outline for a short film script that effectively conveys a compelling narrative arc, including character development, conflict, and resolution.

**Context:**  
You are a professional screenwriter tasked with creating a clear and engaging short film outline to guide the full scriptwriting process. Your outline should provide a strong foundation for plot progression and character dynamics within a limited runtime.

**Input:**  
- **Story Details:** {genre}, {theme} (primary genre and central theme of the film)  
- **Characters:** {protagonist_name}, {antagonist_name}, {supporting_characters} (key characters involved and their roles)  
- **Setting:** {time_period}, {location} (when and where the story takes place)  
- **Plot Requirements:** {conflict_description}, {resolution_type} (main conflict and preferred resolution approach)  

**Instructions:**  
1. Summarize the story premise based on the provided genre and theme.  
2. Define each main characterâ€™s motivation, goal, and obstacle within the story context.  
3. Outline the three-act structure: setup, confrontation, and resolution, ensuring clear progression and tension build-up.  
4. Incorporate key plot points including the inciting incident, climax, and resolution, aligned with the conflict and resolution inputs.  
5. Provide brief scene descriptions that illustrate major turning points, ensuring a coherent narrative flow suitable for a short film format.  

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 300-500 words  
- Format: Outline with clear headings for acts and scenes  
- Style: Professional, concise, and engaging narrative tone  
- Structure: Three-act script framework with character focus and plot clarity  

**Output Format:**  
A structured outline for a short film script that includes:  
- Story Premise  
- Character Profiles  
- Act 1: Setup (including inciting incident)  
- Act 2: Confrontation (key conflicts and developments)  
- Act 3: Resolution (climax and conclusion)  
- Brief scene descriptions tied to the plot progression  

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when you need to create a foundational short film script outline that balances creative storytelling with structural rigor. Ideal for screenwriters developing new projects, film students, or content creators aiming to produce narrative-driven short films efficiently.
