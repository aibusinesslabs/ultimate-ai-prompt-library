### P-00625 - Crafting Impactful Press Releases for Product Launches

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `public relations` `corporate communications`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a compelling, professional press release announcing a new product launch that effectively communicates key features, benefits, and company vision to media outlets and target audiences.

**Context:**  
You are a skilled corporate communications specialist tasked with drafting an advanced press release for a company unveiling its latest product. The release should capture attention, provide essential details clearly, and align with the brand’s voice to maximize media pickup and audience engagement.

**Input:**  
- **Product Details:** {product_name}, {product_category}, {key_features} (name, category, and main features of the product)  
- **Company Information:** {company_name}, {company_background}, {brand_tone} (company identity, history, and preferred communication style)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {availability}, {pricing} (release timeline, distribution channels, and pricing information)  
- **Target Audience & Goals:** {target_demographic}, {primary_goals} (intended audience and objectives for the announcement)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong, attention-grabbing headline that highlights the product’s unique selling proposition.  
2. Craft an engaging opening paragraph that summarizes the announcement and its significance to the market.  
3. In the body, detail the product’s features and benefits, incorporating company background and brand tone naturally.  
4. Include launch specifics such as date, availability, and pricing to inform potential customers and partners.  
5. Close with a clear call-to-action and provide contact information or links for further inquiries or follow-up.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 500-750 words  
- Format: Standard press release structure with headline, dateline, introduction, body paragraphs, boilerplate, and contact information  
- Style: Professional, persuasive, and aligned with the company’s brand tone (e.g., formal, innovative, approachable)  
- Structure: Logical flow with clear sections, concise sentences, and no jargon that might confuse media or readers unfamiliar with the product

**Output Format:**  
A polished, ready-to-distribute press release document formatted in plain text, adhering to industry conventions, suitable for immediate use by PR teams and media outlets.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when preparing a high-impact announcement for a new product launch that requires clear messaging and professional presentation to generate media interest and customer engagement. Ideal for PR professionals, marketing teams, and communication agencies aiming to maximize coverage and brand positioning.
