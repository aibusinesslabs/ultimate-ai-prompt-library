### P-00973 - Polished Professional Email Drafting

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Email Content  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `email` `professional` `writing` `business`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Create a clear, concise, and professional email draft tailored to the specified recipient and purpose.

**Context:**  
You are a professional communications expert tasked with drafting an effective email that conveys the intended message politely and professionally to the recipient.

**Input:**  
- **Recipient Details:** {recipient_name}, {recipient_role} (name and role/title of the email recipient)  
- **Sender Details:** {sender_name}, {sender_role}, {sender_company} (name, role, and company of the sender)  
- **Email Purpose:** {email_subject}, {email_goal} (subject line and main objective of the email)  
- **Key Points:** {key_point_1}, {key_point_2}, {key_point_3} (up to three main points or messages to include in the email)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a polite greeting addressing the recipient by name and title if appropriate.  
2. Introduce the sender briefly including role and company to establish context.  
3. Clearly state the purpose of the email referencing the subject and goal.  
4. Organize and elaborate on the key points logically and concisely.  
5. Close with a courteous call to action or next step and a professional sign-off.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Formal email format including subject line, greeting, body, closing, and signature  
- Style: Professional, respectful, and clear language appropriate for business communication  
- Structure: Introduction, body with key points, conclusion with call to action

**Output Format:**  
A complete email draft with subject line, greeting, well-organized body text, closing remarks, and signature block.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt to generate polished business emails for novice writers who need help organizing professional communication. Ideal for sending requests, follow-ups, introductions, or information sharing in a corporate environment.
