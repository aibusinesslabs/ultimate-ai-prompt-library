### P-01285 - Crafting a Compelling Product Launch Press Release

**Category:** Writing & Content Creation  
**Subcategory:** Press Releases  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Tags:** `press release` `product launch` `content creation` `marketing`

#### Prompt:

**Task:**  
Write a professional and engaging press release announcing a new product launch that captures attention and informs the target audience.

**Context:**  
You are a communications specialist tasked with creating a clear, concise, and compelling press release for a company introducing a new product to the market.

**Input:**  
- **Company & Product Info:** {company_name}, {product_name} (company and product names)  
- **Launch Details:** {launch_date}, {product_features} (official launch date and key product features)  
- **Target Audience & Benefits:** {target_audience}, {product_benefits} (intended customers and main benefits)  
- **Call to Action & Contact:** {cta_statement}, {contact_info} (desired next steps for readers and contact details)

**Instructions:**  
1. Begin with a strong headline that highlights the product launch and its significance.  
2. Write a lead paragraph summarizing the who, what, when, and why of the launch.  
3. Detail key product features and explain how they benefit the target audience.  
4. Include a quote from a company representative to add credibility and personality.  
5. End with a clear call to action and provide contact information for follow-up.

**Constraints:**  
- Length: 150-300 words  
- Format: Formal press release style with a headline, body paragraphs, and contact info  
- Style: Professional, informative, and engaging without jargon  
- Structure: Logical flow from announcement to details, quote, and closing CTA

**Output Format:**  
A formatted press release text ready for publication, including headline, body paragraphs, quote, call to action, and contact information.

#### Use Case:  
Use this prompt when launching a new product to create a polished press release that effectively communicates key details to media outlets and customers. Ideal for marketing teams or small businesses seeking to announce new offerings professionally and clearly.
